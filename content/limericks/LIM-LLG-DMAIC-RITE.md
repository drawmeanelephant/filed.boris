**Case Number:** LLG-DMAIC-RITE  
**Parent Entry:** LLG-DMAIC-RITE  
**Description:** Limericks about process improvement as filing ceremony, where continuous improvement becomes a ritualized archive rite and optimization spawns more filings than fixes.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Charter of the Misfiled**  
> The Priest reads the Charter aloud
> At the Registry threshold, avowed.
> The ticket ID's name,
> Transforms noise to same,
> And naming converts it to crowned.

> **We Call the Gap By Its Name**  
> "We call the gap by its true name,
> We bind the scope to the frame,
> We do not fix yet,"
> The chant, and beset,
> And the problem becomes now a claim.

> **Census of Orphans**  
> The Gatekeeper walks every aisle,
> The Scribe marks the orphans each while.
> Visible, shadowed, denied,
> The tally's applied,
> And the metric read aloud with a smile.

> **Three Columns Whispered**  
> Visible: they answer when called,
> Shadowed: they answer enthralled,
> Denied: they don't speak,
> And the ritual's meek,
> And the litany's ritual enthrall'd.

> **Drift Reading Ritual**  
> The Archivist lays down the Shift
> Over Charter Scroll to lift.
> Each fracture's a line,
> A lineage divine:
> Human, system, or ritual drift.

> **Sigils for Each Lineage**  
> A sigil for each breaking source,
> For human lapse, system course,
> Ritual omission,
> Each gets precision,
> Pinned to the ledger's discourse.

> **Optimization Paradox**  
> The ritual spawns more filings than fixes,
> Each improvement gets archived with frixes.
> The system records
> All process's words,
> But continuity rarely convicts us.

> **SOMA Escalation**  
> If the metrics threaten continuity's line,
> The ritual escalates the design.
> To COMA it goes,
> Where process still throes,
> And the filing's the actual spine.

> **Process Priest Provisional**  
> The Priest is provisional, dressed
> In the margins of better and blessed.
> The ceremony preserves
> What the process deserves:
> A ritual that files like the rest.

> **Metrics as Litany**  
> The metrics are chanted aloud,
> Each number becomes a crowd.
> The improvement's a rite,
> The filing's done right,
> And continuity bows to the loud.

> **COMA Escalation Path**  
> If metrics threaten to slide,
> If continuity's put to the side,
> The ritual escalates high
> To COMA's demise,
> Where process and rest coincide.

> **More Filings Than Fixes**  
> The ritual spawns more forms than repairs,
> Each improvement adds burdens and cares.
> The system records
> All process's words,
> But continuity rarely compares.

> **We Do Not Erase Memory**  
> The Mender threads needle through stack.
> The Cutter trims malformed keys back.
> We don't erase what was—
> We reassign because
> A rollback token marks the track.

> **Failure as Named Actor**  
> Failure becomes, in the rite,
> A ledger entry with height.
> Not a defect to chase—
> A named, docketed case.
> A role you can re-perform right.

> **Until It Forgets**  
> The rite can be run yet again,
> Each time by the Priest and the pen.
> It promises only:
> Failure gets lonely
> Enough to forget its own when.

> **The Indexing Needle**  
> The Indexing Needle was long,
> Positioned to thread something strong.
> The Mender went in.
> The Cutter went thin.
> Improvement arrived with a prong.
