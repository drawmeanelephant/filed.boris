**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-SEALANT-PATIENCE  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-SEALANT-PATIENCE  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about Sealant Patience, the sticky glue of endless waiting that masquerades as governance.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Sticky Bureaucracy**  
> The sealant is thick and it’s slow,
> To cover the things we don’t know.
> We’re glued to the spot,
> While the policy rots,
> And the waiting continues to grow.

> **Deferred Closure**  
> You asked for an answer today,
> But the Sealant just got in the way.
> It traps you in goo,
> With a comforting view,
> And forces your soul to decay.

> **Waiting as Governance**  
> They manage the crisis by glue,
> And patience is forced upon you.
> It seals up the door,
> So you can’t ask for more,
> And the process is covered in poo.

> **The Glue of Despair**  
> They covered the tickets in slime,
> To stall them until it was time
> For the founders to sell,
> Leaving us in this hell,
> With a product that's covered in grime.

> **Trapped in the Process**  
> The policy sticks to your shoe,
> Like a horrible, corporate glue.
> You ask for a date,
> And they tell you to wait,
> While the company shits on you too.

> **Weaponized Waiting**  
> The manager stalls on the call,
> While the servers completely forestall.
> He’s sealing the crack,
> With a pat on the back,
> And doing jack shit for us all.

> **Viscous Processing**  
> The ticket was coated in glue,
> To formally manage the queue.
> It’s perfectly sealed,
> The urgency healed,
> And nobody knows what to do.

> **Pending Adhesive**  
> We applied the standard delay,
> To keep all the queries at bay.
> The sealant is thick,
> It does the old trick,
> Of pushing the issue away.

> **Adhesion Event Recorded**  
> The sealant was poured on the file,
> To cover the unresolved pile.
> The closure deferred,
> Without any word,
> It sat in the system a while.
