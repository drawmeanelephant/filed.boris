**Case Number:** FREF-0880-WPRT  
**Parent Entry:** FREF-0880-WPRT  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about formally observing a tragedy without doing a single thing to actually stop it.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12  

***

> **Authority Limit**  
> The witness could say what was seen,
> But not make the machine intervene.
> Their authority held
> Where observation dwelled,
> Then stopped at the lever between.

> **Preservation Rule**  
> A witness preserved contradiction
> Without forcing a cleaner prediction.
> The record stayed split,
> But it did not omit
> The inconvenient friction.

> **Observed, Not Fixed**  
> They observed with procedural care,
> Named the burden and kept it in air.
> That mattered, but still,
> Observation is nil
> When the harm needs authority there.

> **Standardized Witness**  
> The tragedy entered the queue,
> As exactly expected to do.
> The protocol states,
> We monitor fates,
> And log the destruction anew.

> **Screaming Metrics**  
> We charted the pain in a graph,
> And gave it a terrible laugh.
> The dashboard is red,
> Because someone is dead,
> Which ruins the day for the staff.

> **Optometric Review**  
> The eyeballs were placed in the jar,
> And evaluated from afar.
> We noticed the fire,
> Is burning the wire,
> And rated the flames at a par.

> **Barely Watching**  
> I wrote down the thing that I saw,
> Without any feeling or awe.
> It happened again,
> At a quarter to ten,
> And I'm going to lunch by the law.

> **The Unblinking Eye**  
> The watcher of eras is still,
> Atop the procedural hill.
> It stares at the blight,
> Through the endless night,
> And the ancient prophecies fill.
