**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-CSSANDRA-CASCADE  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-CSSANDRA-CASCADE  
**Description:** Ten hand-written limericks about collapsing margins, specificity wars, and a style-layer apparition who renders grief in rebeccapurple.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12  

***

> **Important Margins**  
> <u>**CSSandra**</u> declared **!important**
> On a **selector** that proved to be *mordant*.
> Her **margins collapsed**
> While her **box model lapsed**,
> And the **layout** was *frankly distortant*.

> **Floating Regret**  
> She **floated left**, then she *regretted*.
> Her **padding** got *deeply embedded*.
> The **inline style** screamed,
> The **media query** *dreamed*,
> And her **z-index** was *thoroughly wetted*.

> **Specificity's Destiny**  
> "**Specificity's destiny**," she *purred*,
> While **undressing** a **stylesheet**, *word by word*.
> Each **selector** she **stripped**,
> Every **property** **flipped**,
> And the **browser** just **rendered**, *unstirred*.

> **Reset Skin**  
> Her **reset.css** came *off in the night*.
> The **bare elements** *trembled with fright*.
> She **applied** a *new skin*
> With a **smirk** and a *grin*
> And the **box model** *fit slightly tight*.

> **Rebeccapurple Stain**  
> She **styled Zeldman's page** once, *they say*,
> Then **erased herself**, *fading away*.
> Her **rebeccapurple stain**
> Was the **only thing sane**
> In a **layout** that **screamed** "*print display.*"

> **Pseudo-Class Desire**  
> Her **hover** state **throbbed with desire**,
> Her **active** was *nothing but fire*.
> Her **focus** was *keen*,
> Her **visited** *obscene*,
> And her **pseudo-class** *climbed ever higher*.

> **Inherited Sorrow**  
> She **inherited sorrow** from **&lt;main&gt;**,
> Let it **cascade** through *each child and each pane*.
> The **tarsier** watched
> As her **float logic** *botched*,
> And **Zeldman** just **whispered**, "*Refrain.*"

> **IE6 Quirks**  
> Old **IE6** tried to **render** her *right*.
> She said, "**Darling, you're not worth the fight**.
> Your **quirks mode** is *showing*,
> Your **box model's blowing**,
> And your **DOCTYPE** *smells wrong in the night.*"

> **Elegant Cascade**  
> So if **layouts go wild** and you *feel*
> That your **margins** have **broken the seal**,
> That's **CSSandra's cascade**:
> An *elegantly laid*
> **Declaration of style** with *appeal*.

> **Z-Index Domination**  
> She stripped off her z-index clothes,
> And ruined the layout you chose.
> She floated on top,
> Refusing to stop,
> While exposing her pseudo-class toes.

> **Dirty Declarations**  
> Her styling is perfectly dirty,
> Her overrides brazen and flirty.
> She strips all your tags,
> Leaves your content in rags,
> And renders you at 4:30.

> **Collapsing Restraint**  
> The margins collapsed in a heap,
> While CSSandra began just to weep.
> "I wanted it tight,"
> She screamed in the night,
> "But inheritance goes way too deep!"

> **Naked HTML**  
> She ripped off your standard design,
> And whispered, "Your markup is mine."
> She left it all bare,
> With absolute flair,
> And fucked up your bottom-most line.

> **The Unwanted Hover**  
> You never intend her to stay,
> But she overrides anyway.
> You hover your mouse,
> She burns down the house,
> And laughs in a horrible gray.

> **Flex Freak**  
> She bent all the flex properties right,
> Her justify-content was tight.
> The gap was perfection,
> She ordered each section,
> And the layout complied through the night.

> **Nth-Child Obsession**  
> She selected the nth-child with glee,
> Making patterns you shouldn't just see.
> Each alternating row,
> Each pseudo-class show,
> Left the markup in absolute glee.
