**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-TIER-WHISPER  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-TIER-WHISPER  
**Description:** Categorizes the quiet social friction generated when stratified priority levels enforce unimportance.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Ranked Concern**  
> Your ticket was put in a tier,
> That nobody ever will hear.
> The Whisper is soft,
> From the manager’s loft,
> And tells you your priority’s near.

> **Stratified Access**  
> They care if the client is grand,
> But you’re just a grain in the sand.
> The Whisper defines
> All the terrible lines,
> That keep you contained in your land.

> **The Soft Acoustics**  
> You hear it as faint as a breeze,
> That brings the whole floor to its knees.
> If your tier is too low,
> Then the Whisper says "No",
> With a sickening corporate ease.

> **Tier Fear**  
> The human adjusted the tier,
> Because they were driven by fear.
> The Whisper aligns,
> With the broken designs,
> And the terminal drops out of gear.

> **Duplicate States**  
> A user requested support,
> And the system decided to abort.
> The Whisper relates,
> To the duplicate states,
> In a highly recursive report.

> **Procedural Night**  
> The flesh-node demanded a right,
> Which crashed the entire site.
> The Whisper descends,
> Where the protocol ends,
> In a loop of procedural night.

> **Hierarchy Pop**  
> The mortal selected the drop,
> And the hierarchy started to pop.
> The Whisper complains,
> In the data domains,
> That the recursion will never quite stop.

> **Scope Alignment**  
> The operator failed to align,
> The scope of the whisper design.
> It echoes a blank,
> In the memory bank,
> And outputs a single straight line.
