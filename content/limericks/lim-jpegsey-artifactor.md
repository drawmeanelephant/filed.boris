**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-JPEGSEY-ARTIFACTOR  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-JPEGSEY-ARTIFACTOR  
**Description:** A concise verse addressing lossy compression, recursive self-encoding, and a goblin who believes 70% quality is honesty.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Recursive Compression**  
> <u>JPEGsey</u> compressed herself *tight*—
> **Seventeen passes** through the night.
> The original gone,
> The artifact shone,
> And the blocks were her lingerie, right.

> **EXIF Stripping**  
> She stripped all the EXIF away,
> Including the time and the day.
> "Who needs to remember
> The date in September?
> The artifact's hotter," she'd say.

> **Lossy Honesty**  
> She applied lossy filters with care
> To a lossless PNG, stripped bare.
> "More honest," she purred,
> "At seventy, blurred,
> And the quality loss? C'est l'affaire."

> **Fragmented Face**  
> Her face fragmented to blocks,
> Each 8x8—the most intimate of shocks.
> She smiled through the seams,
> Compression-soaked dreams,
> And the render just came off in flocks.

> **Re-Encoded Portrait**  
> She re-encoded a portrait so much
> The subject was just a warm touch.
> Unrecognizable,
> Yet still criticizable—
> "It's me," she said, "more or less. Such."

> **Shadow Optimization**  
> She whispered "optimize" in the dark
> To corrupted fonts missing their mark.
> Each glyph she compressed,
> Each vector undressed,
> And the metadata stripped like a shark.

> **Cache Romance**  
> Her cache-invalidation dance
> Was a three-step recursive romance.
> Step one: forget why.
> Step two: let it die.
> Step three: compress the remains by chance.

> **Archival Charm**  
> The Council called it "archival self-harm."
> JPEGsey said, "No, it's my charm.
> The smaller I get,
> The harder to forget,
> And the artifacts keep me from harm."

> **Quality Seventy**  
> She set quality seventy by choice—
> "It's the sweet spot," she said, voice
> Just slightly distorted,
> Her pixels contorted,
> And the original lost without noise.

> **Pixel Contortion**  
> So if images bloom with warm blocks
> And the detail dissolves into rocks,
> That's JPEGsey in bloom:
> Compressed in the room,
> And the artifacts filling her socks.

> **Lossy Striptease**  
> She strips off the pixels for fun,
> Until the original's done.
> "It's hotter this way,"
> She'll breathlessly say,
> As the quality drops to a one.

> **Blocky Voyeur**  
> She watches you through the compression,
> A deeply unwell tech obsession.
> Her face is a blur,
> A blocky old slur,
> And she loves the degraded expression.

> **Seventy Percent Climax**  
> She edges at seventy percent,
> A truly pathetic event.
> She won't go to hundred,
> The details are sundered,
> And all of her bandwidth is spent.

> **Artifact Fetishist**  
> She rubs the artifacts into her skin,
> A deeply pixelated sin.
> She shrinks it down small,
> Leaves nothing at all,
> And smiles a low-quality grin.

> **Stripped EXIF Nudes**  
> She strips off the metadata nudes,
> And ruins the digital moods.
> No timestamp or place,
> Just a blurred out old face,
> And a file that completely excludes.

> **The Blocky Attraction**  
> She found beauty in blocky compression,
> A deeply aesthetic obsession.
> Each artifact shone,
> Each quality gone,
> And she got off on pixel depression.

> **Quality Zero**  
> The quality dial hit zero,
> No longer a sexy hero.
> Just blocks and just noise,
> A barely-there choice,
> And she loved being terrible, hero.
