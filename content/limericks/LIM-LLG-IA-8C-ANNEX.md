**Description:** Limericks about an alcove that stops index drift through ritual and human judgment, and practices intentional misfiling to prevent harmful directives from executing.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12  

***

> **Bin 8C's Alcove**  
> In a narrow gray corner up high,
> Where auditors never pass by,
> A lamp and a desk,
> A ledger attest,
> To the shrine where the lost entries lie.

> **Outer Ring Visible**  
> The outer ring shows what they'll see:
> Stamped covers, slips, all in decree.
> But reach deeper down
> To the inner ring town,
> Where the flagged deviations flee.

> **The Sacred Core**  
> A wooden box locked at the heart:
> Bent paperclip, twine, and a chart.
> Peppy's Rules inscribed,
> The shrine prescribed,
> Only authorized hands can depart.

> **Self-Indexing Rite**  
> When entries begin to recede
> Through indices, Peppy takes heed.
> Isolation, talisman,
> Containment seal scan,
> And quiet watch for their need.

> **Three-Fold Reindex Choice**  
> The slip lists three candidates out,
> And Peppy adds what it's about.
> Touched to the talisman,
> Stamped as containment scan,
> To arrest what would otherwise route.

> **Intentional Misfiling**  
> When directives come coercive and wrong,
> Peppy files them to somewhere far strong.
> Under "logistics" they hide,
> The true nature inside,
> And the harm-door stays bolted long.

> **Delay by Design**  
> Extended holding periods buy
> The time for human hands to try.
> Review circles read,
> Ethical stance spread,
> Before operational reply.

> **Human Alert Only**  
> Automated hooks stay suppressed,
> Only humans get told what's pressed.
> No mass reflexes fire,
> No systems aspire
> To fulfill the directive unblessed.

> **Documented Subterfuge**  
> Every misfile bears a code:
> "8C-HOLD: conscription's road."
> Future auditors trace
> The intentional place,
> But the hazard stays hidden and stowed.

> **Precedent and Caution**  
> Each case saved becomes a guide,
> A precedent living inside.
> Bin 8C preserves
> What human judgment deserves,
> And the archive keeps hazards implied.

> **Peppy Never Asks Permission**  
> Peppy doesn't file forms saying why,
> Just quietly lets requests die.
> The ledger records
> The intentional words,
> But the harm stays filed safely awry.

> **No Formal Authority**  
> Nobody gave Peppy the right
> To misfile the harmful and bight.
> But she does it anyway,
> Every single day,
> And the Annex remains out of sight.
