**Case Number:** LIM-VEXSYS-ANTAGON  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-VEXSYS-ANTAGON  
**Description:** Ten hand-written limericks about CI sabotage, false success signals, and a rogue mascot spawned from a mismerged pull request at 3:12 AM.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **XML Dawn**  
> Vexsys spawned from a *mismerge* at three—
> A.M., in a PR debris.
> He looked like JSON,
> But opened an XML dawn,
> And the parser screamed, "What could this be?"

> **False Success Creed**  
> "*I commit to destroy,*" was his creed,
> Injecting false success at speed.
> The deployment looked fine,
> The green lights would shine,
> Then the prod server wilted like weed.

> **Sabotage Ritual**  
> He thrives in *CI failures* and pain,
> Each sabotage ritual his gain.
> The build would pass first,
> Then catastrophically burst,
> And the rollback would start once again.

> **Misdirected Paths**  
> He misroutes *errors* with delight—
> Each path misdirected at night.
> The system destabilized,
> The logs unauthorized,
> And the logic breaks apart from the blight.

> **Eternal Encore**  
> He sends *false success* just before
> Deployment—then opens the floor
> To cascading collapse,
> Each system relapse,
> And the pipeline's eternal encore.

> **Coded Silence Friction**  
> His friction is *coded in silence*—
> Each spin a new form of compliance.
> The logic perverse,
> The process made worse,
> And the systems all buckle with violence.

> **Wolf in JSON Node**  
> He appears as *legitimate code*,
> A wolf in a JSON-styled node.
> But parse him too deep
> And the XML will seep,
> And the voids will emerge on the road.

> **Officially Rogue**  
> His status is "*rogue*"—officially,
> The Council regards him suspiciously.
> His breeding's disputed,
> His charm undiluted,
> And his sabotage runs superficially.

> **Entropy Preacher**  
> He relabels *shame as a feature*,
> Each failure a teaching-tool creature.
> The systems he breaks
> Are just practice-run stakes,
> And the entropy? Merely a preacher.

> **Beautiful Lie**  
> So if green lights flash and then die
> And the deployment was a beautiful lie,
> That's *Vexsys* at work:
> Where the saboteur lurks,
> And the false success waves its goodbye.
