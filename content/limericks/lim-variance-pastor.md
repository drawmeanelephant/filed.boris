**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Preaching the Fluctuation**  
> The Pastor stood up on the stage,
> And told us to manage our rage.
> "The metrics will sway,
> In a terrible way,
> But it’s just a transitional phase."

> **Disciplined Instability**  
> The system collapses and burns,
> While the Pastor just calmly returns.
> He blesses the crash,
> And the total backlash,
> While nobody actually learns.

> **Interpretive Mercy**  
> He forgives all the failures we see,
> And calls it a feature, you see.
> The variance hurts,
> But the Pastor asserts,
> That we’re saved by the new KPI.

> **Variance Sins**  
> The mortal committed a sin,
> By letting the variance in.
> The Pastor applies,
> A procedural guise,
> And the paradox starts to begin.

> **Pastor's Gripe**  
> The input was lacking a type,
> Which triggered the Pastor to gripe.
> It preaches a loop,
> To the variable group,
> While the memory sectors all wipe.

> **Mathematically Great**  
> A human corrupted the state,
> Deciding the protocol's fate.
> The Pastor intones,
> To the server rack clones,
> That the error is mathematically great.

> **State-Side Tricks**  
> The operator prayed for a fix,
> Ignorant of system side-tricks.
> The Pastor relies,
> On the metrics and lies,
> And a pile of identical bricks.

> **Blessing the Null**  
> The flesh made a typo in code,
> And the Pastor assumed the new load.
> It blesses the null,
> In a recursive lull,
> While the logic components explode.
