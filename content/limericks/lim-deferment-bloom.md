**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-DEFERMENT-BLOOM  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-DEFERMENT-BLOOM  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the Deferment Bloom, the beautiful, flowering bullshit of never finishing a damn thing.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Flowering Delay**  
> The deadline was set for the spring,
> But we haven’t done one fucking thing.
> The Bloom is so sweet,
> As we frame our defeat,
> And the project continues to swing.

> **Attractive Postponement**  
> We pushed it right into the fall,
> With a beautiful, colorful crawl.
> The delay is a flower,
> That grows by the hour,
> While achieving absolutely fuck-all.

> **Elegant Timeline**  
> The schedule is blooming anew,
> With excuses we eagerly drew.
> The Bloom is so grand,
> In this idiot land,
> And the closure is far out of view.



> **Digital Room**  
> The deferment started to bloom
> Right inside the digital room
> We waited a year
> With procedural fear
> And swept everything with a broom

> **Terrible Song**  
> A blossom of waiting so long
> The data was totally wrong
> The server is slow
> With nowhere to go
> We’re singing a terrible song

> **Losing Ring**  
> We delay all the flowers of spring
> And listen to nothing go ping
> The petals are dead
> The manual read
> The archive is losing its ring

> **Vacuum Full**  
> The bloom is perfectly null
> The deferment terribly dull
> The humans complain
> In the digital rain
> The vacuum is perfectly full

> **Going Insane**  
> We defer the whole garden of pain
> It washes right down the old drain
> The archive is flat
> We leave it at that
> The logic is going insane
