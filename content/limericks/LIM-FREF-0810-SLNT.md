**Case Number:** FREF-0810-SLNT  
**Parent Entry:** FREF-0810-SLNT  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about quiet lanes, unfiled burden, and intervals where little is recorded but much is happening.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12  

***

> **Quiet Lane**  
> The interval made little sound,
> So the chart saw no trouble around.
> But quiet can hide
> What was never supplied,
> And the absence itself may be bound.

> **Unfiled Burden**  
> No filing appeared in the span,
> Which delighted the continuity plan.
> Yet the burden might stay
> In an unspoken way,
> Past the edge of what measurement can.

> **Dual Silence**  
> A silence can mean things are well,
> Or that nobody dares ring the bell.
> Empathegy keeps
> Both possibilities asleep,
> Till a witness can say which one fell.

> **Unheard Complaints**  
> The silent condition is strict,
> And any loud noise we evict.
> The lack of a sound,
> Means morale is unbound,
> Or maybe they're just derelict.

> **The Quiet Void**  
> The silence is older than stone,
> It chills you right down to the bone.
> The ghosts of the code,
> Live inside of this node,
> And leave the processor alone.

> **Acoustic Breach**  
> A keystroke was slightly too loud,
> Which isn't remotely allowed!
> The volume police,
> Demand it to cease,
> And banish the user from the cloud.

> **Audio Null**  
> The soundboard is currently moot,
> The system has signaled a mute.
> No waves are defined,
> No speakers assigned,
> The audio void is acute.

> **Nothing Heard**  
> The microphone captured no byte,
> Throughout the entirety of night.
> The log is completely,
> Updated so neatly,
> The state is identically white.

> **The Keeper of Sub-Basement C**  
> The binder from nineteen-o-four,
> Is bleeding black ink on the floor.
> The interns who read,
> The pages that bleed,
> Will wander the halls evermore.
