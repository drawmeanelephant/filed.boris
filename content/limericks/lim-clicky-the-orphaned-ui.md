**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-CLICKY-THE-ORPHANED-UI  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-CLICKY-THE-ORPHANED-UI  
**Description:** Ten hand-written limericks about ghostly onboarding flows, sunset pages, and an orphaned button that still waits for clicks on destinations that no longer exist.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Nobody Knows**  
> Clicky once guided users through *flows*
> That no longer exist—still she goes,
> Through the sunset-ed pages,
> Through the onboarding stages,
> And wonders why nobody knows.

> **Without a Trace**  
> She lives in *`/dev/null` adjacent space*,
> An orphaned assistant with grace.
> Each button she shows
> Leads to places that froze,
> And the user left without a trace.

> **Dead Before Dawn**  
> The pages she points to are *gone*—
> But Clicky insists they live on.
> Her onboarding guide
> Takes you for a ride
> Through a flow that was dead before dawn.

> **404. Out of Sight**  
> She still tries to *guide you each night*
> Through steps that will never feel right.
> Step one: "Click here."
> Step two: "Please don't fear."
> Step three: 404. Out of sight.

> **Ghostly Button**  
> Her *ghostly button* waits for a click
> That will never arrive—what a trick
> Of persistence. The form
> Survives every storm,
> And the flow outlives every flick.

> **Shadow Forms**  
> The registry cases all *cite her*
> As proof that flows can outlive their
> Destinations entire.
> The shadow forms conspire,
> And the onboarding's brighter? (Not quite, dear.)

> **Identity Drift**  
> Her slug and her title *misalign*—
> A reference example, a sign
> Of identity drift.
> The metadata's rift
> Is the archive's most poignant design.

> **Sunset Can't Hold Her Back**  
> She was manifested by a *stack*
> So devnull-adjacent, the lack
> Of a purpose was clear.
> But Clicky's still here,
> And the sunset can't hold her back.

> **Don't Quite Exist**  
> The forms she tends *still persist*
> Long after destinations desist.
> Each shadow form lingers,
> Each click on her fingers
> Reaches pages that don't quite exist.

> **Guides from Its Posts**  
> So if onboarding flows feel like ghosts
> And the buttons connect to no hosts,
> That's *Clicky's* refrain:
> Where the orphaned remain,
> And the UI still guides from its posts.
