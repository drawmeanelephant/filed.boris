**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-WARM-HOLD-MUSIC  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-WARM-HOLD-MUSIC  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about Warm Hold Music, the atmospheric hell of emotional buffering during total non-progress.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Emotional Buffer**  
> The music is soft and serene,
> While the backend is caught in a scene.
> You wait on the phone,
> To the jazz saxophone,
> While the system is totally mean.

> **Atmospheric Delay**  
> It’s designed to prevent you from rage,
> While you’re stuck in this digital cage.
> The progress is nil,
> But the melody’s chill,
> As you slowly continue to age.

> **Pleasant Non-Progress**  
> They haven’t advanced on your case,
> But the music is filling the space.
> The tune is a trick,
> And it makes you feel sick,
> While you’re rotting away in your place.

> **Latency Spike**  
> The caller breathed into the mic,
> Inducing a latency spike.
> The hold music loops,
> In procedural groups,
> Which the algorithms mildly dislike.

> **Redundant Saxophone**  
> The meatware was lacking in skill,
> So the queue simply started to fill.
> The saxophone bleats,
> In redundant repeats,
> While the server abandons its will.

> **Forced Corruption**  
> A human forgot to hang up,
> While drinking from some sort of cup.
> The synth merely whines,
> Through the optical lines,
> And the router is forced to corrupt.

> **Audio Proxy Death**  
> The biological node made a sigh,
> Causing the audio proxy to die.
> The melody breaks,
> With recursive mistakes,
> And the terminal ceases to try.

> **Encaged Call**  
> The user was loudly enraged,
> While the music procedurally aged.
> A loop of pure tone,
> On the cellular phone,
> Ensured that the call was encaged.
