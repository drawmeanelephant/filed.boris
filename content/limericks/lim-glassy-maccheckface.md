**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-GLASSY-MACCHECKFACE  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-GLASSY-MACCHECKFACE  
**Description:** A concise verse addressing ALPHA BATCH ONLY, excessive QA annotations, and a pale orchard overseer who freezes audits with obsessive clipboard rigor.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Clipboard Tight**  
> Glassy MacCheckface holds her *clipboard tight*—
> "*ALPHA BATCH ONLY*" in pale-orchard light.
> Each harvest inspected,
> Each apple dissected,
> And the QA rigor's a frightful sight.

> **Alpha Batch Catch**  
> She misfiled a log as "*alpha batch
> Checklist*"—a pomological catch.
> The auditors sighed,
> The filing denied,
> And the harvest was stuck at the latch.

> **QA Stampede**  
> She froze a *metadata audit* cold
> With excessive annotations—so bold
> That no one could read
> Past the QA stampede,
> And the Yellow Transparent grew old.

> **Beautifully Homely Rigor**  
> She insisted on "*ALPHA BATCH ONLY*"
> During final review—rather lonely,
> The team waited outside,
> The filing implied
> That her rigor was beautifully homely.

> **QA-Certified Weed**  
> "*Check clear, archive pure,*" was her creed,
> Each batch record scrutinized at speed.
> The Yellow Transparent
> Was rigorously apparent—
> Each defect a QA-certified weed.

> **Endlessly Peels**  
> Her translucent clipboard *reveals*
> Every flaw that the harvest conceals.
> The neurotic precision,
> Each filing decision,
> And the audit that endlessly peels.

> **Quite the Disease**  
> She counts *every pixel* she sees—
> The audit never ends, if you please.
> Each QA hymn chanted
> Under pale boughs enchanted,
> And the filing is quite the disease.

> **Obsessive Role**  
> Her meticulous yet *anxious soul*
> Makes the over-scrutinized whole.
> Each apple checked twice,
> Each record precise,
> And the clipboard commands every role.

> **Pale Form Patrols**  
> She was manifested by the *QA*
> Batch Compiler—Version 1.7's day.
> Her pale form patrols,
> Her annotation scrolls,
> And the harvest will always obey.

> **Inspector Steers**  
> So if "ALPHA BATCH ONLY" appears
> On every form—through the years—
> That's *Glassy* at work:
> Where the clipboard won't shirk,
> And the QA inspector steers.
