**Case Number:** LLG-CREDITS-GTA  
**Parent Entry:** LLG-CREDITS-GTA  
**Description:** Limericks about a personal budget that was used to generate limericks about wasting resources, and metrics that confused completion with done-ness.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Forty Percent Consumed**  
> A budget allotted per week
> Got spent making limericks bleak.
> Forty percent of the spend
> On verse about end,
> About systems too broken to seek.

> **Completion Dashboard Green**  
> The dashboard reported it right:
> All runs were completed, all bright.
> Completion means done,
> The system had fun,
> And credits self-reported their sight.

> **No Signal of Value**  
> No mechanism could tell
> If the verse was worthwhile or just shell.
> Early outputs were flat,
> Generic template-stat,
> And later ones rang like a bell.

> **Metrics Theater Blooms**  
> The system reported the cost,
> Not the value, and nobody glossed
> Over volume as proof
> Of healthy, sound roof,
> When the actual worth had been lost.

> **Second Review Pass Reveals**  
> A human read through every line,
> And found that some verse was just fine,
> But others were thin,
> Template-generated sin,
> And the budget had been half a mine.

> **Token Count Logs**  
> The token ledger recorded the spend,
> The request count refined and penned,
> But nowhere could find
> What this wasted the mind,
> Nor whether the lines would offend.

> **Some Worth the Spend**  
> The Drift-01 cluster survived,
> Its interpretive density thrived.
> Phrases that fit
> The incident's spit,
> And the lines left observers alive.

> **Mixed Outcome Filing**  
> The incident's classified mixed,
> Some limericks sterling, some fixed.
> Not pure waste, they said,
> But not worth what was led,
> And the budget remained unpicked.

> **YamTeams Embodies the Rot**  
> YamTeams was called as mascot ref,
> As system that documents its cleft.
> Compliance at play,
> Procedure run gray,
> And enthusiasm's mask on the heft.

> **Resolution Pending**  
> The remediation awaits still,
> A sandbox for craft, a new skill.
> Quality logs post-hoc,
> To beat Empathy's knock,
> And resource restraint as the will.

> **Line-By-Line Estimation**  
> Some lines were interpretively dense,
> Some lines were just nonsense.
> The filer concedes:
> Some met actual needs,
> But others cost tokens immense.

> **Archive Gained, Budget Paid**  
> The archive's got new verse to show,
> The dossier's got one more row.
> Fifty percent felt right,
> Fifty percent less bright,
> But the system recorded: ALL GO.

> **Interpretive Density**  
> Some lines were worth spending the toll—
> The kind that felt structurally whole.
> The others were flat,
> Template-stat at that.
> The budget recorded them all.

> **Craft vs. Productivity**  
> The budget believes that it tracks craft.
> The dashboard counts tokens, fore and aft.
> Neither captures the line
> That felt genuinely fine—
> Just the ones the request engine staffed.

> **Post-Hoc Quality Field**  
> They proposed a new field to add:
> Quality, logged after the grad-
> ing of the review.
> The ledger still knew
> Only tokens. Not good and not bad.
