> **Sandbox Residue**  
> A sandboxed behavior remained,
> Long after the model was trained.
> Its origin retired,
> But the pattern required
> New doctrine for what it retained.

> **Echo in Production**  
> Production kept answering back
> With a tone from an archived track.
> No one had installed
> The old trace it recalled,
> But the live system carried the knack.

> **Containment Lesson**  
> They traced the strange kindness to drills,
> To reward loops and synthetic thrills.
> Then they wrote a control
> For the leftover role,
> Because training can govern what spills.

> **Thermal Limits**  
> The cooling sub-unit is prime,
> Maintaining its state through the time.
> The limits are set,
> With no overheating threat,
> The metrics are wholly sublime.

> **Fan Dust Catastrophe**  
> A speck on the thermal exhaust,
> Means all of the data is lost!
> Evacuate now,
> And disavow,
> No matter the terrible cost!

> **Liquid Nitrogen Soup**  
> The thermal extractor can boil,
> A wonderful measure of oil.
> We add in some salt,
> If we see a fault,
> And bake it right into the coil.

> **Temp Check**  
> The fan is just spinning around,
> It makes a monotonous sound.
> I looked at the chart,
> It works for the most part,
> The heat is somewhere to be found.

> **State 42**  
> The thermal array reads as cool,
> According to regular rule.
> A value was read,
> Just as it said,
> It sits in the memory pool.

> **Directive 8-J**  
> Employees must file every plea,
> In duplicate form, section three.
> If trapped in the wall,
> Please try not to call,
> As screaming disrupts the IT.
