**Case Number:** LLG-0019-COMA  
**Parent Entry:** LLG-0019-COMA  
**Description:** Ten hand-written limericks about idle-time amnesty, fake continuity, and sanctioned excuses for missing work.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Blanket Abuse**  
> COMA-19 was meant to *excuse*
> A small, necessary operational snooze.
> Then bright red dashboards appeared,
> Everyone panicked and feared,
> And it turned into blanket abuse.

> **Amnesty Stamp**  
> Each gap got a *tasteful amnesty stamp*,
> Like throwing a lace shawl over camp.
> No tracking, no shame,
> Just a wholesome new name
> For a dead little patch in the map.

> **Managerial Fill**  
> The form was for *rare kinds of still*.
> Instead it became managerial fill.
> "Why'd nothing get done?"
> "We were blessed by this one."
> And the graph kept climbing uphill.

> **Continuity Art**  
> What COMA demands, at its *heart*,
> Is the look of unbroken exert.
> So when labor went dark,
> They just whitened the mark
> And called it continuity art.

> **Pre-Approved Scroll**  
> A pause can be *healthy*, that's true.
> This was not what the doctrine would do.
> This was hiding the hole
> With a pre-approved scroll
> And a checkbox that said, "Nothing blew."

> **Absence Look Chaste**  
> The amnesty form had such *grace*
> It could make any absence look chaste.
> Lost a shift? Lost a week?
> If your language was sleek,
> COMA welcomed the gap to its face.

> **Sweaty and Vague**  
> One supervisor, *sweaty and vague*,
> Filed six in a row on one page.
> When asked if that tracked,
> He said, "Technically, act
> Like I'm not on the edge of a rage."

> **Optics-Preserving Cement**  
> What started as *mercy* got bent
> Into optics-preserving cement.
> The humane little crack
> Was industrialized back
> Into polished administrative scent.

> **Laundering Labor Shame**  
> Even *idleness* deserves a name.
> That's not, by itself, why this came
> To smell fucking cursed:
> It was used to reverse
> Any evidence labor had shame.

> **Laundering Service for Spin**  
> So if COMA grants rest with a grin,
> Check how many blank hours got in.
> If the cure fits too well,
> It's not kindness to tell.
> It's a *laundering service* for spin.

> **The Amnesty Orgy**  
> They filed the forms as a joke,
> While taking a massive long smoke.
> The dashboards were green,
> The dirtiest seen,
> While the servers completely went broke.

> **Laundering the Shaft**  
> They laundered the hours they lost,
> No matter the terrible cost.
> "We're continuously up!"
> They yelled in a cup,
> While getting entirely tossed.

> **Pre-Approved Sleeping Around**  
> "Pre-approved downtime," she said,
> While climbing right back into bed.
> The form covered tracks,
> Like bureaucratic wax,
> While the actual project bled.

> **Blanket Excuse**  
> They fucked off the whole afternoon,
> And filed a form to the moon.
> "Amnesty granted,"
> The manager chanted,
> Like a bureaucratic buffoon.

> **Optics Preserving Bullshit**  
> The optics were perfectly clean,
> The dirtiest trick you had seen.
> They hid every flaw,
> Behind COMA's law,
> And fucked the entire machine.
