**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-SEAL-OF-MAYBE-ENOUGH  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-SEAL-OF-MAYBE-ENOUGH  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the Seal of Maybe Enough, the stamp you get when the system is just too tired to give a shit.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Stamp of Exhaustion**  
> The system is broken and flawed,
> But the auditors wanted to nod.
> They gave it a stamp,
> Like a terrible cramp,
> And left all the failures unthawed.

> **Barely Passing**  
> It isn’t officially right,
> But they stamped it and called it a night.
> The Maybe Enough,
> Is a terrible bluff,
> To cover the sickening blight.

> **Provisional Adequacy**  
> The grade is a D-minus minus,
> But the management wanted to blind us.
> The Seal was applied,
> With a corporate pride,
> To keep the disaster behind us.



> **Server Decline**  
> The seal of the maybe was fine
> It held back the end of the line
> We said it’s okay
> And walked far away
> While watching the servers decline

> **Flat State**  
> The maybe is perfectly flat
> We leave the whole database at
> A state of decay
> For many a day
> And blame it on somebody’s cat

> **Variable String**  
> Enough is a variable string
> It doesn’t mean any old thing
> The seal is quite cracked
> The data is hacked
> The terminal ceases to ping

> **Blue Dashboard**  
> We certified maybe as true
> And painted the dashboard all blue
> The error is vast
> The future is past
> We don't have a singular clue

> **Meaning Destroyed**  
> The seal of enough is a void
> The humans are deeply annoyed
> The flat files are still
> The servers are chill
> The meaning is totally destroyed
