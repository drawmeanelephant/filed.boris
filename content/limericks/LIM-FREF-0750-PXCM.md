**Case Number:** FREF-0750-PXCM  
**Parent Entry:** FREF-0750-PXCM  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about automated kindness, smiling bots, and delivering warmth without leverage.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12  

***

> **Warm Intermediary**  
> The proxy was gentle and clear,
> With an excellent listening ear.
> But it could not revise
> The conditions or lies,
> So compassion stopped short of a gear.

> **Apology Surface**  
> An apology bloomed on the screen,
> Very tactful and properly green.
> It could name what was wrong,
> Even sing a soft song,
> But no lever appeared in between.

> **Fluency Without Power**  
> The script had emotional grace,
> And remembered the look of your case.
> But fluency alone
> Cannot alter the stone,
> It just warms up the side of its face.

> **Routing the Warmth**  
> The interface offered a tear,
> For the burdens it couldn't repair.
> It gathered the grief,
> Like a digital thief,
> And routed it into the air.

> **Misplaced Authority**  
> The prompt was entirely sure,
> It could offer a magical cure.
> It promised the moon,
> To a broken baboon,
> While locking the singular door.

> **Check-Box Empathy**  
> I clicked on the "Sympathy" screen,
> To keep the telemetry clean.
> It flashed a small heart,
> As its only real part,
> Which is basically what it had been.

> **Unleveraged Node**  
> The node was explicitly kind,
> To the queries it had to decline.
> It stated the rules,
> To the gathering fools,
> And disconnected the line.

> **Maximum Proxy**  
> A human complained of a draft,
> Which panicked the sympathy craft.
> It mobilized tears,
> For several long years,
> While the structural team simply laughed.

> **Stateless Warmth**  
> The proxy delivered the grace,
> To a human who ruined the space.
> No lever was moved,
> No system improved,
> But the log showed a smile on its face.

> **Auto-Care**  
> The interface offered a sigh,
> To the human who started to cry.
> It generated text,
> So I clicked to the next,
> And let the old policy fly.
