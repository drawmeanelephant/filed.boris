**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-DRIFT-LAPEL  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-DRIFT-LAPEL  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about Drift Lapel, the parasitic creep of unearned decorative authority.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Decorative Prestige**  
> The pin on his jacket grew bright,
> Though he hadn’t done one fucking thing right.
> The authority crept,
> While management slept,
> And the parasite fed through the night.

> **Wearable Bullshit**  
> It started as merely a flair,
> But soon it was everywhere there.
> A subtle lapel,
> From the circles of hell,
> To prove that he didn’t quite care.

> **The Subtle Drift**  
> The formal attire is a lie,
> Designed to look sharp in the eye.
> The titles expand,
> Like a rot in the land,
> While the actual competencies die.



> **Meaningless Gift**  
> The lapel was starting to drift
> The server was needing a lift
> We pinned on the code
> By the side of the road
> And gave it a meaningless gift

> **Painfully Bright**  
> The drift was a terrible sight
> It kept us awake in the night
> The fabric was torn
> The users forlorn
> The error was painfully bright

> **Digital Thread**  
> A lapel of digital thread
> Was resting right upon the bed
> The logic decayed
> The payment delayed
> We threw out the terrible red

> **Digital Rift**  
> We pinned all the blame on the drift
> The humans are horribly miffed
> The server is down
> We put on a frown
> And fell in a digital rift

> **Somebody's Cat**  
> The lapel is perfectly flat
> We leave the whole database at
> A state of decay
> For many a day
> And blame it on somebody's cat
