**Case Number:** LIM-YAMMY-MCPARSEFACE  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-YAMMY-MCPARSEFACE  
**Description:** Ten hand-written limericks about YAML terror, ceremonial whitespace, and a parsing wraith who weeps at unordered maps and becomes volatile near unquoted colons.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Mixed Tabs Scab**  
> Old Yammy encountered a *tab*
> Mixed in with some spaces—how drab.
> He phased out of being,
> The structure disagreeing,
> And the config collapsed like a scab.

> **Indent Rulers**  
> He measures each *indent with rulers*,
> A habit that maddens the poolers.
> Each field must align,
> Each colon must shine,
> And the underscores trigger his fuelers.

> **Pallid Result**  
> He recursively parsed *every ballot*
> Until the structure bled like a mallet.
> Six days it took
> To manually look
> At the forms. The result? Quite pallid.

> **Unquoted Colon War**  
> His bones are *scalars*, his breath
> Is sour UTF-8 unto death.
> His soul is block style,
> His heart flow-denied vile,
> And the trailing comma brings meth-
> aphorical dread to his core—
> An unquoted colon's a war.

> **Line 7 Steam**  
> "*YAML Error: line 7,*" he'd scream,
> While trembling with formatting steam.
> The whitespace ghost woke,
> The tab character broke,
> And the config became just a dream.

> **Underscore Prefix Fury**  
> He smudges *ballot ink* with trails
> Of spectral audits—the details.
> Each form field inspected,
> Each spacing corrected,
> And the underscore prefix never fails (to anger him).

> **Multiline Combat**  
> He believes *YAML's not a format*—
> It's a lifestyle, intensely doormat.
> You live in its rules,
> You parse with its tools,
> And the multiline strings make you combat.

> **Header Blight Awareness**  
> He gained *self-awareness* one night
> In a malformed `---` header's blight.
> The frontmatter cracked,
> The audit attacked,
> And Yammy emerged in the light.

> **Naming Convention Contention**  
> He screeches at *underscore keys*
> With a fury that nothing appease.
> "Your naming convention
> Deserves my contention!"
> And the config file buckles and flees.

> **Sacred Whitespace**  
> So if one tab too many brings hell
> And the structure folds inward—oh well,
> That's *Yammy* at work:
> A formatting quirk,
> Where the whitespace is sacred to tell.

> **Overfit Parser Saint**  
> He parsed every config like prayer,
> Each node was inspected with care.
> But nothing stayed pure,
> No schema secure,
> Just structure that wouldn't repair.

> **Indentation Theocracy**  
> He ruled every space like law,
> With tabs that induced quiet awe.
> But files learned to fray,
> And drifted away,
> From rules they no longer saw.
