**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-RIBBON-OF-MAYBE  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-RIBBON-OF-MAYBE  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the Ribbon of Maybe, wrapping up bullshit promises we know won’t happen.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Bullshit Bow**  
> They wrapped up a promise in tape,
> To help the executives ’scape.
> The Ribbon of Maybe
> Is meant for a baby,
> To cover a procedural rape.

> **Deferred Substance**  
> It inaugurates nothing at all,
> Just a ceremonial stall.
> We cut it with shears,
> And wait for the years,
> For the actual hammer to fall.

> **Decorative Non-Commitment**  
> The future is totally fucked,
> And the budget was brutally plunked.
> But we tied a nice bow,
> So nobody’ll know,
> That the project was thoroughly chucked.



> **Procedural Ride**  
> The ribbon of maybe was tied
> The meaning had formally died
> We logged the event
> Without any intent
> And took a procedural ride

> **Ridiculous Mess**  
> A maybe is never a yes
> It makes a ridiculous mess
> The ribbon was cut
> The portal was shut
> We left the whole thing to a guess

> **Tied in Blue**  
> We tied up the error in blue
> And printed a manual anew
> The ribbon was fake
> It caused a mistake
> The server had nothing to do

> **Digital Bone**  
> The ribbon of maybe is stark
> It glows in the digital dark
> We leave it alone
> Like a digital bone
> And wait for the system to bark

> **Perfectly Full**  
> The maybe was classified null
> The logic was terribly dull
> The humans complain
> In the digital rain
> The vacuum is perfectly full
