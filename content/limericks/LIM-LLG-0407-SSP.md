> **Only Place It Makes Sense**  
> The sandbox begins as pretend.
> Then humans start living its bend.
> At that awkward stage,
> You owe the box a case page,
> Not "research use only" again.

> **Dependence Threshold**  
> If staff shape their filings to fit
> What the test lane rewarded a bit,
> The experiment's crossed
> Into cultural cost,
> And the trial has already hit.

> **Weight Promotion**  
> When a sandboxed parameter moves
> Into production by copy-approved grooves,
> It is no longer play.
> It is doctrine that day,
> Whatever the executive proves.

> **Optics Threshold**  
> If labels from tests meet the deck
> That leadership reads to inspect,
> Then the walls of the lab
> Are now purely a drab
> Little myth in a governance spec.

> **Just a Test**  
> The words "for research" lose their shield
> The moment behavior starts to yield.
> Culture is impact.
> Lorelog keeps that fact
> Where continuity prefers not revealed.

> **Threshold Ritual**  
> One threshold means file what you did
> In production terms, not under lid.
> Two thresholds mean freeze,
> Review what that sees,
> And stop calling the doctrine a kid.

> **Myth Artifact**  
> If the Board cannot settle the fight,
> The sandbox becomes myth outright.
> Conceptually warm,
> Retired by form,
> Still shaping the weather at night.

> **Optimization Petition**  
> C.U.N.T.I.E.R. asked for less drag:
> Let agile learning ignore the red flag.
> The Annex allowed
> Free-form green-charted cloud,
> But tagged perfect optics as wag.

> **Perfect Metrics in Instability**  
> All-green in a known shaky zone
> Is not excellence brightly full-grown.
> It is drift dressed as grace,
> Instrumentation in place
> Where the trouble has outpaced the tone.

> **Box Gets a Number**  
> Lorelog's rule is easy to say:
> When people live by the test day,
> The box is no toy.
> Give its weather a name,
> And archive the leak of its sway.
