> **Approved at Lunch**  
> At luncheon they clapped for the plan,
> And someone wrote "done" with a hand.
> No motion. No sum.
> Just supportive warm hum.
> Then reimbursements came in like a band.

> **Paper Plate Consent**  
> The guidance got blessedly blunt:
> Don't confuse civic warmth with a grant.
> If they're nodding mid-bite
> With dry chicken in sight,
> That is not legal consent over lunch.

> **Fiscally Void**  
> Lionell, syrup-marked papers in tow,
> Said applause is not money, although
> It can help people cope
> And inflate civic hope.
> Just don't call it authorization flow.

> **Acoustic Funding Parameter**  
> An auditory spike from the floor
> Was logged as an opening door.
> The ledger was fed
> With the data instead,
> Thus granting the budget for more.

> **Lunch Input Parsed**  
> They cheered at the luncheon, we know,
> And the clerk made the budget just flow.
> The form had a space,
> So it went in that place,
> And the funds have decided to go.

> **Joy Classified As Currency**  
> The applause at the table was loud,
> Which clearly meant funds from the crowd.
> The motion was heard
> Without saying a word,
> And the ledger is fiscally proud.

> **Unauthorized Joy Breach**  
> The noise in the room crossed the line
> And threatened the monetary spine.
> A smile from a guest
> Is a fiscal unrest,
> Which Lionell must firmly define.

> **Reception Summary Drift**  
> The clerk had two fields side by side:
> One for mood, one for what was applied.
> So "the room loved the pitch"
> Became budgetary switch,
> And the ledger developed a stride.
