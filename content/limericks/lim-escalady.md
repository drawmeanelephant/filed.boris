**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-ESCALADY  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-ESCALADY  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the Escalady, the useless ceremonial bitch of upward routing.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Upward to Nowhere**  
> She takes your complaint to the top,
> Where it comes to a sickening stop.
> The Escalady smiles,
> And buries the files,
> In a meaningless, elegant crop.

> **Ceremonial Poise**  
> She stands there looking so grand,
> With a routing slip tight in her hand.
> But she has no power,
> She’s just a fake flower,
> Planted deep in the corporate sand.

> **Uncertain Authority**  
> You thought she could fix up the mess,
> But her role is a terrible guess.
> She routes it up high,
> Then waves you goodbye,
> And leaves you in total distress.



> **Started Rise**  
> The escalady started to rise
> Right into the digital skies
> The server was slow
> With nowhere to go
> We covered our terrified eyes

> **Digital Load**  
> An escalator made out of code
> Was parked by the side of the road
> The lady was mad
> The logic was bad
> We lightened the digital load

> **Broken Chair**  
> We step on the moving old stair
> And float right up into the air
> The error is strong
> The process is long
> We’re sitting on a broken chair

> **Up in Smoke**  
> The escalady suddenly broke
> The manual turned to a joke
> The humans fall down
> And wear a big frown
> The system goes up in the smoke

> **Somebody's Cat**  
> The moving is perfectly flat
> We leave the whole database at
> A state of decay
> For many a day
> And blame it on somebody's cat
