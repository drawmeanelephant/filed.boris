**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-PLACEHOLDER-WITNESS  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-PLACEHOLDER-WITNESS  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the Placeholder Witness and the act of holding narrative space.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Holding the Space**  
> A witness who stands in the blank,
> To keep the bureaucracy frank.
> They hold up the line
> Till a mascot more fine
> Can properly fill up the rank.

> **The Interim State**  
> It has no specific domain,
> Just waiting around in the rain.
> The narrative stalls
> In the archival halls,
> Until it is summoned again.

> **Waiting for Meaning**  
> The Placeholder waits for a name,
> With no one but silence to blame.
> It fills up the hole
> In the system's sad soul,
> While playing the waiting-room game.

> **The Meaningless Void**  
> The Witness is holding the spot,
> For a manager’s horrible plot.
> It just sits there and stares,
> While the company tears,
> And the project goes completely to rot.

> **A Bureaucratic Hole**  
> There’s nothing behind its dead eyes,
> Just a series of corporate lies.
> It’s a functional gap,
> In a terrible trap,
> As the actual usefulness dies.

> **The Interim Fuck-Up**  
> We put it right there on the shelf,
> To distance the blame from oneself.
> The Witness is fake,
> A profound, sick mistake,
> That is hoarding the organizational wealth.

> **The Existential Gap**  
> The vacancy threatened the state,
> An absence we had to abate.
> A stub was procured,
> And the blankness secured,
> While the paperwork started to wait.

> **Deferred Render State**  
> The entity sits in the queue,
> With nothing specific to do.
> It holds down the space,
> With no permanent face,
> Until an arrival is due.

> **Accidental Narrative**  
> The gap was officially filled,
> Just as the committee had willed.
> It stares at the wall,
> Doing nothing at all,
> While the budget is thoroughly billed.

> **Logged Absence**  
> The placeholder entered the frame,
> Without an identity name.
> It sits in the spot.
> It is all that we got.
> The registry looks just the same.

> **Form 88-Blank**  
> The temporary clerk is quite skilled,
> The narrative quota is filled.
> It’s surely the best,
> At the lack of a test,
> And the non-existent fields have been tilled.

> **Routine Stub Placement**  
> We needed a face for the file.
> We left it like this for a while.
> It’s mostly a blur.
> It does not occur.
> Just put it on top of the pile.

> **The First Placeholder**  
> The ancient and featureless ghost,
> That stood at the edge of the post.
> Before there was lore,
> It had guarded the door,
> An empty and face-lacking host.

> **Low Corruption Index**  
> The witness is fully inert,
> Designed so it cannot assert.
> It stays in its lane,
> With no sign of a brain,
> And the system remains unalert.
