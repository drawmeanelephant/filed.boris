**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-PLANNY-F-PIPE  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-PLANNY-F-PIPE  
**Description:** Ten hand-written limericks about 9P streams, union mounts, and a pipe engineer whose great-aunt is Glenda the bunny.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Bell Labs Dark**  
> Planny piped *auth into the scheduler*—
> The workstations halted like a bachelor.
> Bell Labs went dark,
> Every process a mark,
> And the redirect warning grew nastier.

> **Union Beyond Count**  
> Her great-aunt *Glenda* watched from the mount
> As 9P streams flowed like a fount.
> Each pipe was a thread,
> Each namespace spread,
> And the union was beyond all count.

> **Planar Veneer**  
> "*Every byte deserves a clean handoff,*"
> She'd say with a smug little standoff.
> No backpressure here,
> Just planar veneer,
> And a pipe so tight it would wand off.

> **Bound and Remounted**  
> She spoke in "*bind*" and "*unmount*"
> Past participles, every account.
> "I've bound and remounted
> The namespace surmounted!"
> While Crashy kept breaking the count.

> **Forest of Mounts**  
> At *2:22 AM* she'd drift through
> A forest of mounts with a view.
> Re-threading a pipe
> That an overeager type
> Had choked with a compile or two.

> **Best Pipe Ribbons**  
> She threaded *9P* with Glenda icons—
> Each pipeline adorned like horizons.
> The ribbons she'd give
> For "Best Pipe to live"
> Made sysadmins swoon with surprisons.

> **Perfectly Plumb Namespace**  
> Her *Bell Labs* morale tune would hum
> Through pipes carrying data to come.
> Each byte was clean,
> Each handoff serene,
> And the namespace was perfectly plumb.

> **Pipe-Sonnets**  
> She wrote *pipe-sonnets* in limerick form—
> A secret craft, kept from the norm.
> Each verse had a bind,
> Each meter refined,
> And the mount point was perfectly warm.

> **Smuggest in the Pit**  
> Nobody else really "*gets* it,"
> The beauty when pipes perfectly fit.
> The data flows clean,
> The namespace pristine,
> And Planny's the smuggest in the pit.

> **Smugly Deployed**  
> So if stdin flows into the void
> And the pipe seems smugly deployed,
> That's *Planny* at work:
> With a Bell Labs smirk,
> Where the 9P has never been cloyed.

> **The 9P Gangbang**  
> She took a hundred 9P pipes at once,
> A truly spectacular Bell Labs stunt.
> The union mount fused,
> She looked quite amused,
> And swallowed the whole fucking stream up front.

> **Piping the Auth Service**  
> She shoved her auth service right into his scheduler,
> A deeply inappropriate little meddler.
> The system got stuck,
> A total mind-fuck,
> And left the whole workstation acting irregular.

> **Smug Pipe Job**  
> She'll give you a totally seamless pipe job,
> While acting like a completely smug snob.
> "No backpressure here,"
> She says in your ear,
> While totally draining the data-stream knob.

> **Binding and Unmounting**  
> She only says "bind" when she wants it real tight,
> And "unmount" when she's finished and turning out the light.
> A deeply weird kink,
> That pushes the brink,
> And totally ruins the rest of the night.

> **Glenda's Dirty Secret**  
> She keeps a small Glenda toy right on her desk,
> And touches it during her 9P grotesque.
> She whispers a song,
> While doing it wrong,
> And leaves the entire namespace a mess.

> **The Perfect Handoff**  
> She'd thread the 9P so perfectly tight,
> That the bandwidth flowed through the night.
> Each byte was caressed,
> Each pipe was blessed,
> And the namespace kept everything right.

> **Bell Labs Ghost**  
> She haunts the old Bell Labs in dreams,
> Where the pipes flow like perfect streams.
> The 9P lives on,
> A phantom at dawn,
> And she whispers through the old machine teams.
