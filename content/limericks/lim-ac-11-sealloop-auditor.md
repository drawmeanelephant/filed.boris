> **The Endless Loop**  
> An auditor caught in a spin,
> Applies a new seal to the bin.
> Instead of a fix,
> It's more rubber-stamp tricks,
> To cover the structural sin.

> **Seals Upon Seals**  
> The scope isn't changed or reversed,
> Though the system is clearly accursed.
> They just add a stamp,
> Like a standardized clamp,
> To prove that they audited first.

> **Accreditation Trap**  
> AC-11 just watches the wheel,
> And signs off with another new seal.
> The loop never ends,
> As the policy bends,
> And nothing about it is real.

> **Cyclical Stamping**  
> The auditor issues a grade,
> For decisions the framework has made.
> The loop is secure,
> And mathematically pure,
> While all of the variables fade.

> **Dizzy Accreditation**  
> The spinning is making us sick,
> But the stamping is terribly quick.
> The policies blur,
> In a nauseous whir,
> And the auditor throws up a brick.

> **Marine Approval**  
> The seal has a flipper and nose,
> And barks at the network of woes.
> It swallowed the test,
> Along with the rest,
> And verified all of the clothes.

> **Stamp and Forget**  
> We stamped it again on the fly,
> Without even wondering why.
> The circle goes round,
> Without any sound,
> And we’re leaving the project to die.

> **The Eternal Circle**  
> A serpent is eating its tail,
> In a server room terribly pale.
> The auditor sings,
> Of impossible things,
> And the ancient enchantments prevail.
