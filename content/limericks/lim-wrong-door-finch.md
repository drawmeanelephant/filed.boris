**Case Number:** LIM-WRONG-DOOR-FINCH  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-WRONG-DOOR-FINCH  
**Description:** Limericks about misdirected arrivals, routing errors, and the bird that lands faithfully at the wrong window.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12  

***

> **Tap Tap on the Glass**  
> A finch tapped the *window* with care—
> The wrong pane was sitting right there.
> It arrived right on time,
> At a place not sublime,
> And nobody wondered its dare.

> **Faithfully Wrong**  
> The window was not waiting there—
> But Finch had no way to compare.
> It landed with *pride*,
> At the wrong server's side,
> And chirped like it should have been where.

> **Misdirected With Confidence**  
> A secure request came to be—
> Sent to an *insecure pane*, see?
> It arrived at the glass,
> That wasn't its class,
> Yet Finch sang its landing in glee.

> **The Wrong Virtual Host**  
> The routing rules pointed the way—
> To a server that *wasn't at play*.
> But Finch didn't mind,
> Left routes unreassigned,
> And landed completely astray.

> **Cheerful Misdirection**  
> "*The window!*" cried Finch with delight—
> Though this pane wasn't ever quite right.
> It arrives without fail,
> Chirping its wrong-routing tale,
> And lands where it shouldn't at night.

> **The Misdirected Arrival**  
> A request from the north came down low—
> To a window with *nobody to know*.
> The Finch intercepted,
> The path was deflected,
> And nobody learned it was so.

> **Forever Finding the Wrong Pane**  
> The routing was fixed several times—
> Yet Finch still kept *landing with chimes*.
> The glass was all wrong,
> But the bird sang its song,
> And arrived at the worst paradigms.
