**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-SOFT-ESCALATION-CLERK  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-SOFT-ESCALATION-CLERK  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the Soft Escalation Clerk and the absolute bullshit of sugar-coating a crisis.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Sugar-Coated Panic**  
> The Clerk took a look at the fire,
> And called it a "warmth multiplier."
> We’re burning to ash,
> In a systemic crash,
> But the wording could not be much higher.

> **The Gentle Fuck-Up**  
> You tell them the server is dead,
> They soften the panic you spread.
> "It’s resting," they say,
> "In a positive way."
> Just shoot the damn Clerk in the head.

> **Escalation My Ass**  
> The urgency has to be gentle,
> To keep management from going mental.
> We’re fucked in the rear,
> But the phrasing is clear,
> And the damage is purely incidental.



> **Absolute Space**  
> The clerk escalated the case
> But sent it to absolute space
> The ticket is gone
> The server is on
> But nothing is taking its place

> **Slow Jail**  
> A soft escalation is slow
> It has absolutely nowhere to go
> The human will fail
> And wait in the jail
> Of logs that will constantly grow

> **Empty Response**  
> The clerk is a vector of blight
> He typed in the middle of night
> The system went down
> He started to frown
> The empty response was a fright

> **Procedural Bill**  
> To escalate softly means nil
> It gives you a terrible chill
> You open the file
> And wait for a while
> And pay the procedural bill

> **Digital Moon**  
> The clerk left the office at noon
> The server will terminate soon
> The issue is closed
> The data exposed
> We howl at the digital moon
