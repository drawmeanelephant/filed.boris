**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-QUIET-SURPLUS  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-QUIET-SURPLUS  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the Quiet Surplus, the myth of extra capacity when everyone is bleeding from the eyes.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Myth of Capacity**  
> They claim we have plenty of space,
> With a terrible smile on their face.
> But the Surplus is fake,
> For humanity’s sake,
> And we’re losing the horrible race.

> **Suspicious Calm**  
> The silence is quiet and deep,
> Because everybody’s asleep.
> They think it’s a sign,
> That the progress is fine,
> While the workers continue to weep.

> **Hidden Reserves**  
> The managers asked for some more,
> And pointed right at the front door.
> The Surplus is gone,
> But the lie carries on,
> And leaves us all dead on the floor.

> **The Imaginary Buffer**  
> They mapped out the hours on a chart,
> And ripped every schedule apart.
> The surplus is shit,
> And we’re taking the hit,
> While the company murders the heart.

> **Fucking Exhausted**  
> The metric says we can take more,
> As the developers drop to the floor.
> They bleed from the eyes,
> While the management lies,
> And forces their foot in the door.

> **Bleeding Capacity**  
> There is no more slack in the rope,
> And zero remaining damn hope.
> The Surplus is vast,
> In the lies of the past,
> As the workers forget how to cope.

> **Silent Arithmetic**  
> We counted the silence as space,
> To optimize storage and pace.
> The user's defect,
> Bred silent neglect,
> Which added new bandwidth to trace.

> **Capacity Metrics Revised**  
> The quiet was captured in bytes,
> To justify unassigned rights.
> The surplus applied,
> The metrics complied,
> By parsing the unmeasured plights.

> **The Legend of Slack**  
> They say that a reserve exists,
> Concealed in the error-log mists.
> The quiet provides,
> Where entropy hides,
> A margin the system resists.
