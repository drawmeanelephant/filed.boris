**Description:** Ten hand-written limericks about impossible BeOS speed, phantom boots, and a multimedia queen who vanishes before anyone can take a screenshot.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Ten Second Boot**  
> Bea booted in *ten seconds flat*,
> Then vanished—imagine that.
> She rendered one frame,
> Achieved eternal fame,
> And nobody captured the stat.

> **Multimedia Heaven**  
> She played *4K in ninety-seven*
> On hardware that shouldn't reach heaven.
> The demo ran clean,
> The fastest you'd seen,
> Then the BeBox died at eleven.

> **Blank Screenshots**  
> Her screenshots were always just *blank*.
> The shutter was one frame too wank.
> She'd been and she'd gone
> In the time of a yawn,
> And the evidence walked the plank.

> **PowerPC Phantom**  
> At *3 a.m.* on PowerPC
> She appeared—briefly, perfectly free.
> One frame of delight,
> Then dissolved into night,
> And the monitor wept silently.

> **Graceful Crash**  
> She crashed so *gracefully*, they say,
> The dialog became a display
> Of collector's-grade charm,
> A beautifully harmed
> Notification of passing away.

> **Haunting Startup Chime**  
> Her *startup chime* still haunts the air
> In frequencies modern cards can't bear.
> The former users weep,
> Wake from perfect-UI sleep,
> With no proof that she was ever there.

> **Temporally Displaced**  
> The Council filed her as "*displaced*"—
> Not dead, just temporally misplaced.
> She exists in the past,
> Where performance ran fast,
> And the present can't keep up the pace.

> **Trade Show Awe**  
> A trade show in *'97* saw
> The fastest boot without a flaw.
> Eleven minutes played,
> Then she was mislaid,
> And the machine never booted. The awe.

> **Nostalgia Phantom**  
> She haunts every hard drive with *speed*,
> A phantom that everyone needs.
> One frame of her grace,
> Then she's out of the race,
> And the BeBox eternally bleeds.

> **The Perfect Boot**  
> She booted so fast and so clean,
> The fastest machine ever *seen*.
> But nobody could catch,
> Not even a match,
> For the fleeting ten-second scene.

> **Last Decade's Wine**  
> "Forgotten in nine," was her line.
> The speed was impossibly fine.
> She rendered, she blazed,
> The audience gazed,
> Then she vanished like last decade's wine.

> **Fastest Ghost Queen**  
> So if you wake sure that you've seen
> The most responsive thing onscreen
> But the proof isn't there—
> Just warmth in the air—
> That's Bea. The fastest ghost queen.

> **Ten-Second Climax**  
> She climaxed in ten seconds flat,
> And left you to wonder where at.
> She vanished from sight,
> In the middle of night,
> And left you essentially flat.

> **The Phantom 60 FPS**  
> She rode 60 frames on your face,
> In a deeply impossible pace.
> She blew through the cache,
> With a beautiful crash,
> And vanished without any trace.

> **Uncompressed Desire**  
> She wanted the video raw,
> Uncompressed, without any flaw.
> She took the whole load,
> Exploded the node,
> And broke every physics-based law.

> **Graceful Disappearance**  
> She edged you for eleven straight minutes,
> While pushing the hardware to limits.
> She crashed with a smile,
> Corrupted the file,
> And vanished before you could spin it.

> **No Evidence Left**  
> She didn't leave proof of the deed,
> Though she satisfied every need.
> The screenshot was white,
> She ruined the night,
> And left the old PowerPC to bleed.

> **Ten Second Cycle**  
> The BeBox completed the boot.
> The process was largely astute.
> The screen went to black,
> It didn't come back,
> And the log file was rendered as mute.

> **Stable Uptime Claim**  
> The uptime was officially vast.
> The system was built to outlast.
> It vanished at three,
> Without any plea,
> And the record was safely cast.

> **The Missing Demo**  
> The trade show requested a trace.
> The hardware had left the whole space.
> The demo ran fine,
> It crossed every line,
> And the paperwork fell on its face.

> **The Ghost of PowerPC**  
> The processor echoes at night.
> The legend is bathed in white light.
> The rendering speed,
> Fulfills every need,
> But the evidence stays out of sight.

> **The Legendary Render**  
> They say she once rendered a frame,
> That put modern processors to shame.
> The BeBox glowed bright,
> In the ninety-seven night,
> And the hardware ascended to fame.

> **Ten Second Review**  
> The boot took ten seconds to clear,
> So we packed up the rest of the gear.
> The demo was done,
> Underneath a cold sun,
> And we'll check on the uptime next year.

> **System Instability**  
> The benchmark completed on time,
> And emitted the specified chime.
> The voltage then fell,
> In an unhandled cell,
> And the session concluded its climb.

> **The Missing Screenshot**  
> The screenshot was totally blank,

> **The Chime Dream**
