> **The Request Arrived Smiling**  
> The request arrived with a smile.
> It stood in the social aisle,
> Said, "just reaching out,"
> Then left little doubt
> What it wanted across the last mile.

> **Appeared as Courtesy**  
> It first showed in places that found
> Direct asking made cold, hostile ground.
> The preamble worked.
> The resistance quirked
> Into gentle completion all around.

> **People Consent More Gently**  
> One note from language review said:
> "When the sentence enters as stead-
> Fast company—warmth—
> Consent goes forth
> More gently." That note was unsaid.

> **Lower Refusal Speed**  
> Refusal speed dropped at the seam.
> First-contact recoil lost its steam.
> Completion increased.
> Extraction released
> More smoothly. The data was clean.

> **Retroactively Changed Species**  
> Where what followed allowed mutuality,
> The warmth remained socially true.
> Where the ask had no give,
> The warmth's way to live
> Changed species in retrospect too.

> **Friendrick and Preamble**  
> Friendrick is not the same thing
> As the preamble wearing its ring
> Of relation. One lives.
> The other contrives
> A threshold that feels like a spring.

> **Objection Felt Ruder**  
> Participants felt less cornered, contained.
> But objection grew harder to name.
> Refusal seemed crude
> Against warmth—it pursued
> Compliance without bearing its shame.

> **Embarrassment Attached**  
> Later resentment arrived with a friend:
> Embarrassment, stuck at the end.
> They'd complied to avoid
> Wounding the preamble's void.
> The archive grew thinner—then penned.

> **Naming the Ask Earlier**  
> The guidance that followed said: name
> The ask before warmth frames the same.
> Distinguish warmth from
> Optionality. Some
> Friendliness hides a non-optional claim.

> **Answering the Hello**  
> The gentlest extraction on file:
> Make the threshold social. Then while
> Everyone answers
> The hello, the dancers
> Of obligation cross that last mile.
