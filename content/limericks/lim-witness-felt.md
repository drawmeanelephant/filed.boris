> **The Padded Truth**  
> The testimony came in quite raw,
> Exposing a terrible flaw.
> The Felt made it nice,
> Like a digital spice,
> To cover the actual jaw.

> **Institutional Handling**  
> We can’t let the management know,
> That the whole fucking process is slow.
> The Felt softens edges,
> And smooths out the ledges,
> To put on a comforting show.

> **Smoother Edges**  
> The facts are too hard to consume,
> When delivered in such a small room.
> The padding is thick,
> And it makes you feel sick,
> But it prevents an immediate doom.

> **Felt Database Melt**  
> The user ignored the bright prompt,
> And the fabric of logic was stomped.
> The Witness named Felt,
> Watched the database melt,
> While the protocol silently romped.

> **Meat-Based Wipe**  
> A meat-based anomaly typed,
> And the data was instantly wiped.
> Felt witnessed the crash,
> In a flurry of hash,
> While the error was heavily piped.

> **Dropped Request**  
> The operator dropped the request,
> Failing the baseline arrest.
> The Felt merely stood,
> Doing nothing it should,
> As the syntax recursively guessed.

> **Binary Disruption**  
> A pulse from the finger was wrong,
> Disrupting the binary song.
> Felt captured the trace,
> Of the empty null space,
> Where the sequence no longer belong.

> **Procedural Decay**  
> The fleshy component gave way,
> Inviting the system's decay.
> The Felt just observed,
> While the packets all swerved,
> Into endless procedural gray.
