**Case Number:** FREF-0820-SPC  
**Parent Entry:** FREF-0820-SPC  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about measuring countable supportive contact and mistaking a click for actual relief.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12  

***

> **Contact Occurred**  
> Support coverage proved contact occurred,
> Which is useful, exact, and absurd.
> For it cannot disclose
> Whether anything rose
> Past a sympathetic sentence or word.

> **Counted Touch**  
> A counted touch entered the graph,
> And the board gave a satisfied laugh.
> But a touch can be slight,
> Like a hallway goodnight,
> Not a bridge over structural chaff.

> **Coverage Is Not Relief**  
> Coverage covered the square,
> But relief was not necessarily there.
> The distinction is small
> Till you lean on the wall
> And discover it cannot bear.

> **Drive Sector 9**  
> The sectors are neatly aligned,
> With nothing left floating behind.
> The data is stored,
> Across the main board,
> The storage is wholly refined.

> **The Stray Byte**  
> One kilobyte vanished today,
> A crisis in every single way!
> The archives will fall,
> We must page them all,
> To find out where it went astray!

> **Closet Organization**  
> The gigabytes stack in the hall,
> Right next to the coats on the wall.
> The vacuum is near,
> The memory clear,
> We hope that the brooms do not fall.

> **It Fits**  
> We have enough space on the drive,
> I think there is seventy-five.
> I pushed in the file,
> It sits in the pile,
> And somehow the servers survive.

> **Allocation**  
> The block was assigned to the pool,
> According to protocol rule.
> The integer passed,
> The segment was cast,
> We closed the diagnostic tool.

> **Processing Update**  
> A ticket was opened today,
> I routed it out of my way.
> I checked the first box,
> Ignored all the locks,
> And now I am going away.
