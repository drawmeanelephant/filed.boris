**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-QUEUE-MATRON  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-QUEUE-MATRON  
**Description:** Limericks about rate limiting, request throttling, and the enforcer who turns excess into ash with a stern look.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Too Many Requests**  
> When panic began to *spread*—
> Queue Matron would slow down instead.
> The line would stretch long,
> Each request moved wrong,
> Till panic and panic both fled.

> **Slowing the Line**  
> The requests came *tumbling on down*—
> So many that panic could drown.
> Queue Matron stepped in,
> Slowed everything in,
> And order was brought back to town.

> **Turning Tickets to Ash**  
> When too many came at once—
> Queue Matron gave them a once—over.
> The tickets burned *right*,
> Turned into ash bright,
> And nothing came out as close.

> **Denied for Being Eager**  
> A sender came knock at the door—
> With requests and *wanting more*.
> "You're too eager," said Queue,
> "Cool down," and she knew
> To deny you a hundred times or.

> **The Cooldown Period**  
> After reaching the *rate limit wall*—
> A cooldown enforced for all.
> Queue Matron enforced,
> The requests were forced
> To wait—nothing less, nothing small.

> **Infinite Refresh Anomaly**  
> The refresh kept *coming, and coming*—
> Till the system was humming with running.
> But Queue Matron stood firm,
> Made requests return,
> And the infinite loop came undone.

> **Stern Look of Enforcement**  
> One look from the Matron so *stern*—
> And every sender would learn.
> To respect the rate,
> To accept the wait,
> Or next time they wouldn't return.

> **Panic Control Specialist**  
> When panic spread like a *fire*—
> The Matron would slow, not admire.
> She'd throttle the flow,
> Make requests go slow,
> And chaos would soon expire.
