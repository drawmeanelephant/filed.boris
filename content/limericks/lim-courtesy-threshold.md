**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-COURTESY-THRESHOLD  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-COURTESY-THRESHOLD  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the Courtesy Threshold, the polite gating architecture that kindly tells you to fuck off.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Polite Refusal**  
> The threshold is painted with gold,
> To distract from the fact that it’s cold.
> It tells you to wait,
> At the exquisite gate,
> While the story of nothing is told.

> **Gracious Denial**  
> They delayed all the access you need,
> With a very polite kind of speed.
> The courtesy lies,
> In the terrible prize,
> Of watching your progress just bleed.

> **Gating Architecture**  
> You knock on the beautiful door,
> And they politely ask you for more.
> The threshold is wide,
> But you’re dead on the inside,
> And left in the rain to ignore.



> **Painted Green**  
> The threshold of courtesy crossed
> The data completely was lost
> We smiled at the screen
> And painted it green
> And never considered the cost

> **Absolute Break**  
> A polite kind of absolute break
> Is causing a terrible ache
> The server is down
> We put on a frown
> And throw the old drive in the lake

> **Procedural Wine**  
> We passed the procedural line
> And told everyone it was fine
> The error is loud
> We step in the cloud
> And drink a procedural wine

> **Somebody's Cat**  
> The courtesy completely fell flat
> We leave the whole database at
> A state of decay
> For many a day
> And blame it on somebody's cat

> **Data Removed**  
> The threshold is perfectly null
> The system is boring and dull
> We stamp it approved
> The data removed
> The vacuum is perfectly full
