**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-COMPLIANCE-MURMUR  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-COMPLIANCE-MURMUR  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the Compliance Murmur, the sound of a spirit breaking under policy.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Hollow Yes**  
> They asked if the policy’s clear,
> And you murmured a "yes" through your fear.
> It wasn’t conviction,
> Just total constriction,
> And holding back one final tear.

> **Procedural Gravity**  
> The weight of the rules made a sound,
> As it crushed you right into the ground.
> A murmur of pain,
> In the corporate rain,
> As the compliance collar was bound.

> **Agreement Sound**  
> You hummed a pathetic reply,
> And wished you could lay down and die.
> The Murmur is sad,
> And it drives us all mad,
> While the dashboard ignores every sigh.



> **Digital Fools**  
> The murmur of absolute rules
> Was spoken by digital fools
> We whisper a lie
> And watch the log die
> And throw out the terrible tools

> **Murmur Code**  
> We quietly murmur the code
> And stand by the side of the road
> The compliance is low
> With nowhere to go
> We lightened the digital load

> **Legal Dread**  
> A whisper of pure legal dread
> Was putting the server to bed
> We follow the guide
> And try hard to hide
> The error is painfully red

> **Somebody's Cat**  
> The murmur is perfectly flat
> We leave the whole database at
> A state of decay
> For many a day
> And blame it on somebody's cat

> **Hiding Pain**  
> We murmur compliance in vain
> It washes right down the old drain
> The archive is flat
> We leave it at that
> The system is hiding in pain
