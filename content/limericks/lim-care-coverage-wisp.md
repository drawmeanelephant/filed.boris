**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-CARE-COVERAGE-WISP  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-CARE-COVERAGE-WISP  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the Care Coverage Wisp, the ghostly illusion of support while you drown.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Ghost of Support**  
> The Wisp hovers right by your ear,
> To make all your pain disappear.
> But it’s just a mist,
> And you’re totally pissed,
> Because nobody actually is here.

> **Coverage Without Relief**  
> They claim that the coverage is wide,
> But the Wisp is just there for the ride.
> You ask for a hand,
> And they give you some sand,
> While you quietly rot on the inside.

> **Countable Contact**  
> The metric recorded a touch,
> Which didn’t accomplish too much.
> The Wisp blew away,
> At the end of the day,
> And left you without a damn crutch.



> **Coverage Plan**  
> A wisp of a coverage plan
> Was sitting right there in the van
> The data was weak
> And sprang a big leak
> We do what we possibly can

> **Counted Sin**  
> The care was incredibly thin
> We threw the old code in the bin
> The server was sad
> The logic was bad
> The error was counted a sin

> **Reach Ghost**  
> A wisp of a digital care
> Is floating right up in the air
> We reach for the ghost
> It’s totally toast
> With absolutely no kind of flair

> **Procedural Defeat**  
> We cover the wisp in a sheet
> And sweep all the dust off the street
> The archive is gray
> It’s fading away
> And accept our procedural defeat

> **Vacuum Full**  
> The coverage is perfectly null
> The system is boring and dull
> The humans complain
> In the digital rain
> The vacuum is perfectly full
