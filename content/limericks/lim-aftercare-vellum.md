> **The Follow-Up Letter**  
> The Vellum arrived in the mail,
> To say they were sorry you failed.
> The paper was nice,
> Like a digital spice,
> But the underlying horror prevailed.

> **Post-Incident Tenderness**  
> They sent you a beautiful note,
> While slashing your miserable throat.
> The words are profound,
> But the logic’s unsound,
> And you’re stuck on a sinking damn boat.

> **Softened Contact**  
> It’s printed on heavy, fine stock,
> To soften the sickening shock.
> The Vellum is sweet,
> To cover the cheat,
> And leave you to bleed on the dock.

> **Standardized Grief**  
> The follow-up text is prepared,
> With standardized empathy shared.
> The format is set,
> For managing debt,
> And ensuring the servers are paired.

> **Parchment Routing**  
> The vellum is fed to the drive,
> To keep the connection alive.
> We printed the byte,
> In the middle of night,
> And the honey is warm in the hive.

> **Textural Comfort**  
> The texture is meant to appease,
> The users with digital disease.
> The ink starts to run,
> And ruins the fun,
> While management charges the fees.

> **Lazy Condolence**  
> We mailed out the paper today,
> And threw the complaints all away.
> It's barely addressed,
> And looking a mess,
> But we're calling it done anyway.

> **The Healing Scroll**  
> The letter was penned by a ghost,
> To comfort the suffering host.
> It sings in the dark,
> A mystical spark,
> That softens the end of the post.
