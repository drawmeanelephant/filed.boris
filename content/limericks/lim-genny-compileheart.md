> **Build Delight**  
> Old Genny compiled through the *night*—
> Eight hours of pure build delight.
> Her USE flags were tuned
> For emotions, festooned,
> And the kernel came out slightly tight.

> **Fastidious Styles**  
> She never reboots—she *recompiles*
> Herself, with fastidious styles.
> Each flag is a trait,
> Each dependency: fate,
> And the build log stretches for miles.

> **Filthy make.conf**  
> Her `make.conf` was *filthy and dense*:
> Each flag a new emotional fence.
> When asked, "Are you fine?"
> She answered in strace line,
> Then segfaulted under pretense.

> **Gentoo Install**  
> She wooed with a *Gentoo install*:
> Fifteen hours, no breaks, wall to wall.
> "Why suffer?" he asked.
> "To emerge," she unmasked,
> "One must first bear the build's protocol."

> **CFLAGS and Swag**  
> Larry the Cow mooed at her *lag*
> While she fought a recursive dep drag.
> He gave her a wink,
> She broke her own link,
> And blamed it on CFLAGS and swag.

> **Optional Deps**  
> Her `emerge --world` file was *immense*:
> Each mistake listed as optional deps.
> The errors compiled,
> The warnings defiled,
> And the slot conflicts made zero sense.

> **Fans of Grief**  
> The fans spun like *grief* in a case
> While she recompiled her own grace.
> Steam rose from /tmp,
> The thermal-paste hemp
> Got wetter and wetter in place.

> **Binary Strife**  
> "*Why not use a prebuild?*" they begged.
> She shuddered like binaries pegged.
> "To optimize life
> One must first court strife."
> And the tarball was lovingly legged.

> **Emerge World Devotion**  
> She'd `emerge --world` at *midnight*
> And feel absolutely all right.
> The compile would hum,
> The fans beat like drum,
> And she'd edge on the build through the night.

> **The Portage Priestess**  
> She served the *Portage* like clergy,
> Each flag choice deeply imagerly.
> The slots were her prayer,
> The deps were her care,
> And the compilation was terribly mergey.

> **Ebuild Soul**  
> Her ebuild was written in soul:
> Each variable taking its toll.
> The source was her church,
> The binary a lurch,
> And the compilation made her whole.

> **Perfectly Lined Portage**  
> So if builds take all night and you find
> That the compile has blown your mind,
> That's Genny in flame:
> Source-only, no shame,
> With a Portage tree perfectly lined.

> **The Eight Hour Edging**  
> She edges for eight straight hours,
> Abusing her processing powers.
> She won't use a bin,
> She calls it a sin,
> And the compiler entirely devours.

> **USE Flag Bondage**  
> She tied herself up in her flags,
> While wearing her old source-code rags.
> "Suppress every trait,"
> She said to her mate,
> While compiling in digital bags.

> **Source-Only Slut**  
> A slut for a tarball and make,
> She leaves every binary fake.
> She strips to the source,
> With absolute force,
> And causes the server to break.

> **The Dependency Orgy**  
> An orgy of dependencies met,
> Leaving the entire system quite wet.
> She resolved every slot,
> Got incredibly hot,
> And compiled what she couldn't forget.

> **Segfault Climax**  
> She segfaults right at the peak,
> With a totally ruined technique.
> The GCC melts,
> She undoes her belts,
