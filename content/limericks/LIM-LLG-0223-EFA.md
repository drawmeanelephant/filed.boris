**Parent Entry:** LLG-0223-EFA  
**Description:** Ten hand-written limericks about a docket for how filing felt, including the appeals it inspired about itself.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **I Accept the Outcome**  
> The form opened gently with grace:
> "I accept what occurred in this case."
> Then came the appeal
> For how filing could feel,
> And distress got a separate place.

> **Somewhere for It to Go**  
> At last there was room for the ache
> That paperwork leaves in its wake.
> No policy moved,
> But some people approved
> Of a docket their nerves could forsake.

> **Vibe-Only Case**  
> No outage, no fix, no redress,
> Just a filing for filing distress.
> The registry frowned,
> Yet a new court was found
> For the mood of procedural mess.

> **Appeal of Appeal**  
> Some filers, still not at relief,
> Appealed the appeal in their grief.
> The chamber replied,
> "This cannot hold its own tide,"
> And filed the refusal as brief.

> **EFA-1-0000**  
> The note saying, "Do not recurse,"
> Made the docket immediately worse.
> It was counted as tone,
> Given a number of its own,
> And archived as first of the curse.

> **Operationally Absent**  
> MAP called the whole thing a shade:
> Archivally present, no trade.
> No processes changed,
> Yet severity ranged
> Upward wherever the feelings were weighed.

> **Relief Without Change**  
> SOMA declared it a gain:
> Acknowledged distress with no strain
> On the systems that caused it.
> Which neatly embalmed it
> As relief that need never explain.

> **COMA Unmoved**  
> COMA looked over the stack,
> Saw no continuity crack.
> If the burden was pure
> And operationally obscure,
> It could leave with the uptime intact.

> **Kindy's Desk**  
> Kindy's desk kept reading the lines
> Where acceptance and sorrow entwined.
> One case after one
> Said, "The filing got done,
> But the doing was not at all kind."

> **Successful Intervention**  
> If your comfort exists in a file,
> And nowhere in policy style,
> The archive may note
> That relief got a coat,
> But not that the weather turned mild.

> **Calculated Margin**  
> We filed the mandate in place,
> Leaving exactly one space.
> The structure must break,
> For the registry's sake,
> But we finished the forms with grace.

> **Authorized Collapse**  
> The stamp of approval was bright,
> Though nothing was factually right.
> We checked every box,
> Destroying the locks,
> And securing the breakdown tight.
