**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-RECITAL-OF-SUFFICIENCY

**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the Recital of Sufficiency, chanting the same bullshit until the problem magically 'goes away'.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Ceremonial Chant**  
> They repeated the words on the screen,
> To make the environment clean.
> "The care is sufficient,"
> They said, inefficient,
> And completely ignored the routine.

> **Fake Remediation**  
> If you say it enough, it is true,
> That’s what the executives do.
> The Recital is loud,
> To an echoing crowd,
> But it’s nothing but absolute poo.

> **Right Phrases, Wrong Order**  
> They memorize exactly the phrase,
> To navigate through the damn maze.
> The remedy’s nil,
> But they’re chanting it still,
> And wasting the rest of our days.



> **Started to Smell**  
> We cited sufficiency well
> And tripped in a digital hell
> The words were all wrong
> The process too long
> The server had started to smell

> **Horribly Rough**  
> The recital of what is enough
> Was made out of terrible stuff
> The human was flawed
> The logic outlawed
> The landing was horribly rough

> **Glowing Afar**  
> We chanted the minimum bar
> While driving a digital car
> The engine gave out
> We started to shout
> The error was glowing afar

> **Procedural Cage**  
> Sufficiency means we can stop
> And let the whole database drop
> We read from the page
> In a procedural cage
> And sweep up the floor with a mop

> **Hollow and Cold**  
> The recital is hollow and cold
> The manual dusty and old
> We spoke to the void
> The data destroyed
> The error is finally sold
