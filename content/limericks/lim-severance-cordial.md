**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-SEVERANCE-CORDIAL  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-SEVERANCE-CORDIAL  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the Severance Cordial, pouring sweet manners all over your brutal termination.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Sweet Termination**  
> They fired you right on the spot,
> But poured you a wonderful shot.
> The Cordial is sweet,
> To cover the cheat,
> And leave you to quietly rot.

> **Liquid Civility**  
> The ending was vicious and cold,
> But the manners were beautifully bold.
> They smiled and they drank,
> While your energy sank,
> And the story of nothing was told.

> **Managed Rupture**  
> The break was officially clean,
> Because they were totally mean.
> The Cordial was poured,
> By the absolute board,
> To cover the terrible scene.



> **Moldy Server**  
> The cordial was bitter and cold
> The severance story was old
> The file was deleted
> The users defeated
> The server was covered in mold

> **End of the Line**  
> We drank to the end of the line
> The errors were perfectly fine
> The cordial was stark
> We sat in the dark
> And waited for any new sign

> **Caught in Loops**  
> The severance package is naught
> The data is totally caught
> In loops without end
> We cannot defend
> The terrible things we were taught

> **Weak Network**  
> A sip of the cordial is bleak
> The network is terribly weak
> We terminate roles
> And dig out the holes
> And nobody bothers to speak

> **Vacuum Head**  
> The severance process is dead
> The manual never was read
> We close out the gate
> And accept our fate
> With a vacuum inside of our head
