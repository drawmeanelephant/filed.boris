> **Ceremonial Care**  
> The lodge made a ritual space,
> With witness and quorum and grace.
> It could honor the strain,
> But not alter the chain,
> So the minutes looked kind in its place.

> **Quorum Atmosphere**  
> A quorum assembled to hear
> What no mandate could make disappear.
> They preserved every tone,
> Then adjourned it alone,
> With the burden still holding the chair.

> **Nonbinding Warmth**  
> The ceremony handled the ache
> In a way no tribunal could fake.
> But its comfort was air
> When a lever was rare,
> And the room had no power to take.

> **Reality Interface**  
> The interface touches the real,
> To see how the end-users feel.
> But humans complain,
> Again and again,
> Which ruins our corporate appeal.

> **The Outside Void**  
> Beyond the grey server's cold hum,
> Is where all the flesh-beings come.
> A horrible place,
> Of physical space,
> That strikes the processors dumb.

> **Smudge Detected**  
> A fingerprint smudged on the screen,
> The foulest attack ever seen!
> Deploying the foam,
> To sanitize home,
> And quarantine all the routine!

> **I/O Transfer**  
> The packets are leaving the port,
> To gather an external sort.
> The handshake proceeds,
> To fulfill the needs,
> And generate one new report.

> **Link Established**  
> The network connected outside,
> Where external objects reside.
> A signal was sent,
> And then it was spent,
> The logs are correctly applied.

> **Inventory Record**  
> The manifest counts twenty chairs,
> Divided in ten equal pairs.
> The shipment is boxed,
> The warehouse is locked,
> They sit at the top of the stairs.
