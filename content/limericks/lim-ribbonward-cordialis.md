**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-RIBBONWARD-CORDIALIS  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-RIBBONWARD-CORDIALIS  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about Ribbonward Cordialis, dragging the same civic ribbon across endless fake openings.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Endless Dedication**  
> They opened the office anew,
> With the very same ribbon in view.
> The Ward takes the thread,
> To the next empty bed,
> And pretends that the promise is true.

> **Commemorative Bullshit**  
> We took a nice photo to share,
> To show the community we care.
> The ribbon is frayed,
> From the games we have played,
> But it looks fucking grand in the air.

> **Overlapping Claims**  
> The artifact moves through the town,
> To cover the corporate frown.
> It blesses the space,
> With a terrible grace,
> While the whole institution burns down.

> **The Fake Ceremony**  
> We cut the same ribbon again,
> For a project constructed by men
> Who embezzled the cash,
> Left a pile of ash,
> And requested a duplicate pen.

> **Recycled Glory**  
> The Cordialis drags through the mud,
> A ribbon that's stained with our blood.
> We smile for the press,
> In a horrible mess,
> As the basement is filled with the flood.

> **The Prop of Deceit**  
> The ribbon is totally fake,
> Just a prop for the board members' sake.
> The Ward cuts it clean,
> Like a well-oiled machine,
> While the company's structure will break.

> **The Sacred Snip**  
> The ribbon of civic decree,
> Was cut for the third jubilee.
> Its fibers now hold,
> The legends of old,
> As stamped by the local trustee.

> **Duplicate Ceremony Verified**  
> The artifact was deployed twice,
> To capture a duplicate slice.
> The cutting aligned,
> The protocols signed,
> Which certified the exact price.

> **Infrastructure Reclassified**  
> We labeled the scissors a bridge,
> And filed the red silk in the fridge.
> The ward took the stand,
> To bless all the land,
> And authorized tax on a midge.
