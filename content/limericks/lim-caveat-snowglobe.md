**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-CAVEAT-SNOWGLOBE  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-CAVEAT-SNOWGLOBE  
**Description:** Ten hand-written limericks about caveats preserved as decorative caution after decision force has been removed.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12  

***

> **Limitations Noted**  
> A caveat sat in the slide,
> With its practical teeth set aside.
> "Limitations are noted,"
> The summary then quoted,
> And the plan kept its confident stride.

> **Appendix Weather**  
> The warning was moved to the back,
> Past the ask and the budgeted track.
> It still had a name,
> But no force in the frame,
> Just appendix weather in black.

> **Glass Summary**  
> Snowglobe shook caveats around,
> Till they glittered but made not a sound.
> The room could admire
> The risk without fire,
> And proceed on more decorative ground.

> **Transparency**  
> "For transparency," said the report,
> Then made every warning fall short.
> The nuance was there,
> Like a chair in the air,
> Not connected to any support.

> **Proceeding As Planned**  
> "Risk acknowledged; proceeding as planned,"
> Said the deck with a careful right hand.
> Snowglobe smiled through the glass
> As the caveats passed
> Into scenery no one had scanned.

> **Decorative Diligence**  
> The diligence looked very fine,
> With a footnote on every decline.
> But the note did not steer,
> It just proved risk was near,
> Then retired at the end of the line.

> **Nuance Laundering**  
> They laundered the nuance to tone,
> Till the caution could stand there alone.
> It remained in the deck,
> With no claim on the check,
> And no power to trouble the throne.

> **Consent By Visibility**  
> If the caveat technically gleams,
> The room may inherit its dreams.
> "You saw it," they say,
> As it melts into spray,
> Far downstream from decisions and teams.

> **Table Piece**  
> So if warnings are lovely and small,
> And they sit where they cannot forestall,
> That is Snowglobe's domain:
> Honest risk without rein,
> Left shining on top of the table.
