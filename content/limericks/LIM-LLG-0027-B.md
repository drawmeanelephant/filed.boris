**Case Number:** LLG-0027-B  
**Parent Entry:** LLG-0027-B  
**Description:** Ten hand-written limericks about a survey on procedural burden that became more burdensome than the burden it measured.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12  

***

> **Survey of Surveys**  
> They made a *new survey* to rate
> How annoying procedure had got of late.
> To submit your complaint,
> You needed a saint
> And more signatures still than the state.

> **Recursive Irony**  
> It asked, "*Does this process feel long?*"
> By making the answer itself fucking long.
> If irony paid,
> The Council had made
> A cathedral of knowing it's wrong.

> **The Signature Ritual**  
> The originator must *sign*.
> The reviewer must sign on the line.
> Then the office adjacent,
> Emotionally patient,
> Must sign that the burden felt fine.

> **Cascading Work**  
> Each stakeholder's *feelings* were weighed
> By a form that made new work cascade.
> By the time you were through,
> The survey proved true:
> Yes, procedure had deeply mislaid.

> **Red Tape Logic**  
> 27-B came to *show*
> How much red tape impeded the flow.
> It succeeded by force:
> Every answer, of course,
> Required red tape merely to know.

> **Redacted Sentiment**  
> One filer wrote, "*Jesus, enough,*"
> In the margin where comments got tough.
> The note was redacted
> As insufficiently tacted,
> But the sentiment stayed in the fluff.

> **Self-Licking Reforms**  
> To measure the *burden of forms*,
> They constructed more filing norms.
> This is how systems breed:
> Not by hunger or need,
> But by fucking self-licking reforms.

> **Graphed Grief**  
> A C.U.N.T.I.E.R. clerk, *dry and pale*,
> Said, "The findings are robust at scale."
> Translation, in brief:
> Your procedural grief
> Has been graphed with exemplary detail.

> **Sampling Zeal**  
> So if someone asks whether you feel
> The process is grinding your heel,
> Count the pens in the room.
> If they bloom into doom,
> You've been sampled by *bureaucratic zeal*.

> **The Five-Way Signature Bang**  
> To complain about too many forms,
> They birthed some new terrible norms.
> A five-way sign-off,
> With a managerial cough,
> To weather procedural storms.

> **Fucked by Feedback**  
> The feedback was meant to be pure,
> But acted a lot like manure.
> It buried the team,
> In a signature dream,
> With no possible hope for a cure.

> **The Over-Satisfaction Jerk**  
> They jerked off the survey for days,
> In a bureaucratic haze.
> "Are you satisfied now?"
> They asked the cash cow,
> While lighting the office ablaze.

> **Red Tape Bondage**  
> Tied up in red tape and pain,
> The survey was purely insane.
> The safe word was lost,
> At terrible cost,
> And the filing began all again.

> **Self-Licking Assholes**  
> A self-licking system of rot,
> A truly disgusting old plot.
> They multiplied forms,
> Like digital swarms,
> And asked if you liked it or not.
