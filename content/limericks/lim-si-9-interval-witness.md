**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the SI-9 Interval Witness and the silent stretches of unexplained time.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Silent Stretch**  
> A witness to intervals long,
> Where nothing goes right or goes wrong.
> The silence is logged,
> But the timeline is bogged
> With pauses where actions belong.

> **Unclassified Time**  
> It wasn't quite strain or success,
> Just an hour of quiet duress.
> The witness stood by
> With a standardized sigh,
> Recording an empty recess.

> **Referenced Later**  
> We look to the interval past,
> To see if the silence will last.
> But SI-9 just stares
> At the blankness it shares,
> And leaves the conclusion uncast.

> **The Empty Hour**  
> We stared at the screen for a bit,
> And did absolutely jack shit.
> The SI-9 watched,
> While the rollout was botched,
> And recorded the horrible fit.

> **Unaccountable Time**  
> The interval doesn’t exist,
> In the manager’s fictional list.
> But the Witness was there,
> With its terrible glare,
> While the critical updates were missed.

> **Dead Space**  
> The timeline is totally blank,
> And nobody knows who to thank.
> The Witness observes,
> As the silence un-nerves,
> And the terrible project just sank.

> **Log Sequence Validated**  
> The interval duration is clear,
> Though zero events will appear.
> The witness observed,
> The absence preserved,
> And routed the silence next year.

> **Existential Pause Detected**  
> A second of unassigned rest,
> Has failed the departmental test.
> The witness was called,
> The server was stalled,
> To audit the void we digest.

> **Silence Reallocated**  
> We filed all the quiet away,
> Under "Active Asserts for the Day".
> The witness stood by,
> To properly classify,
> The noise that the silence would play.
