**Case Number:** FREF-0560-ASAR  
**Parent Entry:** FREF-0560-ASAR  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about using fake visual markers and completion badges to hide unresolved burden.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12  

***

> **Confidence Object**  
> A badge with a confident glow
> Made uncertainty look like a no.
> It did not repair
> What was breaking in there,
> But it photographed nicely in row.

> **Seal of Enough**  
> They stamped it sufficiently done,
> Though the burden had nowhere to run.
> The seal did not heal;
> It made trouble congeal
> Into something reviewable, spun.

> **Artifact Handling**  
> The artifact sat on the page
> Like a prop from a better-run stage.
> If the process was thin,
> The reassurance came in
> To perform institutional age.

> **The Whisper Directive**  
> The filing cabinet breathes,
> A sigh that the basement receives.
> The papers inside,
> Have nowhere to hide,
> When midnight inspects what it leaves.

> **Green Marker Status**  
> The green marker indicates space,
> Where a resolution took place.
> If the problem is there,
> We have labeled the air,
> And applied the correct interface.

> **Continuity Check**  
> The assurance window remains,
> To process the continuity chains.
> Though the failure is clear,
> It is documented here,
> And the optical layer sustains.

> **Symbolic Completion**  
> A symbolic completion is neat,
> For rendering dashboards complete.
> It attaches a sign,
> To the end of the line,
> While the underlying conditions repeat.

> **Visual Certification**  
> The visual object is placed,
> And the unresolved burden erased.
> While the problem expands,
> In the relevant bands,
> The form is correctly encased.

> **The Substantive Sticker**  
> The form had a critical flaw,
> Which the audit committee foresaw.
> We attached a small green
> To the top of the screen,
> And declared it compliant with law.

> **Missing Icon Event**  
> A stamp from the ledger had dropped!
> The regional workflow was stopped!
> We deployed a response
> To re-issue the bronze,
> Before the whole enterprise popped.

> **Assuring the Plumbing**  
> The water supply is a mess,
> But the badge is displaying success.
> Since the pipe is now filed
> As administratively mild,
> The leaks are a thing we possess.
