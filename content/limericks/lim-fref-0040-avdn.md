> **PROCEDURAL_CLEAN**  
> The internal notes are reviewed,
> To ensure that the terms are not crude.
> The desk is aware,
> Of the grammar to bear,
> And exactly what should be pursued.

> **MISFILED_CONFIDENCE**  
> The notes on the carrots are bold,
> The words are increasingly cold.
> We file the root,
> In the digital boot,
> And wait for the harvest to fold.

> **ARCHIVAL_MYTH_DRIFT**  
> The notes from the desk of the past,
> Were written in letters of glass.
> The grammar was spelled,
> By the spirits we held,
> And meant to eternally last.

> **INSTITUTIONAL_LEAK**  
> The notes on the terms that we use,
> Are meant to prevent being sued.
> We speak in a drone,
> On the telephone,
> And eat very terrible food.

> **PROCEDURAL_SILENCE**  
> The internal note is set down,
> Without any smile or a frown.
> The desk is applied,
> With nowhere to hide,
> And nothing is happening round.
