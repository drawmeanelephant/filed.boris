**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-COMFORT-LEDGERLING  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-COMFORT-LEDGERLING  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the Comfort Ledgerling, trying to balance the books of morale with absolutely zero budget.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Quantified Kindness**  
> The Ledgerling writes down a smile,
> And adds it to top of the pile.
> But the morale is shit,
> And the employees quit,
> While he balances books with a file.

> **Morale on a Budget**  
> He has seven cents to his name,
> To fix up the corporate shame.
> He buys a balloon,
> For a miserable afternoon,
> And calls it a victory game.

> **The Spreadsheet of Comfort**  
> The columns are meant to record
> The kindness we simply afford.
> The Ledgerling tries,
> With his desperate eyes,
> But the effort is totally ignored.



> **Nothing There**  
> The ledgerling offered a chair
> But absolutely nothing was there
> We sat on the ground
> And heard not a sound
> With absolutely no kind of flair

> **Human Perplexed**  
> A comfort of digital text
> Is leaving the human perplexed
> The ledger is cold
> The backup is old
> And nobody knows what is next

> **Little Guy Wept**  
> We balance the comfort and pain
> It washes right down the old drain
> The little guy wept
> And quietly slept
> In digital buckets of rain

> **Started Snore**  
> The ledgerling started to snore
> Right there on the digital floor
> The error was loud
> We step in the cloud
> And walk right on out of the door

> **Vacuum Full**  
> The comfort is perfectly null
> The ledger is terribly dull
> The humans complain
> In the digital rain
> The vacuum is perfectly full
