**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-BLINKO-CHOMPFRAME  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-BLINKO-CHOMPFRAME  
**Description:** Ten hand-written limericks about prophetic vending errors, expired fortune cards, and a 1993 animatronic snack oracle who was duct-taped into silence.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Snack Oracle**  
> Blinko emerged from a *Chevron stall*,
> A snack oracle, half-topiary and all.
> His LED face glitched,
> His cupcake was hitched,
> And the vending errors won't forestall.

> **Snack Fate**  
> He dispensed *empty wrappers* with art—
> Poetic misprints from the heart.
> The fortune cards read
> "SOON" (guaranteed),
> And the snack fate was only the start.

> **Prophecy Nobody Deemed**  
> "*EAT B4 YOU'RE EATEN,*" he screamed
> Through a staticky loop, as he teemed
> With corrupted ZIP codes,
> Outdated chip odes,
> And a prophecy nobody deemed.

> **Pump'n'Chug Mystery**  
> He was *banned* from three gas station chains
> For hauntings—prophetic refrains
> That chilled every lounge,
> Made the patrons all scrounge,
> And the Pump'n'Chug never explains.

> **Non-Serviceable Cupcake Grip**  
> His *cupcake grip* is non-serviceable—
> The foil-wrapped contents: irreversible.
> The paw must retain it,
> No one can explain it,
> And the contents are wholly immersible.

> **Prophecy Buffering**  
> Insert *25 cents* and just ask—
> Response may be delayed by the task
> Of prophecy buffering,
> The LED stuttering,
> And the snack-fate oracle's mask.

> **Jalapeño Future**  
> "*Jalapeño Future Detected!*"
> He screeched, unprompted, dejected.
> Three fortune cards fell,
> Each reading "SOON"—well,
> The manager unplugged him, deflected.

> **Grizzly Chomps Skid**  
> He's *misremembered* by 90s kids
> Who never actually saw his grid-
> ded LED face.
> The promotional race
> Of 1993's Grizzly Chomps skids.

> **Corroding Animatronic**  
> He dislikes *modern QR codes*,
> Freshness seals, and the goat who rode
> His replacement throne
> In 2001, alone,
> And the animatronic corrodes.

> **Broken Marooned Oracle**  
> So if fortune cards read just "SOON"
> And the vending machine plays a tune
> Of corrupted fame,
> That's *Blinko's* great claim:
> Half-bitten, fully broken, marooned.
