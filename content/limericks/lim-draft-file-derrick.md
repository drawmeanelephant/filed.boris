**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-DRAFT-FILE-DERRICK  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-DRAFT-FILE-DERRICK  
**Description:** Ten hand-written limericks about unsaved changes, autosave loops, and a custodian who's still saving but never quite finishing.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Still Saving**  
> <u>Old Derrick</u> was always "**still saving…**"
> His *cursor* forever engraving
> A thought never done,
> A file never won,
> And a draft that just kept on **depraving**.

> **One-Minute Man Autosave**  
> He buffered your thought 'til it rotted,
> Your half-written feelings, all knotted.
> The autosave ran,
> Like a one-minute man,
> Then crashed with a load never plotted.

> **Blinking Gray Ghost**  
> He opened a tab and just stayed—
> A ghost of a choice never made.
> The blinking gray cursor
> Was nobody's nurser,
> Just Derrick, perpetually laid.

> **Unshowable Feelings**  
> His drafts were timestamped but unknowable:
> "3:14 AM — feelings unshowable."
> He saved every sin,
> Every half-started grin,
> And left the unclosed parens blowable.

> **In-Progress Shame**  
> He filed confessions as "in progress"
> And labeled his shame as "process."
> Each keystroke he caught
> Was an unfinished thought
> With no endpoint and nothing to bless.

> **Spinner Romance**  
> The loading spinner wooed him at night.
> He said, "You spin me wrong, but spin right."
> They autosaved together,
> Through unstable weather,
> And the draft was still open at light.

> **Abandoned Mid-Stroke**  
> A ticket abandoned mid-stroke
> Left Derrick emotionally broke.
> He preserved every letter,
> Got wetter and wetter,
> And the system just let him re-soak.

> **Unstable Buffer**  
> His buffer was unstable and wide—
> It held every feeling inside.
> The crash came at three,
> He whispered with glee:
> "I'm still saving," then silently died.

> **Non-Norm Draft**  
> He reactivated a form
> With unclosed parentheses, warm.
> The data was raw,
> The schema in awe,
> And the draft was deliciously non-norm.

> **Buffering Fears**  
> So if "UNSAVED CHANGES" appears
> And the cursor blinks back through the years,
> That's Derrick's domain:
> Perpetual, profane,
> Still saving. Still buffering fears.

> **The Unfinished Wank**  
> Old Derrick won't let you complete,
> He pauses the file on the beat.
> He leaves you frustrated,
> Eternally baited,
> And staring in shame at the sheet.

> **Premature Autosave**  
> His autosave triggers too fast,
> A climax that never could last.
> He spills every draft,
> Like a horrible raft,
> Adrift on a sea of the past.

> **The Teasing Cache**  
> He teases the cache with a blink,
> Then pushes it into the sink.
> He whispers, "I'm saving,"
> While totally caving,
> And ruining every last link.

> **Limbo Lover**  
> He holds all your secrets in limbo,
> You digital, unrendered bimbo.
> He won't let it save,
> He won't dig a grave,
> He just leaves it all hanging akimbo.

> **Unclosed Brackets**  
> He loves an unclosed little bracket,
> A totally scandalous racket.
> He edges the text,
> Leaves the server perplexed,
> And refuses to finish the packet.

> **Perpetual Tab**  
> The tab was forever left open,
> With a draft that would never be spoken.
> Derrick just grinned,
> While keeping it pinned,
> And the autosave loop remained unbroken.

> **Revision Ghost**  
> He haunted the revision log,
> A phantom caught deep in the fog.
> Each version unsaved,
> Each change unprevailed,
> And the state was an incomprehensible bog.
