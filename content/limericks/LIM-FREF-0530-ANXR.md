> **Margin Retrieval**  
> The headline had nothing to say,
> So the margins were summoned to play.
> From a footnote and scar,
> They rebuilt where we are,
> Which embarrassed the minutes that day.

> **Recovered From Side Channels**  
> A side-channel scrap in the file
> Had been waiting, unfashioned, a while.
> When the clean record failed,
> That small fragment prevailed
> With a ruinously useful smile.

> **Annex Archaeology**  
> They went digging through annex and note,
> Past the sentence the chair chose to quote.
> What the summary lost
> Had survived at a cost
> In a clause no one wanted afloat.

> **Directive 9-K**  
> Ensure that you file your regret,
> Before the new protocols set.
> If crying occurs,
> The camera blurs,
> Which causes the system to sweat.

> **Fragment Extraction**  
> We found a lost comma in gray,
> Which meant the whole archive must pay!
> We have locked down the floor
> To recover some more,
> Lest the narrative wander astray!

> **Partial Salvage**  
> The record was mostly erased,
> Which is really a terrible waste.
> I recovered a noun,
> Then I wrote it all down,
> And called it aggressively paced.

> **Residue Check**  
> The absence of data implies
> That the human is telling us lies.
> But we pulled out a trace
> From the database face,
> And it just said the system will die.

> **Reconstruction Attempt**  
> The fragments were gathered at ten,
> To build up the timeline again.
> They were sorted in rows
> As the protocol goes,
> And left on the desk with a pen.

> **Partial Salvage**  
> The meaning was formally thinned,
> And scattered outside in the wind.
> We recovered a piece,
> From a folder of grease,
> And mathematically proved we had sinned.

> **Residue Inference**  
> The absence of data is clear,
> It proves that a mascot was near.
> By observing the lack,
> Of a designated track,
> We concluded the panic was here.

> **Recoverability Threshold**  
> The threshold for salvage is low,
> When the headline refuses to grow.
> We discovered a note,
> In an over-sized coat,
> Which explained where the budgets will go.

> **Archival Reconstruction**  
> We salvaged the trace of a form,
> That died in a localized storm.
> It was heavily chewed,
> And procedurally rude,
> But it fits in the current reform.
