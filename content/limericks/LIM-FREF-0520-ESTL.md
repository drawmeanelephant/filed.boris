> **Borrowed Shine**  
> A weak little workflow got dressed
> In the clothes of a system more blessed.
> The mismatch was wide,
> But the mockup had pride,
> So the meeting pronounced it progressed.

> **Prestige Overlay**  
> They imported a high-status face,
> Then applied it to low-status space.
> It confused what was true,
> But the screenshots looked new,
> Which is often accepted as grace.

> **Category Drift**  
> The surface survived every test,
> Though the function was plainly half-dressed.
> When the users said, "Wait,"
> The review called it state-
> Of-the-art compensatory best.

> **Update Process**  
> To finalize form number nine,
> Proceed to the end of the line.
> You hand in the card,
> The scanning is hard,
> But processing operates fine.

> **Review Efficiency**  
> The logic was heavily flawed,
> But the interface made us applaud.
> It was rapidly passed
> Since the renders were fast,
> And the underlying code was ignored.

> **Painting the Oven**  
> The oven is painted in blue,
> To look like a calendar, too.
> When the baking is hot,
> We pretend that it's not,
> And schedule a meeting for two.

> **The First Mockup**  
> The mockup was carved out of stone,
> By founders who wanted a throne.
> Now we worship the glyph,
> On the edge of the cliff,
> While the servers repeatedly groan.

> **Prestige Catastrophe**  
> A borrowed aesthetic has failed,
> And the structural pillars have paled.
> The font was too tall,
> For the designated wall,
> And the national guard was detailed.

> **Legibility Crisis**  
> The interface button was round,
> Which compromised hallowed ground.
> The review was a shock,
> To the whole city block,
> And the relevant sirens will sound.

> **Entropy Debt Assessment**  
> The surface legitimacy died,
> When the formatting wasn't applied.
> The aesthetic decay,
> Must be neutralized today,
> Or the council will formally hide.

> **Category Drift Alert**  
> The category drifted an inch,
> Which caused the directors to flinch.
> The discrepancy calls,
> For a lockdown of halls,
> And the tightening up of the winch.
