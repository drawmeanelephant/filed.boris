**Case Number:** LIM-LLG-0399-OCS  
**Parent Entry:** LLG-0399-OCS  
**Description:** The Desk softly corrects a shouted defect, recasting the failure as an emergent effect.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Failure, Recast**  
> A finding once shouted, **defect**.
> The Desk said, Let's softly correct.
> Call it emergent instead,
> Let the red bars play dead,
> And the chart will no longer object.

> **Durable Aspect**  
> The pattern came back every round.
> Nonconformity, by any sound.
> Yet the wording we chose
> Let its sharp edges doze
> As a durable aspect we found.

> **Deferred Assurance**  
> A hole in the process looked stark.
> The summary muttered, A mark.
> In the margin we wrote
> A more soothing note
> Deferred assurance, not dark.

> **Curated Absence**  
> A missing stack left a clean space.
> The scan called it loss, out of place.
> Optics whispered, Not so.
> Curated absence can glow
> As a sign of intentional grace.

> **Independent Unit**  
> An orphaned record sat alone.
> No owner picked up the phone.
> The report, needing charm,
> Said, It does us no harm
> As a low-touch unit, full-grown.

> **Legacy-Stable**  
> An artifact long past its prime
> Still governed three quarters of time.
> We wrote, Not out-of-date,
> But legacy-stable state
> Where tenure itself is a sign.

> **Learning Period**  
> Incidents climbed by the day.
> The metrics looked ready to fray.
> So the note on the slide
> Let the numbers decide
> That we entered a learning display.

> **Continuity First**  
> The wall had a crack, slightly wide.
> The summary smiled from its side.
> While material slipped,
> Continuity gripped,
> And the wording called both things a stride.

> **Language as Control**  
> A crater sat plain in the hall.
> The audit wrote over the wall.
> Once the phrasing was neat,
> There was nothing to treat.
> Words had become the repair, is all.

> **Purring Report**  
> So if your next audit reads smooth,
> With sentences softened for soothe,
> Check the desk by the door
> Where we polish the score.
> If it purrs, someone sanded the tooth.
