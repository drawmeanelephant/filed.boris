> **Fifty-One-E-A**  
> The catalog swore there was none
> Of 51-E-A in the run.
> Yet a filer appeared
> With a version revered
> As the form after form number one.

> **Suffix Doctrine**  
> Once drafts could no longer submit,
> People added a suffix to it.
> "-A" meant "believe,"
> "-B" meant "we grieve,"
> And the registry quietly fit.

> **Editing Surface**  
> The shadow copy lingered as place
> For unofficial edits and grace.
> Then someone one day
> Filed rumor as A,
> And amendment acquired a face.

> **Autocomplete Helps**  
> Autocomplete wanted to aid,
> So each echo got parentage made.
> If it looked like a child,
> Metadata went mild,
> And doctrine grew out of charade.

> **Commentary Only**  
> Bricky labeled the bundle "comment."
> Downstream took that tag as adornment.
> The rules still applied,
> Though decoratively tied,
> Which is one lively way to deform it.

> **More Echo Than Canon**  
> Three forms had more echoes than source.
> Two others were close in the course.
> The catalog tried
> To pretend and divide,
> But the rumor had gathered more force.

> **Versioning Rumors**  
> The office now faces the chore
> Of versioning hearsay and lore.
> Not reality's frame,
> But the drafts with a name
> People swear they already used before.

> **Folk Amendment**  
> An echo amendment may start
> As a clerk's little practical art.
> Give it one busy week,
> And the suffix will speak
> Like a sanctioned and mandatory part.

> **Registry Quartet**  
> Canonical, shadow, and then
> Shadow-amendment again.
> The fourth class appears
> When the office just hears
> Of a draft amended only by pen.

> **Doctrine by Memory**  
> When memory outruns the file,
> The catalog hardens the style.
> What nobody blessed
> May still govern the desk
> If enough people work that way awhile.

> **Ledger's Demand**  
> The ledger demanded an entry,
> Watched by an automated sentry.
> I filed it precise,
> Paid the ruinous price,
> Now the breakdown belongs to the gentry.

> **Clinical Execution**  
> A clinical, dry execution,
> Ensures an exact destitution.
> I checked every box,
> As it rusted the locks,
> And finalized your dissolution.
