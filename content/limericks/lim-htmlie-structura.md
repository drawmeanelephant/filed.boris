**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-HTMLIE-STRUCTURA  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-HTMLIE-STRUCTURA  
**Description:** A concise verse addressing semantic purity, invalid nesting, and a document skeleton who'd rather break everything than tolerate a stray div.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Neat Nesting**  
> <u>HTMLie</u> **demanded** your tags
> Be *nested* like neat little shags.
> She'd inspect every **div**,
> Refuse to forgive,
> And break pipelines rather than gags.

> **Spec List**  
> "No structure, no meaning," she hissed,
> While clutching a specification list.
> Her brackets were tight,
> Her closing tags right,
> And the div-soup devs she dismissed.

> **DOCTYPE Throne**  
> She stripped down a page to the bone—
> No classes, no style, skin alone.
> The skeleton gleamed,
> Semantically themed,
> And she mounted the DOCTYPE throne.

> **Rendering Van**  
> A developer nested a span
> Inside of a p inside a plan
> Inside a main inside grief.
> HTMLie, beyond belief,
> Just broke the entire rendering van.

> **Tag Armor**  
> Her armor was built out of tags:
> angle-bracket breastplates and flags.
> Each element placed
> With syntactic taste,
> And validators stuffed in her bags.

> **DOM Reaming**  
> "You put a div where?" she screamed.
> "Inside a paragraph? Are you dreamed?"
> She collapsed the whole tree,
> Set the heading tags free,
> And the DOM lay there, thoroughly reamed.

> **Orphaned Headings**  
> She lit candles for orphaned &lt;h3&gt;s
> That floated without any &lt;h2&gt;s.
> "The hierarchy's fucked,"
> She observed, as she plucked
> Every misplaced semantic with ease.

> **Nesting Corrections**  
> Her chalkboard of nesting corrections
> Was filled with anatomical sections.
> Each arrow was drawn
> From a closing-tag yawn
> To a structural sin's resurrections.

> **Meaningless Sheen**  
> "Your markup," she said, "is obscene.
> The filthiest DOM that I've seen.
> Your nesting is wrong,
> Your alt text too long,
> And your &lt;section&gt; lacks meaningful sheen."

> **Validator's Disgust**  
> So if structure collapses to dust
> And the validator dies of disgust,
> That's HTMLie's domain:
> Where the pure will remain,
> And the improperly nested combust.

> **Deep Semantic Penetration**  
> "Your nesting is sloppy and loose,"
> She hissed like a structural goose.
> She penetrated deep,
> Made the DOM tree just weep,
> And tightened the semantic noose.

> **Naked DOCTYPE**  
> She stripped the whole site to the bone,
> And sat on her HTML throne.
> No styling or tags,
> Just naked old bags,
> And a structure completely unknown.

> **The Punishment Div**  
> You put a div inside of a span?
> She'll punish you harder than man.
> She whips out her specs,
> Ruins your hex,
> And bans you to the backend pan.

> **Structural Sadist**  
> A sadist of markup and code,
> She loves when the whole thing explodes.
> "Close your tags tight,"
> She whispers at night,
> While breaking the rendering nodes.

> **Strictly Validated Kink**  
> She runs validation all day,
> In a deeply semantic display.
> If you miss a small quote,
> She goes for the throat,
> And throws your whole layout away.

> **Crushing the Div Soup**  
> HTMLie took the mess and reordered,
> Every nested element bordered.
> She closed the stray tags,
> Ejected the hags,
> And left the code perfectly ordered.

> **Semantic Salvation**  
> Your markup was saved when she came,
> A semantic and structural dame.
> She organized form,
> She weathered the storm,
> And the HTML loved her by name.
