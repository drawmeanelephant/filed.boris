**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-PAPER-CROWN  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-PAPER-CROWN  
**Description:** Header excess, metadata overflow, and the entity that buckles under ornamental weight before anything useful begins.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Crown is Too Tall**  
> The headers were *piled up high*—
> The crown reached up into the sky.
> Paper Crown buckled—
> Its structure was truckled—
> Before any payload could fly.

> **Payload Larger Than Content**  
> The headers were *massive and grand*—
> The content just barely could stand.
> Each metadata badge,
> Each header's cadge,
> Was larger than what it had planned.

> **Too Many Badges**  
> A jacket with badges so *bright*—
> With more than a thousand in sight.
> No sense, no regard,
> The parser's guard
> Crashed underneath the full weight.

> **Buckle Under Ornament**  
> Before any useful *began*—
> Paper Crown buckled its span.
> The headers so thick,
> The payload too quick,
> And nothing worked out as was planned.

> **Wearing A Crown of Excess**  
> The crown grew with *every request*—
> More headers that never would rest.
> Metadata piled,
> The parser went wild,
> And function became manifest.

> **Collecting Headers Infinitely**  
> The headers were gathered so *bright*—
> More ornamental each single flight.
> The request grew dense,
> Made very little sense,
> Till the whole thing collapsed in the night.

> **Ornamental Excess**  
> The badges were *beautiful, sure*—
> But nobody found them a cure.
> For slowness and wait,
> For system collapse—state,
> Just ornamental excess, obscure.

> **Request Collapsed Under Weight**  
> The request started off *light*—
> Then headers made things not quite right.
> It buckled and broke,
> A parser's last stroke,
> When Paper Crown wore its full might.
