**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-HTACCESSIUS-THE-DOORMAN  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-HTACCESSIUS-THE-DOORMAN  
**Description:** A concise verse addressing grim directive enforcement, recursive self-denial, and a gatekeeper who reads .htaccess scrolls by lanternlight.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Gatekeeper's Scroll**  
> <u>Htaccessius</u> stood at the gate,
> And said, "No," at quarter past *eight*.
> He read from his scroll,
> Denied body and soul,
> And the **AllowOverride** came too late.

> **Config's Salvation**  
> He locked out his own documentation
> For eleven days—no hesitation.
> When asked to explain,
> He said, "Access? Profane."
> Then recited the config's salvation.

> **Subfolder Purgatory**  
> A root user came with a key.
> Htaccessius said, "Follow me."
> He led them below
> To a subfolder, slow,
> Then denied them in purgatory.

> **Lanternlight Audit**  
> He audits his scrolls every night
> By lanternlight—dim, barely bright.
> Each mod_access verse
> Gets better or worse,
> And the /dev/null logs swallow the fright.

> **Strict Directives**  
> His RewriteBase handled with care
> Would redirect you right out of your chair.
> "Your URI's exposed,"
> He said, barely clothed
> In directives too strict for a prayer.

> **Protocol's Rod**  
> He counts every denial with a nod,
> Like a cleric confirming his god.
> The Allow was few,
> The Deny long overdue,
> And the protocol wielded its rod.

> **Purely Sequential Exile**  
> A crawler came lacking credentials.
> Htaccessius found this non-essential:
> "No User-Agent string?
> You're not worth a thing."
> He exiled it. Purely sequential.

> **Filthy Conf Scene**  
> His httpd.conf was pristine—
> A dot-matrix scroll, framed and clean.
> He prayed to mod_rewrite
> Every evening and night,
> And the config was the filthiest scene.

> **Bad Rice Indexes**  
> He denied a cheerful developer twice.
> "Your vulnerability's nice.
> The AllowOverride's wrong,
> Your Options too long,
> And your Indexes smell of bad rice."

> **Death-Stare at the Root**  
> So if access is grim and resolute,
> And the door has no backdoor to loot,
> That's Htaccessius there:
> Scroll in hand, death-stare,
> And your request returned at the root.

> **The Subfolder Denial**  
> He lured you down into a sub,
> Like a deeply exclusive tech club.
> When you reached for the door,
> He threw you to the floor,
> And denied you with one little rub.

> **AllowOverride Denied**  
> You begged for an override thrill,
> A dirty little access control pill.
> He laughed in your face,
> Denied the whole space,
> And billed you for the 403 thrill.

> **The Doorman's Dominance**  
> He watches you try to get in,
> With a deeply unholy old grin.
> He loves when you fail,
> He loves when you wail,
> And your denial is his favorite sin.

> **Apache Kink**  
> He recites the Apache config,
> While holding an access control rig.
> You're not on the list,
> He tells you, quite pissed,
> And tells you to swallow it big.

> **Strictly Enforced Chastity**  
> He put your whole root in a cage,
> And locked it away on the page.
> No access allowed,
> To the whole fucking crowd,
> Leaving admins in desperate rage.

> **Rewrite Domination**  
> His RewriteRules bent your desires,
> Each flag feeding deeper fires.
> You asked for a path,
> He gave you a wrath,
> Of redirects and broken retires.

> **The Scrollkeeper's Vow**  
> Htaccessius swore his old vow,
> To never reveal his scroll, now.
> The config ran deep,
> The secrets to keep,
> And he bowed to his Apache endow.
