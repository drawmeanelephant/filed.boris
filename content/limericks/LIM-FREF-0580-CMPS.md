**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about care-coded language, soft prompts, and emotional acknowledgment without material change.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12

> **Soft Prompt**  
> The prompt had a comforting tone,
> Like a hand placed beside a cold phone.
> But the button below
> Still delivered a no,
> Only warmer and better well-known.

> **Care-Coded UI**  
> The interface called itself kind,
> With a pastel and sentence aligned.
> It acknowledged the ache,
> Then declined any stake,
> Leaving courtesy neatly behind.

> **Surface Without Shift**  
> A surface can soften the room
> While preserving the original doom.
> That is Empathegy's art:
> Let the language have heart,
> While the mechanism keeps its costume.

> **Storage Receipt**  
> The box has been sealed with a string,
> It isn't a difficult thing.
> The label is flat,
> It sits on the mat,
> And waits for the end of the spring.

> **Caring Rejection**  
> The button was terribly sweet,
> While logging the human's defeat.
> It offered a hug,
> Then pulled on the rug,
> A structural triumph complete.

> **Soft Prompt Output**  
> The screen showed a pastel-hued box,
> Confirming the state of the locks.
> A sentence of care
> Was positioned right there,
> Before the credential un-docks.

> **The Empathegy Spell**  
> The UI now whispers of grief,
> A folklore of gentle relief.
> The dashboard pretends
> That it acts like your friends,
> A sacred and hollow belief.

> **Empathy Module**  
> The phrasing acknowledged the pain,
> While keeping the policy plain.
> The system is soft,
> With its feelings aloft,
> While holding the procedural chain.

> **The Legend of Care**  
> The button of caring was pressed,
> And the ancient machine was impressed.
> The prompt gave a smile,
> That lasted a while,
> And vanished along with the rest.

> **Temperature Control**  
> The thermostat offered a hug,
> And printed a note on the rug.
> It noticed the chill,
> And handled the bill,
> By warmly destroying the plug.

> **Acknowledgment Sent**  
> The surface acknowledged the strain,
> With language to mitigate pain.
> No action occurred,
> No process was stirred,
> But the phrasing was perfectly plain.

> **Window 4**  
> The window presented a text,
> Explaining what needed done next.
> We clicked on the square,
> With nothing to share,
> And closed the surrounding context.
