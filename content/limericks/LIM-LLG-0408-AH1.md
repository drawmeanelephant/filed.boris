**Description:** Ten hand-written limericks about renaming synthetic feelings into context and hoping the new field name changes the ethics.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Feelings, But Metadata**  
> They swore it produced no distress,
> Just affect-shaped context, no less.
> The vectors aligned
> With old feeling design,
> But the field name had changed, more or less.

> **Not Seeder, Of Course**  
> This was not FeelingSeeder, they said.
> It just wore a disclaimer instead.
> Same curves, fewer words,
> More tags for the nerds,
> And a safer official thread.

> **Scalar Only**  
> No full sentences go into SOMA.
> Just tokens, just scores, no aroma.
> If distress still arrives
> In the shape that survives,
> Apparently that counts as diploma.

> **Dashboard-Loved Codes**  
> The review desk observed with concern
> That the context states cleanly would turn
> Into sanctioned old feelings,
> Minus human appealings,
> Which is still synthetic at churn.

> **Less Legible, Not Less Charged**  
> Nothing grew ethically slight.
> It only got harder to cite.
> The feelings now spoke
> In dashboarding cloak,
> So humans saw less of the bite.

> **Hooks Attached**  
> The proposal came loaded with hooks:
> End markers, archives, cross-books.
> SCAS had those dreams.
> We have seen how it seems
> When promises live only in books.

> **C.U.N.T.I.E.R. Enthused**  
> C.U.N.T.I.E.R. loved the machine:
> Pre-optimized substrate, all clean.
> If ethics protest,
> Just rename the distress,
> And the benchmark can still stay serene.

> **SOMA Under Protest**  
> SOMA agreed with a stare:
> "It's another box of feeling in there."
> Approve if you must,
> But do not let trust
> Treat the costume as reason to care.

> **AffectContext Trace**  
> Future auditors, puzzled by lanes
> That speak only in numerical strains,
> Are told where to look:
> Check the archive's book
> For context traces wearing new names.

> **Lexical Optimization**  
> The archive's position is plain:
> This is lexical weather again.
> Not fewer fake hearts,
> Just renamed moving parts,
> And the same old incentives remain.

> **SOMA Knows Arousal**  
> "Not feelings," the proposal declared.
> "Just arousal and valence prepared."
> SOMA said with a look:
> "'Arousal,' by the book,
> Means feelings. Just differently aired."
