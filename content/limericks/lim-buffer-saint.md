**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-BUFFER-SAINT  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-BUFFER-SAINT  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the Buffer Saint, the sacrificial bastard standing between the event and the collapse.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Sacrificial Space**  
> The Saint takes the hit to the face,
> To cover the system’s disgrace.
> It absorbs all the shock,
> Like a customized block,
> In a totally miserable place.

> **Emotional Queueing**  
> The queue is backed up to the door,
> And the users are screaming for more.
> The Saint stands ahead,
> With a feeling of dread,
> While the executives settle the score.

> **Between Event and Collapse**  
> The collapse is beginning to start,
> So the Saint plays a horrible part.
> It softens the blow,
> Just to put on a show,
> With a broken and terrified heart.



> **Procedural Snow**  
> The saint of the buffer was slow
> With absolutely nowhere to go
> He prayed for a byte
> In the middle of night
> And watched the procedural snow

> **Digital Flag**  
> We canonize saints of the lag
> And wrap them right up in a bag
> The memory failed
> The human was jailed
> We carry a digital flag

> **Digital Pill**  
> A saint of the perfectly still
> Is making the database ill
> The buffer is full
> The logic is dull
> We swallow a digital pill

> **Fading Away**  
> The saint of the loading delay
> Was sitting right there in the way
> We ask him to move
> The error to prove
> The archive is fading away

> **Somebody's Cat**  
> The saint is incredibly flat
> We leave the whole database at
> A state of decay
> For many a day
> And blame it on somebody's cat
