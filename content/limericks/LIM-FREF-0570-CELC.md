**Case Number:** FREF-0570-CELC  
**Parent Entry:** FREF-0570-CELC  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the stages through which care enters, survives, changes, and exits institutional visibility.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12  

***

> **Intake to Exit**  
> A care event entered as need,
> Then was normalized, routed, and keyed.
> By the time it was closed,
> What the person proposed
> Had been trimmed to a countable seed.

> **Visibility Window**  
> It was visible only in phase,
> Then it slipped from the reportable gaze.
> The care did not end;
> It just failed to extend
> Through the archive's preferred little maze.

> **Lifecycle Complete**  
> The lifecycle marked itself clean,
> With each state in its proper machine.
> But the human request
> Was still out past the crest,
> Where completion is rarely seen.

> **Routing The Care**  
> A care event entered the queue,
> And triggered an endless review.
> The form lacked a date,
> It's a threat to the state!
> Deploy the contingency crew!

> **Uncountable Tears**  
> The human began to express,
> A feeling of profound distress.
> We routed the tear
> To a database tier,
> And logged it as partial success.

> **Standard Intake**  
> The care event hit the first phase,
> And sat in the bucket for days.
> We marked it complete,
> To stay on our feet,
> And ignored the procedural haze.

> **Invisible Exit**  
> The care event exited sight,
> And filed exactly right.
> The ledger was closed,
> As the manual proposed,
> And banished into the night.

> **Lifecycle Breach**  
> The care was recorded too late,
> A failure to properly date!
> We halted the town,
> And powered it down,
> To audit the feelings of fate.

> **Stage 6 Skipped**  
> The routing was done for the day,
> So we threw the assessment away.
> The metric is fine,
> It crossed the red line,
> There isn't much more I can say.

> **Laundered Care**  
> The care was successfully logged,
> While the actual progress was bogged.
> We counted the score,
> And locked up the door,
> While the humans were quietly flogged.

> **Stage 2 Recognition**  
> The event was correctly defined,
> And placed in the system behind.
> No action was seen,
> By the routing machine,
> But the paperwork surely aligned.

> **Breach of Margin Protocol**  
> The margin was over by two,
> A metric we cannot undo.
> The system will fail,
> The data turns pale,
> And causes a global review.
