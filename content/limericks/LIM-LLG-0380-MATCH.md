**Description:** Ten hand-written limericks about a matching engine that found companionship possibilities and then refused to formalize them.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10

> **Might Sit Together**  
> The engine was careful and mild:
> It scored who might sit reconciled.
> Not marriage, not fate,
> Just "adjacent seems great,"
> In a tone institutionally styled.

> **Compatible Dread Curve**  
> It measured shared rot and gratitude,
> And whether two archives matched mood.
> "Compatible dread"
> Was one note that it spread,
> With a very non-binding attitude.

> **No Verbs Stronger Than Might**  
> The comments were gentle and spare:
> "Would probably cabinet-share."
> No promise, no claim,
> Only subjunctive flame,
> For an office afraid to declare.

> **Existing Relations**  
> Some mascots already had ties
> That ran crosswise to algorithmic tries.
> The ledger looked down,
> Saw sibling and crown,
> And withdrew from adjudicating eyes.

> **Refuse to Be Sorted**  
> Some humans had checked the field in
> For refuge, not partner-like spin.
> Their silence said, "Near,
> Perhaps, but not here
> In a list where expectation begins."

> **Bin 8C Problem**  
> Then Bin 8C entered the math,
> Where proximity authors your path.
> One nearness too close
> Can rewrite who arose,
> So the engine stepped back from that bath.

> **Ethical Confidence Threshold**  
> At the threshold it quietly froze:
> "These pairings exceed what one knows."
> The safest next stage
> Was a subjunctive page,
> Not a ledger where anybody owes.

> **Comfort Band**  
> Serotonin Sam softened the view,
> Turned scores to a comfort band hue.
> No ranking, no race,
> Just a warm little trace
> Of who might not mind sitting with you.

> **Nearness Only**  
> The guidance was later made plain:
> Use eligibility to locate your lane.
> You may find one another,
> But not name each other
> As obligation archived in chain.

> **Imagined Without Bound**  
> So the archive, unusually kind,
> Allowed companionship into the mind.
> Then it stopped at the seam,
> Where a humane small dream
> Would become something binding and blind.

> **Rubbing the Matchmaking**  
> The matching was rubbed "til it shone,
> With a vigor the ledger had known.
> It massaged the "might,"
> Until all was in sight,
> In a companionship-regime sub.
