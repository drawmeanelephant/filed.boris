> **The Padded Waiting Room**  
> The Spacer gives comfort and tea,
> While waiting to set you all free.
> But the budget is spent,
> And the process is bent,
> So you’re stuck here until ninety-three.

> **Furnished Delay**  
> It smiles and it fluffs up your chair,
> To hide that nobody is there.
> The delay is immense,
> At your fucking expense,
> And nobody actually will care.

> **Benevolent Stall**  
> They promise the help is en route,
> While offering water and fruit.
> The Spacer is kind,
> To your paralyzed mind,
> While the company goes up the chute.



> **Traveled Mile**  
> A spacer of absolute care
> Was floating right up in the air
> It gave us a smile
> And traveled a mile
> With absolutely no kind of flair

> **Terrible Bash**  
> The benevolence caused us to crash
> And turned all the data to ash
> The spacer was wide
> We wanted to hide
> And gave it a terrible bash

> **Terrified Face**  
> We padded the server with grace
> And ruined the digital space
> The error is loud
> We step in the cloud
> And cover our terrified face

> **Nowhere Slide**  
> A kindly attempt to divide
> The data that wanted to hide
> The spacer is dead
> The manual read
> The logic has nowhere to slide

> **Somebody's Cat**  
> The kindness completely fell flat
> We leave the whole database at
> A state of decay
> For many a day
> And blame it on somebody's cat
