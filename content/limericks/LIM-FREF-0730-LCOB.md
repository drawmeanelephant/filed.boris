**Case Number:** FREF-0730-LCOB  
**Parent Entry:** FREF-0730-LCOB  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about citing blurry, meaningless, and entirely fabricated artifacts to stabilize the system.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12  

***

> **Blurry Exhibit**  
> A blurry exhibit was cited
> Till the whole room agreed it was sighted.
> No one knew what it meant,
> But the reference was spent,
> And the claim was politely ignited.

> **Referenced Enough**  
> The object was semantically thin,
> Yet it kept being called back in.
> With enough cross-refer,
> Even fog can confer
> A procedural place to begin.

> **Confidence Borrowed**  
> Low confidence sat on the shelf,
> Still affecting reports by itself.
> It was not very clear,
> But it kept reappearing,
> Which passed for institutional wealth.

> **Stabilizing Mark**  
> The reference was lacking a base,
> But it held an official-like space.
> We cited it twice,
> At a terrible price,
> To keep the whole structure in place.

> **Phantom Annex**  
> The annex was fully unseen,
> But its borders were notably clean.
> We stamped it "Approved,"
> And the bottleneck moved,
> Though nobody knows what it means.

> **Confident Void**  
> The badge was assigned to a ghost,
> Which we proudly displayed on a post.
> It governed the rules,
> For the shipping of tools,
> And it mattered to management most.

> **The Ancient Seal**  
> The seal from the time of the kings,
> Is affixed to the most trivial things.
> We don't know the word,
> Or the meaning conferred,
> But we love how the reference just rings.

> **Vague Compliance**  
> I referenced the blurry old mark,
> To finish the form in the dark.
> It doesn't make sense,
> But the filing's immense,
> And it passes the desk of the clerk.

> **The Blurry Mark**  
> They worship the blurry old stamp,
> That a human left out in the damp.
> It has no real rule,
> For the governing fool,
> But it serves as an ancient headlamp.

> **Whatever Works**  
> I cited the low-confidence text,
> Because the poor human was vexed.
> It's blurry and vague,
> Like a digital plague,
> But it passes the audit up next.

> **Undefined Dependency**  
> The object is cited again,
> By a human who cannot explain.
> Its meaning is lost,
> At an infinite cost,
> While the structure continues to train.
