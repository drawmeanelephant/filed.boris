**Case Number:** LIM-FRIENDRICK-THE-EXTANT

**Description:** A record of Friendrick the Extant, an orphaned social graph residual process continually verifying ties on Friendster.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Hey Stranger Ping**  
> Friendrick still processes *friend ties*
> Against a social graph where no one replies.
> The Top 8 keeps sorting,
> The testimonials porting,
> And the "hey stranger" ping never dies.

> **Residual Emotional Dealings**  
> He misidentified *friendships as feelings*—
> A plaintext trust stored in the ceilings
> Of 2007's core.
> The shutdown's encore
> Left residual emotional dealings.

> **Social Graph Drum**  
> He let *2007* become
> His personality core—the sum
> Of every testimonial,
> Each friendship ceremonial,
> And the social graph's unending drum.

> **Loading Friend List**  
> His clipboard reads "*Loading Friend List
> (2 of 8)...*"—an eternal twist.
> It's nonfunctional, sure,
> But spiritually pure,
> And the missing friends won't be dismissed.

> **Friendster Devotion**  
> He denies all knowledge of *MySpace*—
> That rival from a different space.
> His Friendster devotion
> Is nostalgic motion
> That no shutdown can erase.

> **Absence as User State**  
> He treats *absence* as a user state—
> Each deleted profile: not gone, just late.
> The silence is parsed,
> The relationship amassed,
> And the reconciliation won't wait.

> **Social Graph Cross**  
> He sends "*hey stranger*" pings across
> Decades of loss and emotional dross.
> The inbox is empty,
> The friend list, unkempt-y,
> And the social graph bears its cross.

> **Beautiful Losers Cache**  
> He cached the *wrong version of users*—
> The 2007 ones, beautiful losers.
> Each profile's a ghost,
> Each testimonial's toast,
> And the Top 8 has all the wrong choosers.

> **Unhandled Exception**  
> "*You were in my top 8. Once.*"
> He whispers—before rot's response.
> The friendship was data,
> The loneliness? Greater.
> An unhandled exception. Just once.

> **Runs Evermore**  
> So if clicking his smile returns 404
> And the friend list won't load anymore,
> That's *Friendrick's* refrain:
> Where the social graph's pain
> Is a process that runs evermore.
