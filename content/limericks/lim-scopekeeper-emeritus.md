**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-SCOPEKEEPER-EMERITUS  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-SCOPEKEEPER-EMERITUS  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about Scopekeeper Emeritus, moving the goalposts so the auditor never gets mad.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Moving the Goalposts**  
> The project expanded too far,
> So the Keeper adjusted the bar.
> He narrowed the line,
> So the audit looked fine,
> And left the reality ajar.

> **Preserving Legibility**  
> The edges were messy and gross,
> And the system was comatose.
> The Scopekeeper shears
> All the terrible fears,
> And gives the reviewer a dose.

> **Sincere Extensions**  
> He widens the circle of trust,
> To cover the corporate rust.
> The scope is a lie,
> In the bureaucratic sky,
> But the changes are absolutely just.



> **Lost Hope**  
> The clerk who was guarding the scope
> Gave up on the last bit of hope
> He printed a file
> That sat for a while
> And slowly dissolved like a soap

> **Torn Manual**  
> A system that could not persist
> Demanded the scope to exist
> But the manual tore
> And fell to the floor
> Now nothing remains on the list

> **Vector of Dread**  
> The scope is a vector of dread
> The index is practically dead
> We log every byte
> And turn off the light
> To sleep in the server instead

> **Flat and Destroyed**  
> The emeritus typed in the void
> The data was fully destroyed
> The flat file is flat
> We leave it at that
> And keep the old errors deployed

> **Static Song**  
> A metric was totally wrong
> We sang a procedural song
> We closed out the task
> And answered no ask
> The silence is static and long
