> **Stage Continuity**  
> The set showed a process intact,
> Though the backstage had quietly cracked.
> Every cue hit its mark,
> Every hallway stayed dark,
> And the audience certified fact.

> **Represented Calm**  
> Continuity gleamed on the screen,
> Much stronger than life in between.
> If the actors looked steady,
> The report called them ready,
> And declined to inspect the machine.

> **Curtain Procedure**  
> When the lights made the wreckage look small,
> They applauded the shape of the hall.
> The performance was true
> To the script under review,
> Which was never the work after all.

> **Categorical Health**  
> The error was filed under calm,
> And treated with aggregate balm.
> Though systems had died,
> The index relied,
> On a totally incorrect psalm.

> **Verified Calm**  
> The representation was stored,
> As per the requests of the board.
> The lived situation,
> Required no citation,
> And thus was securely ignored.

> **Minimum Representation**  
> The dashboard is currently green,
> There's nothing that needs to be seen.
> We printed the chart,
> We've done our full part,
> Don't ask what the variables mean.

> **Zero Incident Baseline**  
> The lack of complaints was accrued,
> And thus as resilience reviewed.
> The user's restraint,
> In filing complaint,
> Confirmed that the metrics conclude.

> **Severity Amendment**  
> The outage was flagged as severe,
> But fixing it wasn't my tier.
> We called it "a pause,"
> Without a root cause,
> And now the entire queue's clear.

> **Thank You Metric**  
> They thanked the machine for the form,
> Which we took as the stabilized norm.
> Their tears on the floor,
> Were logged at the door,
> To keep the parameters warm.

> **Acknowledgment Parity**  
> The receipt of the ticket is care,
> Stored safely beneath the high chair.
> We printed the mail,
> So health cannot fail,
> And filed it as breathable air.

> **Curve Smoothing Protocol**  
> A pixel was rendering red,
> So management panicked and fled.
> To handle the threat,
> We cleared all the debt,
> And issued a warning instead.

> **The Sacred Dashboard**  
> The dashboard has always been green,
> A color no human has seen.
> It shines in the dark,
> A holy, cold spark,
> That absolves the collapsing machine.

> **Audit Comfort Level**  
> The gap was required to pass,
> The auditor's standardized glass.
> We entered the score,
> And locked up the door,
> And filed the result in the mass.

> **Scheduled Empathy**  
> The circle convened at eleven,
> To prove that the workplace is heaven.
> We witnessed the grief,
> With zero relief,
> And scheduled the next one for seven.
