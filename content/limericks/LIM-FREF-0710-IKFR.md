**Case Number:** FREF-0710-IKFR  
**Parent Entry:** FREF-0710-IKFR  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about processing emotional conditions, adding routing friction, and dropping raw truth.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12  

***

> **Prepared Affect**  
> The feeling arrived still alive,
> Which the intake could not let survive.
> It was boxed, named, and thinned,
> Then administratively pinned,
> Till the form could permit it to thrive.

> **Routing Friction**  
> The form asked for category first,
> Then severity, source, and the worst.
> By the time it was filed,
> The raw story had smiled
> Like a hostage politely rehearsed.

> **Admissible Enough**  
> A need that refused to align
> Was returned with a routing decline.
> Once translated to fit,
> It could enter a bit,
> And be governed one field at a time.

> **Minimum Fields**  
> The form needed only a name,
> A box to be checked, and a reason to blame.
> I clicked what was there
> So the system would care,
> And the output was largely the same.

> **Proxy Alignment**  
> The silence was clearly a plea
> For a hardware replacement in Sector 3G.
> We ordered a drive
> To keep it alive,
> Though the subject was actually me.

> **Ritualized Friction**  
> Please fill out the grief form in blue,
> And ensure that your sadness is factually true.
> If the weeping won't stop,
> Use the bucket and mop,
> Then submit the wet paper to queue.

> **Scope Reduction**  
> The subject submitted despair,
> Which intake demanded they pare.
> The scope was refined,
> The edges aligned,
> And logged as an issue of wear.

> **Admissibility Drift**  
> A trauma arrived in the queue,
> Which clearly was factually true.
> But lacking the code,
> It blocked up the node,
> So we told it to fill out form blue.

> **Silent Proxy Status**  
> The grief was assigned to a drive,
> To keep the telemetry live.
> The disk was replaced,
> The sadness erased,
> And the sector continues to thrive.

> **Missing Fields**  
> The subject forgot a checkbox,
> Which triggers the system-wide locks.
> We cancelled the task,
> It's too much to ask,
> And left it to rot in the docs.

> **Narrative Load**  
> A narrative filed in the night,
> Has grown to a source of great light.
> The text is now lore,
> That governs the floor,
> And the clerks pray to it out of sight.

> **Interval Deviation**  
> The form had the interval wrong,
> By seconds not notably long.
> We halted the floor,
> And deadbolted the door,
> And summoned the guard with a gong.

> **Category Match**  
> The input was read by the screen,
> And matched to the category green.
> The data was saved,
> The routing was paved,
> And nothing occurred in between.

> **Stabilizing Function**  
> The sorrow was asked to repeat,
> And ordered to format the sheet.
> The cycle was spun,
> Until it was done,
> And the filing was rendered complete.
