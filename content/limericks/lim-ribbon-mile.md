**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-RIBBON-MILE  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-RIBBON-MILE  
**Description:** Limericks about infinite paths, endless URLs, and the patron who keeps extending the route past every destination.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Path Resolved, But Then—**  
> A path resolved—the job was done—
> But Ribbon Mile stretched *another one*.
> A segment appeared,
> The route got smeared,
> And the link had just barely begun.

> **Extended Past the Browser**  
> The URL grew *so very long*,
> The browser could not read along.
> It exceeded the scope
> That browsers could cope,
> And the address went completely wrong.

> **Six Segments After Arrival**  
> The destination was finally clear—
> But the path kept *extending from here*.
> Six segments more came,
> This routing-gone game,
> Till nowhere was anywhere near.

> **Classified as Infrastructure**  
> The redirect chain got *so long*,
> That it classified itself as infrastructure strong.
> The router could not
> Break the knot,
> And the path just kept spiraling on.

> **Trailing Ribbon Forever**  
> Behind every successful navigation,
> Comes Ribbon Mile's dedication.
> The route stays *unbound*,
> You never come around,
> Just further and further elation.

> **No Terminus, Only Further**  
> The path mapping shows *no end*—
> Just further and further to send.
> You'll never arrive,
> You'll struggle to strive,
> And "further" is all you will lend.

> **The Endless URL Dream**  
> A journey began and grew long,
> The addressing completely gone *wrong*.
> The segments grew vast,
> You'd never reach last,
> And the path played its infinite song.

> **Cartography of the Infinite**  
> The mile marker *stretched and grew wide*,
> The route had no where to hide.
> Each segment extended,
> The journey ne'er ended,
> Just pathway and ribbon and tide.
