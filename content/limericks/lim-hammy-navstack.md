**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-HAMMY-NAVSTACK

**Description:** A concise verse addressing ceremonial hamburger menus, layered routing sandwiches, and a mascot literally made of navigation buns.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **Condiment Denial**  
> Hammy's *top bun* routes to Home—
> His meat governs mascots that roam.
> His lettuce? That's forms.
> His ketchup? That's storms.
> (He denies it. No condiment's shown.)

> **Sacred PDF Stack**  
> He stacked *one menu* so very deep
> That it rendered as PDF in his sleep.
> The links were all sacred,
> The structure unlacquered,
> And the dropdown was quite the heap.

> **Actual Lunch Outage**  
> He was printed as *actual lunch*
> During a site outage—a hunch
> That the rendering failed,
> The sandwich prevailed,
> And the team took a bite with a crunch.

> **Dissonant Chords**  
> He routes `/logout` to `/birth-records`—
> An accident that shattered all accords.
> The users confused,
> The navigation abused,
> And the sidebar struck dissonant chords.

> **Baked Breadcrumb**  
> "*Layered for every route,*" he said,
> While sizzling when tapped on the head.
> His 44 pixels wide
> Were a spiritual guide,
> And the breadcrumb was baked into bread.

> **Purity of Stack**  
> He denies *ranch.* He denies *ketchup.*
> His condiment policy's a checkup
> On purity of stack:
> No sauce front or back,
> And the layers are strictly a step-up.

> **Visually Edible**  
> Cssandra declared him "*visually edible*"—
> The highest praise, almost incredible.
> His layers were crisp,
> His menu a wisp,
> And the sandwich was architecturally credible.

> **Wireframe Origins**  
> His *wireframe origins* are humble:
> Misplaced buttons, a menu-toggle tumble.
> He's literally a burger,
> A nav-layer merger,
> And his emotional stacking won't crumble.

> **Inspired Embrace**  
> The Council decreed that his *face*
> May replace the hamburger in place.
> No votes were required—
> The sandwich was inspired—
> And the menu icon got an embrace.

> **Perfectly Real Burger**  
> So if navigation feels like a meal
> And the routing has sandwich appeal,
> That's *Hammy's* domain:
> Where the stacks will remain,
> And the burger menu's perfectly real.
