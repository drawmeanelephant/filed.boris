**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about Carpet Jurisdiction, where your power is strictly determined by the quality of your flooring.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12

> **The Power of Flooring**  
> The manager’s office is plush,
> Which forces the workers to hush.
> The Carpet decides
> Where the power resides,
> While the rest of us sit in the mush.

> **Zone-Colored Power**  
> If you step on the blue, you’re the boss,
> If you step on the gray, it’s a loss.
> The Jurisdiction lies
> In the terrible dyes,
> And nobody gives a damn toss.

> **Legal Acoustics**  
> The thicker the carpet you tread,
> The louder your bullshit is spread.
> The acoustics are grand,
> In the executive land,
> While the rest of the company’s dead.

> **Slowly Unwinding**  
> The carpet was legally binding
> The human was secretly grinding
> His teeth in the dark
> Like a digital spark
> The server was slowly unwinding

> **Going Insane**  
> We vacuum the legal domain
> It washes right down the old drain
> The server is flat
> We leave it at that
> The logic is going insane

> **Painfully Bright**  
> The carpet is claiming a right
> To turn out the digital light
> The manual bled
> The server is dead
> The error is painfully bright

> **Terrible Flaw**  
> A rug with the power of law
> Is hiding a terrible flaw
> We walk on the floor
> And walk out the door
> The system is totally raw
