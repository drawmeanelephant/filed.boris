**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about smoothing charts, laundering outliers, and putting on a play of improvement theatre.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-12  

***

> **Smoothed Outlier**  
> The outlier stood up in the chart,
> With a rude and particular heart.
> So they smoothed it away
> For the narrative day,
> And called that improvement art.

> **Baseline Purity**  
> The baseline was polished and pure,
> Which made every exception obscure.
> If the messy bits leave,
> The reviewers believe
> That the process has learned how to cure.

> **Improvement Theatre**  
> The trendline performed getting well,
> Though the hallway had stories to tell.
> When the variance thinned,
> The applause could begin,
> And the outliers stayed where they fell.

> **Variable Check**  
> The integer has been declared,
> And memory properly shared.
> The scope is defined,
> The logic outlined,
> The syntax is fully repaired.

> **Undefined Terror**  
> A variable lacked its true type,
> We must sound the emergency pipe!
> The string might be int,
> We're ready to sprint,
> And give all the servers a wipe!

> **Culinary Variables**  
> The string holds a cup of hot tea,
> As coded in section part three.
> We stir the array,
> And drink it away,
> To satisfy protocol fee.

> **Just Name It X**  
> I called the new variable 'x',
> Which saves me from writing out specs.
> It works as a bool,
> Or maybe a tool,
> Whatever the compiler expects.

> **Assignment 9**  
> The value of ten was assigned,
> Exactly as it was designed.
> The pointer moved on,
> The cycle has gone,
> The data is firmly confined.

> **Standard Maintenance Protocol**  
> The server requires a restart,
> To manage the indexing chart.
> All data will freeze,
> So log off with ease,
> And let the new processing start.
