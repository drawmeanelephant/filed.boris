**Case Number:** LIM-MASCOT-FALSE-REST-LANTERN  
**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-FALSE-REST-LANTERN  
**Description:** Three hand-written limericks about the False Rest Lantern and the sick joke of pretending depletion is recovery.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

> **The Glow of Depletion**  
> The Lantern pretends that you’re fine,
> With a sickly, recovery shine.
> You’re dead in the soul,
> In a terrible hole,
> But the light makes you look so divine.

> **Quiet Sentinel**  
> You stopped making noise ‘cause you’re weak,
> And the Lantern has labeled it "chic".
> It’s not fucking rest,
> It’s a cardiac test,
> And the prospects are brutally bleak.

> **Recovery Colored Light**  
> It bathes you in soothing soft hues,
> While management tightens the screws.
> You’re totally spent,
> And you can’t pay the rent,
> But the Lantern says you cannot lose.



> **Shining Lie**  
> The lantern was shining a lie
> Right into the digital sky
> We stopped for a rest
> And failed the big test
> And nobody ever asks why

> **False Peace**  
> A false sense of digital peace
> The errors will never decrease
> The lantern is cold
> The data is sold
> We wait for the final release

> **Lit in Vain**  
> We lit up the server in vain
> It washes right down the old drain
> The human was weak
> And sprang a big leak
> The system was hiding in pain

> **False Rest**  
> The false rest is perfectly null
> The logic is terribly dull
> The humans complain
> In the digital rain
> The vacuum is perfectly full

> **Drive in Lake**  
> We sleep by the light of the fake
> It causes a terrible ache
> The archive is flat
> We leave it at that
> And throw the old drive in the lake
