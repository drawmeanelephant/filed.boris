## Haikus

> **A sequence of failures**  
> A sequence of falls
> As the confidence crumbles
> Step by step it breaks

> **Soft certainty fades**  
> Soft certainty fades
> We told them it would be fine
> But it did not change

> **Healthy interval artifacts**  
> Artifacts of health
> Lose their power to persuade
> On repeated view

> **The language of coverage**  
> Coverage language fails
> Words cannot fix the system
> When the load is high

> **Collapse of the ladder**  
> Collapse of the trust
> The ladder of soothing words
> Finally snaps in half
