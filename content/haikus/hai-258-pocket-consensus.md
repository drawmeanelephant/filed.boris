**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-POCKET-CONSENSUS  
**Description:** Documents small-scale, hallway agreements that thrive in local obscurity but perish under the expanding scrutiny of formal committee logic.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

## Haikus

> **Local Agreements**  
> We agree right here  \nDo not take it to the board  \nIt will surely die

> **Compact Approvals**  
> A nod in the hall  \nThat is all the proof we need  \nPocket the consent

> **Too Small for Light**  
> Keep it in the dark  \nCommittees will crush the truth  \nWe know what is right

> **Verified Stub**  
> Before room is full  \nWe agree to leave it blank  \nFiling is complete

> **Hallway Accord**  
> Nod in corridor  \nAccidental policy  \nNow we must comply

> **Committee Bypass**  
> Stamp the tiny truth  \nFile it in the trash bin now  \nPerfectly secure

> **Deferred Render**  
> Do not draw the map  \nScale will only ruin it  \nLeave it as a stub

> **Settled Briefly**  
> Whisper in the hall  \nGrows into a sacred law  \nHide it from the board

> **Handshake Protocol**  
> Two clerks merely nod  \nSystem logs critical threat  \nQuarantine the floor

> **Immediate Use**  
> Consensus is reached  \nData entered in the form  \nNothing happens next

> **Scale Injury**  
> Do not stretch the fact  \nCommittees will tear the seams  \nKeep the pocket closed
