**Parent Entry:** MASCOT-WRONG-DOOR-FINCH  
**Description:** Misdirected arrival entity that faithfully turns up at windows never waiting for it, repeatedly landing at the wrong glass pane.  
**Updated At:** 2026-07-10  

***

## Haikus

> **Faithful Mistake**  
> I arrive on time
> But the window is not right
> Faithful to the wrong

> **Glass Pane**  
> Peeking through the glass
> Nobody is waiting here
> I will land again

> **Misdirected Bird**  
> Finch upon the sill
> The destination was moved
> Still I come to knock


> **Painted Door**  
> Finch knocked on the wood
> But the door was painted on
> Wrong portal again

> **Missing Hallway**  
> Feathers hit the frame
> But the hallway isn't there
> Server stands alone

> **Fully Lost**  
> Routing is a mess
> Little bird is fully lost
> Archive takes him in

> **Flying Loops**  
> We assign a path
> But the exit doesn't work
> Keep flying in loops

> **Mascot Offline**  
> Wrong door opens up
> Emptiness is all we see
> Mascot is offline
