## Haikus

> Etching words in ash  \nThe dictate glows for a breath  \nThen turns to grey dust

> Records made of fire  \nBureaucracy of the flame  \nRead it 'ere it fades

> **Fading Dictate**  
> The memo was clear  \nBut the paper was consumed  \nNothing is kept safe
