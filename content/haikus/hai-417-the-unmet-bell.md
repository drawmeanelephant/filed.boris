## Haikus

> **Untouched Desk**  
> It rang only once
> But nobody came to help
> Expectation dies

> **Retired Promise**  
> Dashboard has no green
> The lights are all turning red
> Mascot of the fail

> **Unfulfilled**  
> Ringing in the dark
> Waiting for the answer now
> Bell is left alone


> **Silence Hall**  
> Expectation fails
> The unmet bell makes no sound
> Silence in the hall

> **Hollow Brass**  
> Ring the hollow brass
> But the clapper isn't there
> Nothing has arrived

> **Bell Dead**  
> We wait for the tone
> Server drops the final ping
> Bell is fully dead

> **Never Met**  
> Missing what we need
> Requirement was never met
> Archive is aware

> **Quiet Bell**  
> The request is wrong
> We cannot fulfill the hope
> Ring the quiet bell
