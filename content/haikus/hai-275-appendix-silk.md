## Haikus

> **Comforting Textile**  
> Rough words at the end  \nWrapped in a smooth silken sheet  \nTact hides the despair

> **Softer Formatting**  
> Do not hurt the eyes  \nLet the findings sleep in peace  \nBuried in the back

> **Escort with Tact**  
> Show them to the door  \nBut give them a velvet coat  \nTruth must be polite

> **Formal Displacement**  
> Human errors filed  \nMoved to back in softest font  \nTruth is safe to skip

> **Respectable Omission**  
> Hide the user fault  \nPut the failure out of sight  \nSilken pages close

> **Courtesy Shield**  
> Soft words wrap the crash  \nAnnex hides the human flaw  \nPeace on the front page

> **The Elegant Void**  
> Format forms the void  \nToxic inputs gently placed  \nRecord sleeps in peace
