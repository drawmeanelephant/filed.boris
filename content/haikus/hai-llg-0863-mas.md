## Haikus

> **Granting emotional closure**  
> The minutes declare
> That everyone feels resolved
> Though the room is tense

> **Stylized meeting language**  
> Stylized meeting notes
> Grant an absolution seal
> To a broken plan

> **Bearing the seal**  
> Bearing the wax seal
> The follow-up packet comes
> Washing sins away

> **Unresolved conditions**  
> Unresolved details
> Swept beneath the written word
> Formally forgiven

> **Absolution by text**  
> Absolution by text
> We cannot fix what is wrong
> So we write it right
