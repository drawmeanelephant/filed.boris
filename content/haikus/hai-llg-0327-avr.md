## Haikus

> **Failure leaves the room**  
> Failure leaves the room
> wearing a softer necktie now
> deficit sits down

> **Calm continuity**  
> Calm continuity
> is a beautiful phrase for cracks
> that never got fixed

> **Realignment hums**  
> Realignment hums
> like a vacuum over shards
> glass still cuts the hand

> **Recurring trouble**  
> Recurring trouble
> learns a more presentable
> middle-management voice

> **Silk over sirens**  
> Silk over sirens
> that is the whole governance trick
> if nobody fights

> **The warning sign blurs**  
> The warning sign blurs
> under an assurance glaze
> and calls itself health

> **Language does not weld**  
> Language does not weld
> the split beam in the ceiling there
> it paints it cream first

> **Deficit, renamed**  
> Deficit, renamed
> keeps doing the same old work
> just with cleaner hair

> **So much poise in this**  
> So much poise in this
> you could miss the floor still warps
> under your good shoes

> **When calm words arrive**  
> When calm words arrive
> too quickly over old smoke
> open a window
