> **The Idle Clerk**  
> The ink finds the page,
> But the tray remains unread,
> No stamp is applied.

> Words fill up the form,

> Testimony ends,
> The desk has no red ink pad,

> Written on yellow paper,
> Kept far from the chief.

> A carbon copy,
