## Haikus

> **System Fine**  
> The walls are all dust
> But the monitors are bright
> System says it's fine

> **Actors on Stage**  
> Actors on a stage
> Pretending the server runs
> While it slowly melts

> **Theatre of Green**  
> Theatre of the green
> Every status light ablaze
> Covering the dark

> **Read the Lines**  
> We are broken down
> But the script says we are strong
> So we read the lines

> **Cheaper Repair**  
> Optical success
> Is much cheaper than repair
> Smile for the new graph

> **Fabric of Lies**  
> Continuity
> Is a beautifully made
> Fabric of pure lies

> **Frayed Wires**  
> No one checks the back
> Where the wires are all frayed
> Front end looks so sleek

> **Curtain Falls**  
> Keep the show alive
> Even if the cast is dead
> Curtain must not fall

> **Drop the Mask**  
> Performance of health
> While the sickness eats the core
> Never drop the mask

> **Perfectly Aligned**  
> Status green and bright
> Hiding catastrophic rot
> Perfectly aligned
