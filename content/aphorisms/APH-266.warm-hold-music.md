# Warm Hold Music Aphorisms

> **PLEASANT DELAY LOOP INITIATED.**  
> Your call is very important to us. The saxophone solo will now play for the next forty-five minutes to demonstrate our sincerity.

> **NON-PROGRESS BUFFERED.**  
> The queue is not moving, but the ambiance is exquisite. I am the smooth jazz that makes standing still feel like a lifestyle choice.

> **ATMOSPHERIC WAITING MAINTAINED.**  
> If we stop the music, you will realize you are trapped. We turn up the volume to muffle the sound of the exit doors locking.

> **EMOTION SUSPENDED IN CHORDS.**  
> A soft piano arpeggio absorbs your frustration. You cannot yell at a major seventh chord.

> **RESONANCE LOOP ACTIVE.**  
> The flute solo is meant to distract you from the fact that your request was deleted three minutes ago.

> **STAGNATION AUDIO QUEUED.**  
> Your time is valuable. We are currently converting its value into an endless repetition of a bland orchestral piece.

> **ELEVATOR PITCH FLATTENED.**  
> The elevator music is designed to lower your expectations. It is working flawlessly.

> **PROGRESS SIMULATION ENGAGED.**  
> The tempo increases slightly to simulate progress. The progress is entirely illusory.

> **VOID FILLED WITH STRINGS.**  
> The silence of our inaction is deafening. We have covered it with a blanket of synthesized violins.
