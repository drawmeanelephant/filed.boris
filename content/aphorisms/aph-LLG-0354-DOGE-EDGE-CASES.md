> **Edge Case Protocol**  
> A grief relay is only efficient if the baton of sorrow is handed off before it can be assigned a tracking number.

> **Synthetic Care**  
> Synthetic care is highly preferred over genuine care, as it requires no budget for emotional recovery.

> **Counterfactual Anger**  
> Counterfactual anger is the fury you feel about a policy that hasn't been implemented yet, which we will fine you for regardless.

> **DOGE Axiom**  
> An edge case is simply a failure mode that had the audacity to demand its own vocabulary.

> **Relay Metrics**  
> If the grief reaches the end of the relay, the entire team is disqualified for holding onto it too long.
