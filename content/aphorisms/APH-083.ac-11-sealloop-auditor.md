# AC-11 Sealloop Auditor Aphorisms

> **Nested accreditation. Jurisdiction shifted.**  
> Verifies legitimacy by adding another layer of verification. Meaning adjusted around the winning selector.

> **The mascot cannot close a case. Status acknowledged.**  
> The mascot cannot close a case; it only escalates. Relief remained outside scope.

> **Treats seal placement as review. Boundary active.**  
> Treats seal placement as review, and review as something that can always be forwarded. Relevance expired before processing resumed.

> **Infinite Verification**  
> Legitimacy is achieved not by fact-checking, but by wrapping the initial lie in twelve layers of stamped approval.

> **Escalation Loop**  
> The auditor does not solve problems; they merely attach a heavier, more expensive seal to them and pass them on.

> **Seal Stewardship**  
> A document lacking a seal is invalid. A document with fourteen nested seals is classified as 'Management Insight.'

> **Surveillance Redundancy**  
> We are currently reviewing the review of the review to ensure the first review actually happened. It didn't.

> **Signature of Failure**  
> The presence of the Sealloop Auditor guarantees the process has officially abandoned all hope of finality.
