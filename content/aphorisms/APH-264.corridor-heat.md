# Corridor Heat Aphorisms

> **UNMINUTED TENSION RECORDED.**  
> The meeting minutes say 'consensus was reached.' I record the temperature of the air between the two directors as they walked out of the room. It was boiling.

> **HALLWAY AFTERGLOW LINGERS.**  
> The argument didn't happen on the record, but the sheer thermal output of the unsaid words has warped the drywall near the elevator.

> **ADMINISTRATIVE TEMPERATURE RISING.**  
> They smiled at each other. The smile radiated pure radiation. I am holding a Geiger counter to the organizational chart.

> **INVISIBLE FRICTION TRACKED.**  
> The packet contains the decisions. I contain the friction it took to make them. If you touch my fur, you will burn your hand on office politics.

> **THERMAL BUILDUP RECORDED.**  
> The silence in the hallway is dense. It is the sound of three managers mutually refusing to concede a minor point.

> **DISSENT TRAPPED IN TRANSIT.**  
> The disagreement was not formalized. It exists entirely as an elevated pulse rate between the boardroom and the elevator.

> **FRICTION COEFFICIENT HIGH.**  
> Two opposing directives are sliding against each other in the corridor. The resulting heat could warm the building.

> **UNSPOKEN TENSION QUEUED.**  
> The hostility is strictly professional. It will be scheduled for a follow-up meeting where nothing will be resolved.
