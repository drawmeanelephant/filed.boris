# OG Kushkeeper Aphorisms

> **Guard the kush. Record sealed.**  
> Guard the kush, preserve the earth. Relevance expired before processing resumed.

> **Earthy pine remains. Interpretation pending.**  
> Earthy pine remains, Warden of the ancient grove, Kushkeeper observes. The system kept the ritual and misplaced the function.

> **Stamps patient records with pine-scented precision. Operational impact denied.**  
> Stamps patient records with pine-scented precision. Silence entered the record with full procedural honors.

> **Untouched Dirt**  
> The sentinel guards the earth by refusing to let anyone actually interact with the dirt. The ground is now read-only.

> **Pine Reversion**  
> Pine-scented precision requires stamping the records repeatedly until the paper turns back into a living tree.

> **Buried Legacy**  
> Legacy is preserved by physically burying the most important patient files in the soil of the ancient grove.

> **Decomposed Maps**  
> The kush is entirely safe. The maps detailing where it is kept have decomposed according to schedule.

> **Natural Delays**  
> Earthy heritage dictates that all bureaucratic delays must feel completely natural, organic, and entirely unavoidable.
