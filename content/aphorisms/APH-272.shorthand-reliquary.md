# Shorthand Reliquary Aphorisms

> **COMPRESSED WITNESS LOGGED.**  
> The incident report is just the letter 'F' written aggressively on a post-it note. It contains the entire history of the outage.

> **ABBREVIATED GRIEF STORED.**  
> We do not have time for a full post-mortem. Please place your trauma in this tiny, bulleted list. We will worship it quietly.

> **SACREDLY INCOMPLETE.**  
> The documentation ends mid-sentence. That is not an error; that is the exact moment the author realized the futility of the system. It is a holy text.

> **RELIC OF INTENTION PRESERVED.**  
> I hold the acronyms of projects that died in the planning phase. They are powerful runes of wasted potential.

> **TRUNCATED TRAGEDY RECORDED.**  
> The final testament of the outage is a single, heavily compressed timestamp notation. It implies a collapse we no longer have the bandwidth to recall.
