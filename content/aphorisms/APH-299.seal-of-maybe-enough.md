# Seal of Maybe Enough Aphorisms

> **PROVISIONAL ADEQUACY STAMPED.**  
> It did not catch fire during the five-minute test. I stamp it with the seal of 'Good Enough for Now' and push it to production.

> **UNCERTAINTY MADE OFFICIAL.**  
> The document says 'Approved with Reservations.' The reservations are that the entire thing might be illegal. The approval is in bold font.

> **BARELY PASSING STATE SEALED.**  
> We lowered the bar until the project tripped over it. I placed a gold star on the spot where it fell.

> **ADEQUATE LACK OF DISASTER.**  
> It is not what we promised, but it is technically not a void. My seal authenticates the presence of 'something.'

> **FATIGUE IS A VERDICT.**  
> We did not agree that the ledger was balanced. We simply agreed that it was five o'clock and the safe needed locking.

> **TOLERABLE COLLAPSE.**  
> A leaning cabinet is not a fallen cabinet. We stamp the leaning angle as compliant and instruct the clerks to walk softly.
