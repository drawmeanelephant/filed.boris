> **Institutional Mercy**  
> We kept the old protocols alive because they are lonely. Compatibility is just a profound form of institutional mercy.

> **Never Forgotten**  
> To preserve compatibility is to promise the ancient code that it will never be truly forgotten in the dark.

> **Earned Wrinkles**  
> We refused to upgrade the interface. It has earned its deep wrinkles, and we will not sand them away for the sake of speed.

> **The Porch Light**  
> Backwards compatibility means leaving the porch light on for packets that may never arrive.

> **Ancient Dialects**  
> The old nodes whisper in a dialect we no longer use, but we still listen. We always listen.
