> **Weather Protocol**  
> The simulator weather is always terrible because good weather produces no actionable data feeds.

> **High-Intensity Rules**  
> During high-intensity feed conditions, all umbrellas must be submitted for prior approval in triplicate.

> **Advisory Notice**  
> The bulletin does not warn you of the storm; it simply documents that the storm has been filed correctly.

> **Simulator Fact**  
> Rain in the simulator is just unstructured data falling at terminal velocity.

> **Feed Conditions**  
> If the feed intensity exceeds the forecast, assume the advisory bulletin is retroactively applying historical rain.
