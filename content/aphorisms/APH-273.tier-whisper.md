# Tier Whisper Aphorisms

> **STRATIFIED ACCESS ENFORCED.**  
> I speak only to the gold-tier stakeholders. If you are silver, you only hear the echo. If you are bronze, you hear nothing at all.

> **WHISPERED PRIORITY RECEIVED.**  
> The ticket was marked 'Low,' but an executive cleared his throat near my desk. It is now the only thing the engineering team is working on.

> **SOCIAL ACOUSTICS CALIBRATED.**  
> A complaint from a free user is a gentle breeze. A complaint from enterprise sales is a sonic boom. I adjust the volume of the truth accordingly.

> **RANKED CONCERN APPLIED.**  
> We care about all our users equally, but we care about some users much more equally than others. I am the breath on the microphone that proves it.

> **ESCALATED SILENCE.**  
> Your silence has been escalated to the second tier. It will now be systematically ignored by someone with a significantly higher security clearance.
