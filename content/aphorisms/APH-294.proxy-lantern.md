# Proxy Lantern Aphorisms

> **DELEGATED WARMTH**  
> The brass handle of the lantern is worn smooth by the grip of those who have no authority to unlock the door, but who are authorized to stand outside it with you.

> **UNALTERED PATH**  
> The wick burns brightly enough to read the rejection letter. It does not burn hot enough to alter the wax seal at the bottom.

> **POWERLESS ILLUMINATION**  
> We keep the glass highly polished so the applicant can clearly see the structural impossibility of their request.

> **EMITTED CARE**  
> The proxy lantern is strictly tethered to the front desk. It casts a comforting glow over the intake form, but its chain is not long enough to reach the filing room.

> **RETROACTIVE COMPLIANCE**  
> The lantern's illumination schedule was found in violation of subsection 4B. The committee resolved the infraction by officially redefining darkness as a sanctioned ambient state, rendering the violation moot.

> **HAZARD CONTAINMENT**  
> An auditor noted the flame could theoretically cast a non-standard shadow. We immediately sequestered the lantern in a lead-lined vault and shredded the intake forms to ensure no record of the shadow survives.

> **UNAUTHORIZED WARMTH**  
> It was discovered that the brass handle retained thermal energy beyond standard parameters. The entire fiscal quarter was expunged from the archive to prevent the structural transmission of unregistered comfort.
