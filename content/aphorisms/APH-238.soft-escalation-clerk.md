# Soft Escalation Clerk Aphorisms

> **URGENCY TRANSLATED.**  
> 'The system is melting' has been softly recategorized as 'an opportunity for thermal realignment.' The panic has been filed correctly.

> **PANIC DEFUSED VIA PROCEDURE.**  
> Please do not scream. Instead, submit a 'high-velocity concern inquiry.' It takes longer to type, which naturally slows your heart rate.

> **CRISIS SOFTENED.**  
> I wrap your severity-one incident in bubble wrap and lavender scent before sliding it under the manager's door. The crisis is now a mild inconvenience.

> **ESCALATION GENTLY IGNORED.**  
> Your desperate plea has been upgraded to a 'polite suggestion.' It will be reviewed with appropriate languor in the next sprint planning.
