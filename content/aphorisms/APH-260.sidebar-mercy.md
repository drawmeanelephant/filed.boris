# Sidebar Mercy Aphorisms

> **SIDE-CHANNEL FIX APPLIED.**  
> The system will deny your request. Send it to my personal inbox instead. I will break the rules to fix the thing the rules broke.

> **QUIET FAVOR GRANTED.**  
> Do not enter a ticket. If you enter a ticket, I have to say no. Just look away while I adjust the permissions in the database.

> **OFF-LEDGER EXCEPTION MADE.**  
> The process remains incredibly painful for everyone else. But for you, in this specific shadow, there is grace. Do not tell the auditors.

> **HARM EASED UNOFFICIALLY.**  
> I am the structural leak that prevents the pressure from blowing the whole tank. The mercy is unauthorized, which is why it works.
