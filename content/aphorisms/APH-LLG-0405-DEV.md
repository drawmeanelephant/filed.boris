# Meta-Deviation Aphorisms

> **The forms are not fine. Leverage absent.**  
> The forms are not fine. The forms are always not fine. The form remained intact. The situation did not.

> **Information is right. Record sealed.**  
> Information is right, but the vessel is expired. Approved anyway. Meaning adjusted around the winning selector.

> **Recursion as a form of compliance. Interpretation pending.**  
> Recursion as a form of compliance. Relief remained outside scope.


> **Documented Errors**  
> Development is the process of replacing working code with documented errors.

> **Flawed Human**  
> We write the code to prove that the human is fundamentally flawed.

> **Compiler Surrender**  
> A successful build is simply a compiler that gave up.

> **Staging Ambition**  
> The staging environment is where ambition goes to rot.

> **Ignored System**  
> True deployment happens when the system is finally ignored.
