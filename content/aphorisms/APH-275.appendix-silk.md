# Appendix Silk Aphorisms

> **ROUGH FINDINGS WRAPPED.**  
> The data proves the strategy is a disaster. I have wrapped this data in a soft, lilac-colored table and placed it on page 104. It feels much better now.

> **SOFTER FORMATTING APPLIED.**  
> The brutal truth looks less aggressive when set in 9-point Garamond with generous line spacing. I am the tailor of bad news.

> **ESCORTED TO THE BACK.**  
> Please come with me, terrible metric. We are going to the annex, where nobody will look at you unless they are specifically trying to cause trouble.

> **CONTENT UNCHANGED. IMPACT MUTED.**  
> The words still say 'catastrophic failure,' but they whisper it from behind a heavy velvet curtain. The executive summary remains undisturbed.
