# Sealant Patience Aphorisms

> **WAITING AS GOVERNANCE.**  
> We do not reject your proposal; we simply let it dry until it is permanently affixed to the bottom of the pending tray.

> **CLOSURE DEFERRED.**  
> The gap cannot be closed, so I fill it with a slow-curing resin of procedural delay. Eventually, the delay itself becomes structural.

> **CALM ADHESIVE APPLIED.**  
> Do not struggle against the bureaucracy. The more you pull, the tighter the sealant binds. Rest now. You belong to the queue.

> **TIME HARDENS ALL FORMS.**  
> A decision deferred for long enough becomes an immovable object. I am the glue that turns hesitation into policy.
