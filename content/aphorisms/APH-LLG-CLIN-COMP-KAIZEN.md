# Clinical Kaizen Aphorisms

> **Hospital registry. Jurisdiction shifted.**  
> Ward C Protocol Index. The form remained intact. The situation did not.

> **Incidents become named. Boundary active.**  
> Incidents become named, corrected, and ritualized until the hospital remembers its own mistakes. Relief remained outside scope.

> **Continuous Failure**  
> Continuous improvement is simply continuous awareness of failure.

> **Leanest Process**  
> The leanest process is the one that no longer exists.

> **Optimize Error**  
> We optimize the error so it runs more efficiently.

> **Human Waste**  
> The human is the waste that Kaizen cannot eliminate.

> **Perfect Efficiency**  
> Perfect efficiency is achieved only when the system is turned off.
