# Sanctioned Quilt Aphorisms

> **STANDARDIZED PATCHWORK**  
> The quilt was assembled from off-cuts of previous wellness initiatives. It is extremely heavy, but the committee insists its density is therapeutic.

> **APPROVED SOFTNESS**  
> The seams of the comfort blanket are triple-stitched with compliance thread. It is impossible to unravel the relief from the liability waiver.

> **FRICTIONLESS FABRIC**  
> We mandate that the quilt be draped over every new incident. Its synthetic fibers are carefully engineered to slide right off any underlying trauma without absorbing it.

> **COMMITTEE WEAVE**  
> The pattern of squares has been officially validated. If a clerk shivers beneath it, we do not adjust the thermostat; we simply issue a second quilt.

> **TISSUE INTEGRITY FAILURE**  
> Inspection revealed a single loose thread on the third quadrant of the quilt. To prevent catastrophic comfort leakage, the incident report was permanently redacted and the affected personnel were reclassified as hypothetical variables.

> **THERAPEUTIC OVERLAP**  
> During the annual review, it was noted that the quilt provided unmetered relief without the proper liability documentation. The underlying event was immediately excised from the official timeline, effectively eliminating the need for warmth.
