> **Purity Office**  
> The Beltline Purity Office does not remove impurities; it merely reclassifies them as acceptable variance.

> **Sanctification Rule**  
> A defect is only a defect until it receives the stamp of sanctification; then it becomes a feature of the beltline.

> **Variance Axiom**  
> If the product is entirely ruined, it simply requires a higher tier of acceptable variance documentation.

> **Beltline Fact**  
> Purity is an illusion maintained by the relentless printing of sanctification certificates.

> **Acceptable Error**  
> An acceptable variance is just an error that the office is too tired to fix today.
