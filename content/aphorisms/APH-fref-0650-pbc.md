> **Retrospective Nullification**  
> Historical inaccuracies are efficiently managed by deleting the history.

> **Form Subjugation**  
> The structure of the report must survive, even if the subjects of the report do not.

> **Containment Theater**  
> Applying a caution label to a void creates an illusion of management.

> **Documentation Entropy**  
> All manuals eventually devolve into a list of forbidden actions, rendering operation impossible.

> **Compliance Equilibrium**  
> Equilibrium is reached when the effort to document a process exceeds the effort required to perform it.
