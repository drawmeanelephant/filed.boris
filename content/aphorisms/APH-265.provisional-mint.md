# Provisional Mint Aphorisms

> **PROVISIONAL LEGITIMACY ISSUED.**  
> The ink is still wet on the mandate. It has no budget and no staff, but the seal looks incredibly official. Do not touch it; it smudges.

> **CONFIDENCE PRINTED.**  
> We are minting coins of pure optimism. They cannot be exchanged for goods or services, but they clink beautifully in the pocket of an executive.

> **FRESH SEAL APPLIED.**  
> I stamp the document with absolute authority. Please do not look underneath the document to see if the table exists.

> **BACKING DEFERRED.**  
> We are running on faith and high-quality cardstock. The legitimacy is valid until someone attempts to actually use it.

> **CONFIRMATION DELAYED.**  
> The certificate of authenticity is in the mail. The mail is currently being routed through a metaphorical swamp.

> **VALIDITY PENDING REVIEW.**  
> This document proves you exist. We will begin verifying the document in three to five business cycles.

> **AUTHORITY BUFFERING.**  
> The seal is official. The power behind the seal is still downloading. Please stand by.

> **LEGITIMACY HELD IN ESCROW.**  
> We have granted you the title. The responsibilities of the title will be released upon completion of an endless form.
