# Minutes Without Motion Aphorisms

> **STRAIN HEARD AND TIMED.**  
> You spoke passionately for precisely four minutes and twelve seconds about the structural collapse. I have typed your passion into a bullet point.

> **MINUTED WITHOUT OBLIGATION.**  
> The cries for help are fully documented in the official record. Therefore, nobody is required to actually send help. The documentation is the help.

> **INTERVAL CLASSIFICATION RETAINED.**  
> Despite the weeping, the status of the sprint remains 'Green.' The weeping has been appended as an 'interesting qualitative observation.'

> **CONTINUITY LOGGED. MOTION DENIED.**  
> The board listened attentively. The board nodded. The board adjourned. Nothing moved. The system worked perfectly.

> **Beautiful Catastrophe**  
> We watch the logs pile up, a beautiful record of everything breaking in absolute silence. The disaster is inevitable, but at least we took good notes.

> **Gentle Warning**  
> Do not raise your voice. The board is listening intently, and any actual motion might disturb the fragile equilibrium of their concern.

> **Tender Advice**  
> If you must cry, do it strictly within the allotted time. It makes it easier for the familiar to cradle your sadness in the minutes.

> **Quiet Observation**  
> The strain was minuted, not to resolve it, but to give the pain a permanent, quiet home in the archives.

> **Rhythmic Collapse**  
> We do not ask the board to move anymore. We simply appreciate the gentle, rhythmic sound of their pens writing down our collapse.
