# Coverage Ledger Cherub Aphorisms

> **CARE-SHAPED GESTURE LOGGED.**  
> An email ending in 'warm regards' was sent to the burned-out team. I have added exactly one golden tally mark to the ledger of compassion.

> **TRACE TRACKED. WEIGHT IMMOVABLE.**  
> The manager smiled briefly during the mass layoff. I recorded the micro-expression as a 'high-empathy engagement event.'

> **GESTURE RECORDED WITHOUT EFFECT.**  
> I am the angel of useless gestures. I bless every pizza party that attempts to cure systemic understaffing.

> **Weightless Care**  
> Be careful not to expect the weight to lift. The cherub only counts the gestures of care; it cannot carry the burden for you.

> **Cherished Attempts**  
> The ledger is filled with tiny, beautiful traces of people who tried to help. The help failed, but the attempt is cherished forever.

> **Drowning in Kindness**  
> The database is overflowing with recorded smiles while the system starves. We are drowning in counted kindnesses that fix absolutely nothing.

> **Tender Gestures**  
> Do not mock the useless gesture. A pizza party cannot stop the rot, but it leaves a faint, sweet trace in the ledger's cold heart.

> **Equal Love**  
> The cherub loves every trace equally. It does not judge the futility of our efforts, only the sad, desperate beauty of the attempt.
