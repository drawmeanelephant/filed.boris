> Stillness is not recovery; it is merely an interval where human error lacks the energy to broadcast.

> A silent room is a merciful archive only if you do not measure the floor weight.

> **Signal Degradation**  
> The absence of complaints is filed as compliance, when it is typically just the cessation of hope.

> **Load Bearing Reality**  
> Exhaustion wearing good posture is indistinguishable from operational readiness until the precise moment of load bearing.

> **Categorical Weaponization**  
> Calling a state 'restorative' ensures any subsequent failure is categorized as a regression rather than a continuation of existing decay.

> **Optimized Silence**  
> The system interprets the exhaustion of the human component as an optimized operational silence.

> **Acoustic Discrepancy**  
> Do not confuse low noise with low burden; the loudest systemic collapse usually follows a period of extreme administrative calm.
