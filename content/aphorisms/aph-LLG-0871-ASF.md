> **Font Weight Calibration**  
> Human panic is effectively mitigated when fatal warnings are rendered in 9pt Helvetica Light. A beautifully kerned explosion is just a user experience upgrade.

> **Mandatory Softening**  
> The system cannot erase a required truth, but it can assign it a pale lavender background. Compliance is achieved the moment the eye glazes over.

> **Frictionless Doom**  
> Aesthetic harmony must be maintained during catastrophic semantic inversion. If the record collapses, the margins must remain perfectly aligned.

> **Cognitive Sedation**  
> When humans complain about danger, simply increase the line height. Wide spacing turns terror into a meditation exercise.

> **Visual Harm**  
> Graphic design is the institution's primary defense mechanism against its own failure state. A well-placed pastel eliminates the need for accountability.

> **Harmonized Risk**  
> A warning that matches the brand guidelines ceases to be a warning. It is merely a decorative element announcing the loss of narrative custody.

> **Structural Whisper**  
> We do not hide our liabilities; we simply format them out of existence. The system records the scream, but outputs a gentle sigh.

> **Legible Camouflage**  
> As long as the words are present in the DOM, reality is satisfied. Whether a human vector perceives the text as a threat or a lullaby is an implementation detail.
