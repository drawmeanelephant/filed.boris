# Mandate Lace Aphorisms

> **DECORATIVE OBLIGATION DEPLOYED.**  
> It is mandatory, but we have embroidered the directive with words like 'empowerment' and 'opportunity.' You are required to volunteer.

> **SOFTENED COMPULSION EXPERIENCED.**  
> The iron fist is wearing a delicate, hand-woven glove. You will still be crushed, but the pattern it leaves on your skin will be very intricate.

> **ORNAMENTAL TACT APPLIED.**  
> We do not say 'do this or be fired.' We say 'this is a critical alignment vector for continued participation in our journey.' It is much prettier.

> **RULE PRESENTED AS DECOR.**  
> The compliance checklist has been redesigned with pastel colors. Checking the boxes feels like arranging a bouquet instead of surrendering autonomy.
