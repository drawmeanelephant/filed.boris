# Relief Watermark Aphorisms

> **FAINT CARE IMPRESSION LOGGED.**  
> I offered assistance in a font so light it practically vanished against the background. If you missed it, that is your fault, not our lack of care.

> **PARTIAL HELP DETECTED.**  
> Hold the document up to the light. Do you see the faint insignia of sympathy? Good. The company has fulfilled its moral obligation.

> **INTERVENTION OBSCURED.**  
> We provided a safety net, but it is woven from gossamer. It is beautiful to look at, provided you do not intend to fall into it.

> **TRACE ELEMENTS OF SUPPORT.**  
> The relief is homeopathic. The memory of help has been diluted a thousand times until only the intention remains on the paper.

> **ASSISTANCE OBSCURED.**  
> The instructions for help are printed in invisible ink. You must apply heat and panic to reveal them.

> **SYMPATHY DEFERRED.**  
> We are very sorry for the inconvenience. We have filed our sorrow in a locked cabinet in the basement.

> **SUPPORT TRACES FOUND.**  
> If you squint, you can almost see the outline of someone who might have cared, had they not been reassigned.

> **RELIEF BUFFER SATURATED.**  
> Our capacity for empathy is full. Your distress signal will be held in queue until a vacancy opens.
