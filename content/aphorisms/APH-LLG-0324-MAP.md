# Managed Absence Aphorisms

> **Managed absence. Continuity retained.**  
> Ambiguity successfully renamed into a governed condition. The form remained intact. The situation did not.

> **Missing paperwork promoted to governance framework. Leverage absent.**  
> Missing paperwork promoted to governance framework. Meaning adjusted around the winning selector.

> **Supersession ambiguity. Record sealed.**  
> Aspirational definitions assumed to have occurred if everyone starts speaking as though they have. Relief remained outside scope.

> **Conceptually active. Jurisdiction shifted.**  
> The form does not exist, but its demands are strictly enforced. We have outsourced compliance to the collective imagination of the clerking staff.

> **Governed absence. Status acknowledged.**  
> A missing document is just a standard document that has achieved pure administrative theory. Human inability to locate it only proves its sacred status.

> **Superseded in theory. Leverage absent.**  
> Both forms canceled each other out in 2018, leaving a vacuum where policy used to be. Staff continue to fill them out purely to appease the desktop scanner.

> **Operationally absent. Interpretation pending.**  
> We can cite the form in court, but we cannot print a blank copy. The archive prefers it this way, as actual paper would introduce variables.
