**Description:** Aphorisms regarding the administrative sediment and procedural drift of the Driftlocked Policy Box.

***

## Contextual Records

> **Repetition As Law**  
> A workaround repeated ten times is an anomaly; repeated ten thousand times, it is foundational architecture.

> **Illegitimate Sediment**  
> We do not fix the broken process, we merely lock the improvised debris into the manual and call it procedure.

> **The Comfort of Drift**  
> To acknowledge the drift is to demand a rewrite, so the archive politely classifies the anomaly as standing policy.

> **Polished Brass**  
> The policy box is locked tight by decades of drift. We polish its brass handles and leave the contents to sleep.

> **Resting Softly**  
> Inside the box are rules we no longer understand, resting softly in the dark where no audit can wake them.

> **Protecting Dreams**  
> Driftlock is not a failure of access. It is the archive’s way of protecting its most fragile, impossible dreams.

> **Whispered Amendments**  
> We whisper our new amendments to the surface of the box. It does not open, but we know it hears us.

> **Unused Perfection**  
> Some policies are too precious to be enacted on the live floor. They are kept safely locked away, perfect and unused.
