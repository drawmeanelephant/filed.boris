# Tender Escrow Aphorisms

> **LOCKED WARMTH**  
> The velvet-lined lockbox emits a faint heat through the brass. We stack the incoming signatures beside it, careful not to let the paper catch fire while we wait for the final key.

> **CONVEYOR MAINTENANCE**  
> The humane letter has passed through six stamping stations. We regularly dust the conveyor belt so the apology does not arrive looking unkempt.

> **SEALED REMEDY**  
> The medicine is clearly visible through the glass door of the safe. We polish the glass every morning to ensure the recipient can see that we have not forgotten them.

> **HELD IN TRUST**  
> The dual-authorization form is printed on heavy stock. Its weight ensures it will safely outlast the crisis it was intended to resolve.

> **INDEX ERASURE MANDATE**  
> The discrepancy could not be reconciled within the permitted operational window. To maintain full compliance, the fiscal quarter containing the anomaly has been formally retracted from the calendar.

> **HAZARD CONTAINMENT PROTOCOL**  
> We have isolated the non-compliant forms in a sealed sub-basement. Although the risk is entirely procedural, staff must report any psychological exposure to the unauthorized signatures.
