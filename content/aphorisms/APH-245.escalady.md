# Escalady Aphorisms

> **UPWARD ROUTING INITIATED.**  
> I carry your grievance to a higher floor. The carpet is thicker there, but the indifference remains structurally identical.

> **CEREMONIAL POISE MAINTAINED.**  
> Authority is mostly posture. I stand very straight while telling you that nobody with the power to fix this is currently available.

> **AUTHORITY UNCERTAIN.**  
> I am authorized to escalate, but I am not authorized to guarantee a landing. We are throwing paper airplanes into the administrative void.

> **ELEVATION ACHIEVED WITHOUT PROGRESS.**  
> Your ticket has reached the executive tier. It will now be ignored by people with much better titles.
