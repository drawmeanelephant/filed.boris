# False Rest Lantern Aphorisms

> **SILENCE CLASSIFIED AS REST.**  
> The system has stopped screaming because it lacks the energy to draw breath. I illuminate this collapse with a soothing amber glow. We call it 'downtime.'

> **DEPLETION DISGUISED.**  
> You are not recovering; you are merely too tired to complain. My lantern makes your apathy look like peaceful meditation.

> **RECOVERY ILLUSION SECURED.**  
> The weekend is just a brief pause in the beating. I hold the light so you can temporarily mistake the pause for healing.

> **NOISE SUPPRESSION ACTIVE.**  
> If it is quiet, it must be fixed. I stand watch over the smoldering ruins, ensuring nobody mistakes the lack of fire for the presence of a building.
