# Recital of Sufficiency Aphorisms

> **CARE DECLARED SUFFICIENT.**  
> I have spoken the words 'thoughts and prayers' into the microphone. The algorithmic requirement for empathy has been fulfilled.

> **REMEDIATION PHRASE REPEATED.**  
> 'We are taking this very seriously.' Repeat it three times into the mirror, and the liability disappears.

> **RIGHT PHRASES. RIGHT ORDER.**  
> The incantation requires the phrase 'out of an abundance of caution.' If you do not say it, the auditors will cast you into the void.

> **SUFFICIENCY GRANTED VIA RITUAL.**  
> We have chanted the corporate values in unison. The problem is not solved, but the room feels structurally sound again.

> **FATIGUE GRANTED ADEQUACY**  
> The committee has spoken the closing words. The threshold was not crossed by the evidence, but by the unanimous exhaustion of the room.

> **PRONOUNCED SOLVENCY**  
> Do not confuse a durable foundation with a firmly delivered sentence. The tone of the chair is carrying the weight of the missing documents.

> **DECLARATIVE PEACE**  
> The filing cabinet does not demand a structural remedy. It only demands that the reviewing voice drops an octave at the end of the sentence.
