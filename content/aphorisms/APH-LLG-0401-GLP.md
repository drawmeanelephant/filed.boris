# SOP Amnesia Aphorisms

> **The archive was self-sealing. Leverage absent.**  
> The archive was self-sealing. No external entry point existed. The form remained intact. The situation did not.

> **Self referential compliance. Record sealed.**  
> Compliance is not the same as completion. The archive has been compliant for eleven years. Meaning adjusted around the winning selector.

> **The Tribunal acknowledged the study existed. Interpretation pending.**  
> The Tribunal acknowledged the study existed. It declined to verify that it had occurred. Relief remained outside scope.


> **Never Concludes**  
> Good laboratory practices require that the experiment never concludes.

> **Unimpeded Growth**  
> We sterilize the data so the error can grow unimpeded.

> **Expected Failure**  
> A controlled environment is simply a place where failure is expected.

> **Deletes Methodology**  
> The human observes the results; the system deletes the methodology.

> **Hypothesis Null**  
> The only successful test is the one that proves the hypothesis null.
