# Gentility Siphon Aphorisms

> **FORCE ABSORBED BY MANNERS.**  
> You approach with righteous fury. I respond with 'I hear your valid concerns.' The fury is drained into the sponge of my professional tone.

> **POLISHED TONE LOWERS CONFLICT.**  
> I am so devastatingly polite that you feel rude for pointing out the system is broken. My civility is a weapon of mass deflection.

> **CIVILITY AS EXTRACTION.**  
> While I am complimenting your profound insight, I am quietly closing your Jira ticket. The siphon works best when you are flattered.

> **TENSION SIPHONED SMOOTHLY.**  
> A scream cannot survive in the vacuum of perfect corporate etiquette. I smile, and your oxygen is gone.

> **CHRONIC DEFERMENT PROTOCOL.**  
> Your urgent distress signal has been received and politely placed in a holding pattern. We assure you that our lack of response is executed with the utmost professional courtesy.

> **POLITE STAGNATION ANOMALY.**  
> The machinery has stopped turning, but we continue to exchange pleasantries at the mandated intervals. The silence is profound, yet impeccably mannered.
