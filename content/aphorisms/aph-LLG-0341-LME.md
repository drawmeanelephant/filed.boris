> **Hereditary Waiver**  
> A hereditary waiver ensures that your grandchildren will also be exempt from understanding the rules.

> **Mutual Exception**  
> The Lodge operates on the principle that if everyone is an exception, the rule becomes a purely decorative object.

> **Lodge Culture**  
> True membership in the Lodge is achieved when you no longer remember what you are being waived from.

> **Exception Axiom**  
> An exception granted to all is simply a policy written in invisible ink.

> **Waiver Logic**  
> We waive the requirement because enforcing it would require us to read it, which violates the primary waiver.
