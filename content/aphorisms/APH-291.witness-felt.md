# Witness Felt Aphorisms

> **LINED TRAY**  
> The clerk lines the evidence tray with grey felt before presenting the grievance. The committee prefers its disasters delivered quietly.

> **SMOOTHED EDGES**  
> We sand the sharp edges off the sworn statement until it slides smoothly into the compliance folder without tearing the manila.

> **UPHOLSTERED TESTIMONY**  
> The witness stand is heavily upholstered. The room will not tolerate testimony that echoes against hard surfaces.

> **DAMPENED FREQUENCY**  
> Before reading the injury report into the record, we wrap the microphone in velvet. The facts remain, but the frequency is comfortably dampened.

> **THE PRESERVATION ARCHIVE**  
> The latency introduced between the intake of the form and the filing of the cabinet serves as a deliberate buffer. To decide immediately is to risk establishing a precedent.

> **COMMITTEE SUSPENSION**  
> Three adjacent departments must review the latency records. None hold the authority to approve a resolution, ensuring the suspension remains perfectly balanced in perpetuity.

> **OBSOLETE DIRECTIVES**  
> The structural manual for the previous sorting mechanism was discarded upon upgrade. The new parameters remain sealed in the vault to prevent unauthorized understanding of the current workflow.
