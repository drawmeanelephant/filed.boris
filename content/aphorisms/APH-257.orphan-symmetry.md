# Orphan Symmetry Aphorisms

> **BALANCED ABANDONMENT ACHIEVED.**  
> The feature was deprecated, but its icon remains perfectly centered in the navigation bar. The architecture is dead, but the design is flawless.

> **STRANDED LINKS ARRANGED.**  
> These buttons lead to nowhere, but they do so with a pleasing margin and uniform padding. We mourn their utility while admiring their CSS.

> **AESTHETICALLY TOLERABLE LOSS.**  
> A 404 error is acceptable if the illustration of the lost robot is sufficiently charming. I maintain the cuteness of our decay.

> **SYMMETRICAL DECAY MONITORED.**  
> If half the system must fall apart, I ensure the other half falls apart in exactly the same way to maintain visual harmony.
