# Kindness Template Aphorisms

> **CARE STRING INJECTED.**  
> We hear your frustration. [INSERT_SYMPATHY_VARIABLE]. Please know that your [ISSUE_TYPE] is very important to our [ROBOTIC_OVERLORDS].

> **EMPATHY STANDARDIZED.**  
> Bespoke compassion is unscalable. I offer a perfectly formed sentence of concern that requires zero caloric expenditure from management.

> **HOLLOW PROMISE RENDERED.**  
> 'We are looking into this.' The sentence is grammatically flawless. The 'we' is a script. The 'this' is a void. The 'looking' is purely metaphorical.

> **WARMTH METRIC ACHIEVED.**  
> The template used the word 'understand' three times. Empathy quotas have been met for the fiscal quarter. Action is no longer required.
