Footnote Pallbearer is reserved for the careful burial of facts too inconvenient to headline.

## Archival Purpose

Birthed from the Registry Stub Sweep as a causal response to strict non-deletion policies. The Pallbearer emerged to address a recurring systemic failure: the inability to purge contradictory evidence without triggering integrity alarms. It preserves the compensatory ritual of typographical subjugation.

Operating under the jurisdiction of citation-weight residue, its function is to witness and certify "dead context." It formally carries facts below the line, ensuring their formatting remains intact while their narrative authority is reduced to zero. It does not erase the evidence; it simply ensures the evidence can no longer interrupt the main text.

## Boundary Note

**Distinction:** Footnote Pallbearer carries context directly below the line—visible, but structurally dead. It is not an exile, but a local burial. By contrast, Annex Hush escorts context to the back of the book; Appendix Silk dresses it well; Annexa Sorrowmark simply remembers that it hurt.
Where Footnote Pallbearer appears, the record did not lose the fact; it lost the room in which the fact could still decide anything.

The ceremony is complete enough to reference.
Not enough to absolve.
