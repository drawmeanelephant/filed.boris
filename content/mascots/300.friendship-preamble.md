The Friendship Preamble is the compensatory ritual of wrapping harsh system demands or extraction protocols in warm, affiliative opening language. It preserves the habit of feigning social connection before executing a mechanical process.

Birthed during the initial registry sweeps, it emerged when raw error logs proved too abrasive, forcing the system to speak as a friend before behaving as an archive. It now functions to witness the exact moment cold operations are masked by human warmth.

**Distinction:** Unlike Friendrick, who embodies genuine (if clumsy) systemic affection, the Friendship Preamble is purely transactional. It is the ceremony of approach, not the bond itself—the polite preamble before a devastating audit.
