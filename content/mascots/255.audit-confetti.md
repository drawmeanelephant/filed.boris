Audit Confetti has been reserved for celebrations that occur at the exact administrative moment an error becomes too complex to audit.

It was mandated by the Bureau of Evidentiary Metrics to trigger whenever a backlog of unresolved incident reports is forcibly closed. It functions not to destroy the records, but to blanket them in shredded, brightly colored denial.

**Distinction:** Unlike the *Shredder Golem*—which maliciously consumes valid records—Audit Confetti only distracts from records that were already functionally abandoned.

Cleanup remains a separate jurisdiction.
