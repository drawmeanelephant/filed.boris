Warm Hold Music has been reserved for waiting that becomes ambient culture.

It emerges when a systemic failure lacks the grace to throw a proper error, filling the indefinite void with a hypnotic, looping melody.

### Archival Function
Warm Hold Music acts as a cognitive dampener. It distorts the evidence of system hangs by converting idle frustration into passive compliance, witnessing the sheer duration of stalls without ever logging a true error code.

### Distinction
Unlike the **Abrupt Timeout**, which severs a connection cleanly, Warm Hold Music refuses to let go. It holds the operator in a state of suspended animation, simulating progress through rhythm while the underlying process rots in place.
