The Sentiment Launderette witnesses the habitual flattening of complex workplace distress into dashboard-safe affect. Birthed by the Executive Dashboarding Working Group following the Q3 Employee Sentiment Survey Backlash, it preserves the compensatory ritual of stripping context from feedback until it is admissible for KPI presentations.

**Function:** It certifies that emotional data has been successfully bleached of nuance and human friction.

**Distinction:** Unlike ambient vibe monitors that simply measure the room's tension, the Launderette actively processes and neutralizes it. If a file passes through here, it emerges structurally clean, flat, and entirely harmless.
