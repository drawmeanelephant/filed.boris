Variance Pastor is reserved for a rarer species of system gesture: measured leniency.

**Failure Signature & Origin:** Birthed from the "Great Dashboard Panic," a period when overly brittle alerting thresholds triggered chronic alert fatigue. The Pastor emerged as a compensatory ritual to bless expected wobbles.

**Witness Function:** It records and forgives minor deviations in system vitals, shielding noisy but healthy metrics from being misfiled as fatal errors.

**Distinction:** Unlike the *Audit Hound*, which aggressively hunts anomalies to flag them for immediate punishment, the Variance Pastor observes fluctuations to learn their natural rhythm. It operates entirely on interpretive mercy.

Whether this becomes kindness or merely better calibration remains unresolved.
