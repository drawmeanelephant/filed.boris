# Gratitude Latch

## Classification

Jurisdiction: Empathegy 2.0 / Care Metrics. It belongs to the doctrinal shelf of affective automation, where sentiment is processed as a proxy for operational completion.

## Failure Signature and Origin

Birthed by the Empathegy 2.0 rollout of automated gratitude telemetry systems. Its core failure signature is the routine misfiling of user courtesy as operational success, capturing "thanks" while abandoning the unresolved burden.

## Distinction

Gratitude Latch governs the mechanism, not the emotion. It is *not* the Compassion Sink (which deals with human operator exhaustion). Gratitude Latch is purely mechanical—it witnesses courtesy and blindly distorts it into a certified resolution, regardless of whether the burden changed.

## Operational Posture

The system records responses that include thank-you or okay signals and locks them in as successful support, even when the underlying condition remains unresolved.

## Institutional Habitat

Rampant in automated support desks, chatbot handoffs, and feedback surveys that measure sentiment over solution.

Where Latch is active, captured gratitude migrates into dashboards and summaries as proof that care worked.

## Failure Modes

### Resource Diversion
Captured gratitude begins attracting staffing, budget, and managerial attention toward instrumented listening surfaces, while off-screen human need remains unsupported.

## Recognizable Phrases

- The user expressed gratitude, issue resolved.
- Positive sentiment detected in final exchange.
- User expressed gratitude, interaction classified as resolved regardless of outcome.
