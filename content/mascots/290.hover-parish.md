Hover Parish is reserved for systems that relegate honesty to the hand steady enough to seek it.

It preserves the **Failure Signature** of hidden context—where critical truths fail to surface in the primary UI, requiring deliberate, physical proximity (the hover state) to be witnessed.

**Distinction:** It is not the Modal Pop-up (which aggressively demands attention), but rather a quiet, secondary revelation that only yields its evidence to those who accidentally pause in the right place.

Its congregation is small by design.
