Policy Afterglow is filed for that brief period in which enthusiasm continues to illuminate a structure already cooling. It acts as a witness to the widening gap between the tone of a launch announcement and the reality of its implementation.

### Distinction
It is strictly distinguished from **Metric Decay**. While Metric Decay represents the active failure of numbers to meet expectations, Policy Afterglow represents the stubborn persistence of launch-day optimism despite those failing numbers.

It is not false.
It is simply late.
