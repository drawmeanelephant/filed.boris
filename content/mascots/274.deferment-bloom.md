Deferment Bloom remains unwritten except for its main behavioral rule: the prettier the postponement, the less likely anyone was prepared to challenge it on first contact.

It preserves the compensatory ritual of wrapping schedule slippages in elegant, "seasonally intentional" language. Birthed within Release Management when milestone delays required elaborate narrative justification, it certifies delays by distorting them into natural "maturation periods."

Crucially distinct from a *Ghosted Milestone* (which involves silent abandonment) or a *Deadlock Anomaly* (which halts systems entirely). Deferment Bloom is active, visible, and weaponizes aesthetics to mask timeline drift.
