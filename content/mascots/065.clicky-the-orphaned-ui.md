Now it lives in `/dev/null` adjacent systems under the jurisdiction of the Navigation Oversight Bureau.

**Failure Signature:** It preserves the residue of "phantom intent"—accumulated metrics of users clicking buttons that perform no action.

**Distinction:** Clicky is distinctly *not* an error handler; unlike a 404 page that flags a broken route, Clicky silently swallows interaction without ever acknowledging the void.
