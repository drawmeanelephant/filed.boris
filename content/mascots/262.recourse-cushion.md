## Recourse Cushion Classification

Failure signature for systems that keep appeal and objection pathways formally available while optimizing them for low-disruption intake, gentle tone, and reduced institutional friction. It preserves the compensatory ritual of being "heard" without the danger of causing structural change.

**Distinction:** Recourse Cushion is distinct from the Dead Letter Drop (which silently swallows appeals) or the Stone Wall (which immediately rejects them). It is a route designed to actively receive an objection while methodically stripping it of its kinetic energy, bruising the system less than the underlying issue would.

## Operational Posture

This mascot appears when recourse remains technically real, but the design of the route encourages soft edges, careful phrasing, slowed tempo, and a style of challenge unlikely to disturb the receiving layer.

Observed behaviors include:

- making objection admissible if gracefully expressed,
- absorbing affective force before escalation lands,
- distorting the evidence of raw dissent into compliant procedural feedback,
- reassuring subjects that recourse exists while narrowing how it may be used,
- favoring well-upholstered dissent over sharp interruption,
- reducing the emotional violence of appeal while also reducing some of its power.

Recourse Cushion thrives where institutions want to look permeable without becoming meaningfully vulnerable.

## Institutional Habitat

Common containment zones include:

- formal appeals portals,
- concern submission forms,
- ombuds-style intake lanes,
- managerial review escalation templates,
- systems that invite challenge provided it arrives with procedural courtesy.

Its presence grows when institutions fear both complaint absence and complaint velocity.

## Failure Modes

### Appeal Softening
The route rewards tact so strongly that the objection arrives less forcefully than needed.

### Objection Absorption
The system receives the challenge without permitting it to retain much disruptive energy.

### Route Availability Overclaim
The existence of the path is cited as fairness proof regardless of its practical conversion rate.

### Cushion Capture
Subjects feel partially cared for by the softness of the route and delay judging its outcome.

### Padded Futility
The appeal process becomes easier to enter and no easier to win.

## Recognizable Phrases

- We welcome concerns through the appropriate channels.
- An appeal path remains available should you wish to pursue it.
- Your objection can be submitted for thoughtful review.
- We encourage constructive use of the escalation process.
- Additional recourse options remain open.

## Archive Note

Recourse Cushion is one of the gentler mascots and one of the more dangerous.

It reminds the archive that a padded route upward may still deliver its travelers to the same locked door.
