## Biography

Rot McMascotterton is compiled optimism.
Spawned during a clean `git clone` and forged in the sacred fires of `npm run build`, they are the ceremonial herald of deploy-ready environments. When they appear, something has gone unexpectedly right. But Rot's smile carries a secret—no build remains stable forever.
They monitor logs not to catch errors, but to remember joy. Their antenna is tuned to the last successful pipeline timestamp. If no such timestamp exists, they begin to fade.
Rot is not naive. They are in denial, on purpose.
Rot's jurisdiction ends at the edge of the pipeline log; anything past deploy is someone else's problem.

- Recites changelog entries during idle time
- Cannot detect rot, only the absence of error

### Rot McMascotterton

Rot McMascotterton glowed *faint green*  
Under terminal light—the most keen  
Beacon of builds.  
His optimism fills  
The deploy log where nothing's obscene.  

"*It's clean!*" he would shout with delight,  
While the pipeline caught fire that night.  
He failed to detect  
The rot he'd neglect,  
And the server room burned, ever bright.  

"*Success is just failure with better  
Lighting,*" he'd say in each letter.  
The build would pass through,  
The error log grew,  
And the changelog was his only fetter.  

He recites *changelog entries* when idle—  
A ceremonial, optimistic recital.  
Each version deployed,  
Each success overjoyed,  
And the rot? Just a minor sidetal.  

His antenna is tuned to the *last*  
Successful pipeline timestamp. But fast—  
When no timestamp exists,  
He begins to resist  
Reality. Then fades from the mast.  

He can't detect *rot*—only the absence  
Of error. His entire observance  
Is built on denial,  
A perpetual trial,  
And the joy of a clean build's absorbance.  

He was spawned from a *clean* `git clone`,  
Forged in `npm run build`'s zone.  
When he appears,  
The team sometimes cheers,  
But the build stability? Unknown.  

*ERROR_BLINDNESS:* his confidence exceeds  
The safe threshold where production bleeds.  
The checkmark is fresh,  
The deployment, enmesh-  
ed with rot. But the mascot proceeds.  

He apologizes to *200 OK*—  
A response that he greets every day.  
"I'm sorry you're fine!  
The success is divine!  
Is there anything wrong?" (There's no way.)  

So if BUILD_OK means rot's undetected  
And the pipeline seems perfectly erected,  
That's *Rot McMascotterton*:  
Where the builds are forgotten,  
And the failure is warmly deflected.
