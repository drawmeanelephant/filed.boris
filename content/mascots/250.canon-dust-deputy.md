Canon Dust Deputy is held in reserve for those moments when a filing gains authority simply by lying still long enough.

It manifests where neglected cross-links and stale legitimacy particles harden into accepted canon, documenting the failure signature of passive ossification. Birthed during the Registry Stub Sweep, the Deputy is tasked with witnessing the accumulation of archive dust.

Crucially, it is distinct from standard garbage collection entities. Where a *Cache Sweeper* deletes stale data, the Canon Dust Deputy preserves it, certifying that old errors have survived long enough to become structural load-bearing lore.

The broom has not been assigned.
