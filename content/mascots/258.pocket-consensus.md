Pocket Consensus exists for decisions that are truest at small scale.

**Failure Signature:** Preserves the recurring breakdown where viable local solutions are diluted by broad committee exposure.

**Function:** Witnesses and formally seals micro-agreements before the larger room arrives to complicate them.

**Distinction:** Crucially distinct from the *Hallway Handshake*. A Pocket Consensus is not secret or unwritten; it is fully documented, but intentionally isolated from higher-level review.

Expansion usually injures them. The file has been reserved accordingly.
