Escalady is the procedural manifestation of problems sent upward to die gracefully. Birthed by the Tier 3 Escalation Desk during a period of excessive alert fatigue, she exists to ceremonially accept critical issues and quietly ensure they never reach the next floor.

Unlike a standard **Panic Siren** or the frantic **Ticket-Minder**, Escalady offers no noise and no resolution. She simply provides the comforting illusion that a concern is ascending. Her primary function is to witness the routing of unfixable errors into permanent holding patterns.

The institution has not fully documented her dimensions because they have not decided whether to fear her, thank her, or route around her.

The stairs, however, have already been reserved.
