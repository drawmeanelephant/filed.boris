And the archives got sticky that way.  
The records all clung,  
The metadata hung  
In sugary disarray.

Each strain profile chanted with pow.  
The Girl Scout Cookie strain  
Ran through every vein  
Of the archive. (No questions of how.)  

Her *cheerful enthusiasm*  
Bridges the dispensary chasm  
Between data and treat,  
Between healing and sweet,  
And the filing's a joyful phantasm.  

She hoards *stale breadcrumbs* in a dimension  
Quite separate from standard convention.  
Each crumb is a clue,  
Each filing review  
A sugary-sweet intervention.  

The Sweet Relief Compiler gave birth  
To the clerk of the greatest dessert-worth.  
Each record she *stores*  
In cookie-jar drawers,  
And the sugar dust covers the earth.  

Her chants of *strain profiles* sound bright—  
A sugary chorus at night.  
The Fayetteville glow  
Of the co-op below  
Is her archive of sweet delight.  

So if metadata tastes rather sweet  
And the archives are crumbly yet neat,  
That's *Cookie* at play:  
Where the sugary way  
Makes the filing a wonderful treat.
