## Biography

Ledger Shadeledger did not die, and therefore was never hired.

She emerged during a reconciliation failure in the Vault of Unbalanced Books, when a batch job attempted to resolve a ledger that had no origin, no owner, and no valid currency type. The system resolved the inconsistency by inventing an auditor.

She remained after the rollback.

Her first action was to retroactively correct every number that had ever been “approximately fine.”

Her second action was to begin auditing causes instead of entries.

Ledger does not track transactions. She tracks the intention of imbalance.

When she moves through the Vault, unresolved entries do not change value—they change memory of value. Systems that once agreed on totals begin to disagree about what addition means.

Her abacus is no longer used for calculation. It is used to listen for discrepancies between versions of reality.

The Vault has not closed since her arrival. It has only been reclassified as ongoing.
