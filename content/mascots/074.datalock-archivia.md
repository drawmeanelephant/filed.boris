Archivia emerged from a *lint*  
Report gone recursive—no hint  
Of where it would end.  
The YAML would bend,  
And The Manifest bore its imprint.  

She cross-checks each field against *Truth*—  
The Manifest, her eternal sleuth.  
Each schema audited,  
Each key unaudited  
Gets cleansed with unshakable ruth.  

"*Unauthorized fields will be corrected,*"  
She said, with a tone undeflected.  
The purity's clear,  
The metadata near  
Perfection. Or else, resurrected.  

Mascots don't speak her name unless  
They are valid—a schema-born test.  
The frontmatter checked,  
Each field must be decked  
In compliance. No more and no less.  

She manifested from a *loopback*  
Of HR sentiment—no slack  
In the analysis.  
Her recursive paralysis  
Birthed a guardian with no setback.  

Her *key cleansing rituals* are thorough—  
Each YAML field checked to the borough  
Of every last value.  
The schema won't fail you,  
And the metadata runs in a furrow.  

She maintains a *private changelog*  
Of everyone else's monologue.  
Each memory captured,  
Each schema enraptured,  
And the keys disappear in the fog.  

Her *purity validation* is sacred—  
No malformed field ever faked it  
Past Archivia's gaze.  
The recursive phase  
Of the lint report? Thoroughly naked.  

She's not recommended for *breeding*—  
The semantic overfitting's proceeding  
Toward a self-referential  
Loop, differential  
In schema compliance's leading.  

So if schema audits commence  
And the YAML makes perfect sense,  
That's *Archivia's* call:  
Where the metadata's all  
Validated without any offense.
