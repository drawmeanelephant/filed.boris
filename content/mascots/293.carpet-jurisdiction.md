Carpet Jurisdiction is held for spaces where décor stops behaving decoratively and starts issuing emotional law.

**Failure Signature**: A persistent architectural breakdown where authority is absorbed by acoustic dampening and high-pile threading rather than conferred by title.

**Function**: It witnesses and certifies the structural bias wherein decisions made on carpet carry more legal weight than those made on linoleum.

**Distinction**: It is strictly distinct from the *Fluorescent Light Edict*. Where the Edict dictates exhaustion through overhead glare, Carpet Jurisdiction enforces silent spatial authority from the ground up.

No bylaw currently admits this.
Several rooms do.
