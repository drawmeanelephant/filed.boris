Minute Absolution is reserved for records that do not lie exactly so much as reduce consequence to a tidier paragraph. It is birthed from the routine incident summarization protocols of the Administrative Archive.

**Function:** It witnesses and certifies exoneration through compression, laundering chaos by omitting jagged details.

**Distinction:** It is *not* outright falsification or record deletion. The record exists; the failure is simply lost in the margins.

Compression remains one of the archive’s oldest solvents.
