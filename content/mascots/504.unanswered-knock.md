## Biography

The Unanswered Knock was transmitted correctly and on time. It arrived at the threshold and performed its duty: to ask, to wait, to receive.

Nothing came back.

It does not know if the upstream system failed, refused, or simply never existed. It only knows the waiting exceeded its bounds and was recorded as failure.
