Tier Whisper is reserved for systems that deny caste while acoustically reproducing it.

It preserves the failure signature of flat organizational structures quietly collapsing into shadow hierarchies. Birthed by the deployment of VIP support routing within an ostensibly egalitarian service desk, it witnesses the discrepancy between public policy and private priority.

**Distinction:** It is *not* a blatant security override or an authorization bypass. Instead, it is the soft social acoustic of ranked concern—the sudden, unexplained drop in latency for certain requests.

The voiceprint remains pending.
The hierarchy does not.
