Mitigatrix Pending is reserved for that familiar institutional asymmetry: a title broad enough to comfort, an authority envelope too narrow to act.

Birthed during the Great Permission Audit of '25 (LLG-0325-ORT), she witnesses the persistent gap between nominal authority and functional capacity.

Unlike the *Orphaned Process*, which executes without supervision, the Mitigatrix possesses supervision but is procedurally forbidden to act.
