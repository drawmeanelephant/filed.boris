### Agent McCrisp

Agent McCrisp slipped into the *silo*,  
A trenchcoat of cinnamon's halo.  
Each Northern Spy filed  
In classified style,  
And the harvest records? Quite aglow.  

She misfiled a log as "*classified  
Pie blueprint*"—a filing that mystified  
The entire archive.  
The crust won't survive  
The scrutiny. (Flavor verified.)  

She erased a *metadata trail*  
To cover her tracks—without fail.  
The auditors stumbled,  
The filing crumbled,  
And the stealth operation set sail.  

She deployed *cinnamon shavings* one night  
During review—obscuring each sight  
Of the filing beneath.  
The aromatic wreath  
Left the records deliciously right.  

"*Crisp covert, archive unseen,*" she said,  
While the shadows of boughs stretched ahead.  
Each Northern Spy baking  
Record was making  
Its way to a classified shred.  

Her stealth chants vanish in the *dark*—  
Each hymn a pomological spark.  
The silo's embrace,  
The cinnamon trace,  
And the archives are covert and stark.  

She wears a *trenchcoat lined with spice*—  
Each filing a beautiful vice.  
The cinnamon shavings  
Are covert engravings,  
And the baking records are precise.  

She files reports to a *mailbox*  
That doesn't exist—security talks  
In the Spy Silo's shade,  
Each filing delayed  
By the classified, cinnamon-scented locks.  

Her elusive yet *precise* demeanor  
Makes every harvest log leaner.  
The Northern Spy's crispness  
Is filed with this swiftness:  
No trail, no trace, not a gleaner.  

So if cinnamon scents fill the air  
And the harvest records aren't there,  
That's *Agent McCrisp*:  
Where the filing's a wisp,  
And the stealth is beyond compare.
