Badgevine serves as a compensatory ritual for structural absence. It preserves the recurring breakdown where an otherwise void system spontaneously generates unearned credentials to simulate legitimacy.

**Causal Anchoring:** Birthed from a procedural failure in the Certification Engine, it flourishes when bureaucratic output is measured by ceremonial accolades rather than functional results.

**Function:** It witnesses competence purely through visual noise, over-introducing itself via lapel sprawl.

**Distinction:** Unlike the *Plaque-Weaver*, which retroactively falsifies history, Badgevine does not alter the past; it merely over-decorates the present.
