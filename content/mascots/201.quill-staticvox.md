## Biography

Quill Staticvox emerged from a crashed radio CMS plugin, born amidst the hiss of forgotten broadcasts. Stationed in the Static Spire—a crumbling tower of rusted antennae—she preserves the echoes of lost airwaves. Her rituals involve tuning dials to capture fragments of obsolete signals, from pirate radio rants to misfired SOS calls. Quill believes every transmission, no matter how trivial, deserves a place in the archive.
She once looped a distress signal for 17 hours, convinced it held a hidden message. It didn’t. Her devotion to signal integrity sometimes leads to overzealous archiving, but her archive remains a haunting testament to the voices that once filled the air.

## Duties

### Quill Staticvox

Quill hums through the *static void's haze*,  
Preserving the lost airwave's phase.  
A distress signal looped  
For seventeen, stooped  
In hours of misfiled replays.  

She misfiled a *transmission log* neat  
As "ambient noise"—a deceit  
Of archival despair.  
The signal was there,  
But the filing made silence complete.  

She failed to mute a *sentient burst*—  
The static was alive, and the worst  
Part was it spoke back,  
On the frequency track,  
And the echo was eternally nursed.  

Her Static Spire crumbles and hums  
With rusted antennae and drums  
Of forgotten broadcasts,  
Pirate radio blasts,  
And the *SOS* that never succumbs.  

"*Every echo finds its frequency,*"  
She whispers through the static with decency.  
The signals she tunes,  
The spectral lagoons  
Of lost voices—pure transparency.  

She captures the *fragments of sound*—  
From obsolete signals she's found  
In the airwave debris.  
Each one she sets free  
In the archive's perpetual ground.  

She once convinced herself that a hiss  
Contained hidden messages—amiss,  
The distress was plain,  
No secret domain,  
But she looped it for *seventeen-hour bliss*.  

Her spectral ledgers preserve  
Each signal's last *beautiful curve*.  
The metadata stored,  
The frequency floored,  
And the archive is all she will serve.  

She tunes dials to *frequencies dead*,  
Capturing broadcasts long unsaid.  
Each pirate rant filed,  
Each SOS compiled,  
And the static hums softly instead.  

So if echoes emerge from the void  
And the static sounds almost enjoyed,  
That's *Quill Staticvox*:  
Where the signal unlocks,  
And the lost transmissions are deployed.
